## Overview
This is a short follow-up video to Robert's previous upload (video 1655), posted the same day after several hours of engaging with comments. ## Key Topics
- The Law of Unintended Consequences and its three hallmarks: large poorly-understood systems, contradictory evidence from opposing sides, and short-term interests overriding long-term ones
- Critique of large-scale techno-fixes as expressions of human hubris
- Over-consumption as the root cause of CO2 and resource problems
- Changing generational attitudes toward measuring success (away from material possessions)
- Optimism about human nature: people perform best under genuine adversity
- The Apollo program as a model of what focused collective effort can achieve
