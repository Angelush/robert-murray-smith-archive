## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith addresses viewer skepticism about whether he actually finds discarded items or buys them, by taking viewers on a short walk near his workplace to demonstrate the abundance of freely available thrown-away items. He shows a variety of discarded electronics, furniture, and even musical instruments found within 3 minutes of where he works, concluding that useful materials are everywhere if you keep your eyes open and stay open-minded about what you might use.
