## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith provides a concise educational overview of nuclear fusion, distinguishing between hot fusion, legitimate cold fusion research, and fraudulent or misguided claims. He explains the physics behind several real cold fusion approaches and contextualizes why confusion and scams proliferate in the field. His main takeaway is that cold fusion is real and scientifically promising, but is surrounded by both honest scientific error and deliberate fraud.
