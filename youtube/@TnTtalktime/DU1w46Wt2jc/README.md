## Overview

Robert argues that home wind turbines have been poorly implemented because manufacturers simply miniaturize commercial designs rather than engineering for the distinct conditions of the urban/home environment. He contends that while solar gets most attention, wind is actually more accessible to the DIY individual since you can build your own turbine at low or near-zero cost, bypassing the expensive payback periods associated with commercial options.
