## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith delivers a short soapbox rant arguing that vague warnings like "don't do that, it'll kill you" are useless and counterproductive. Using a microwave oven transformer (MOT) as his example, he makes the case that only specific, informative safety guidance actually helps people — both those who are cautious and those who will proceed regardless. His core takeaway is that repeating fear-based memes without understanding them contributes to both ignorance and unnecessary anxiety.
