## Overview

Robert reflects on the rising cost of petrol in the UK and Europe, using it as a springboard to revisit the Sinclair C5 — the infamous 1985 electric recumbent tricycle designed by Sir Clive Sinclair. He argues that while the C5 was a commercial disaster at launch, it was simply ahead of its time, and that today's market conditions and cultural acceptance of electric micro-vehicles make it ripe for a comeback.
