## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reflects on his experience with negative comments and trolls on YouTube, sharing practical mental frameworks for dealing with online criticism. His central message is that negative commenters are statistically rare, that their remarks reflect more about themselves than about the creator, and that understanding this should breed empathy rather than anger.
