## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith addresses his dislike of "gurus" — people on internet forums who use dense, jargon-heavy language not to inform, but to assert dominance and control over a community. He explains his channel's philosophy of open, accessible knowledge-sharing and warns that he will delete and ban anyone who behaves this way in his comments.
