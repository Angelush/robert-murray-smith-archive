## Overview

In this short reflective video, Robert Murray-Smith explains the underlying philosophy connecting his seemingly disparate recent projects — hubless wheels, electric vehicles, hydrogen generation, and electrolysis/capacitance. He argues that his work is not random but driven by a belief that everything is interconnected, and that solving the energy generation side of electric vehicles (via microgeneration or hydrogen-on-demand) is the key challenge he is working toward.
