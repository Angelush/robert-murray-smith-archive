## Overview
In this short reflective video, Robert Murray-Smith explains the underlying philosophy connecting his seemingly disparate recent projects — hubless wheels, electric vehicles, hydrogen generation, and electrolysis/capacitance. He argues that his work is not random but driven by a belief that everything is interconnected, and that solving the energy generation side of electric vehicles (via microgeneration or hydrogen-on-demand) is the key challenge he is working toward.

## Key Topics
- The connective thread running through his recent YouTube projects
- Hubless wheels as mechanical gears for low-power electric drives
- Plans to build an electric quadricycle using hubless wheels
- Skepticism about batteries alone as the answer for electric vehicles
- Microgeneration and hydrogen-on-demand as the missing piece for EVs
- His broader philosophy of universal interconnection between all things and people

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Hydrogen (on-demand generation for combined battery/fuel systems)

## Techniques and Methods
- Using a hubless wheel as a gear reduction system paired with a small high-RPM motor
- Onboard microgeneration combined with battery storage as a hybrid energy approach
- Hydrogen on-demand generation for electric vehicle range extension
