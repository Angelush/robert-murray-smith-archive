## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reflects on why he explores such a wide variety of topics on his channel despite being a battery specialist. He argues that while specialisation is essential for deep expertise, it carries the danger of dangerously narrowing one's perspective — and that true invention typically arises from cross-fertilising ideas across unrelated fields. His core takeaway is that a broad scope of curiosity, combined with a core specialisation, is the hallmark of genuinely inventive thinking.
