## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith addresses a recurring question from his audience: how much electricity does a wind turbine generate? He explains that generation output is not the correct metric for evaluating a rotor's performance — torque on the shaft is. The video clarifies the physics of wind power, including the Betz limit, and why claiming output beyond ~30% of available wind energy is likely misleading.
