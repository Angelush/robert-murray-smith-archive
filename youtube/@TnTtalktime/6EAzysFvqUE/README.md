## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith responds to viewer comments questioning why he spends time on projects like low-power air conditioning instead of focusing solely on batteries and supercapacitors. He explains that his view of "the energy problem" has evolved from storage-only, to generation-plus-storage, to a three-part framework encompassing energy generation, storage, and *use*. The video's key takeaway is that his diverse projects share a consistent underlying mission: addressing energy consumption as seriously as energy production.
