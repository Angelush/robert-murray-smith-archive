## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith addresses viewer criticism about his measurement practices in his experimental videos. He explains that his videos are personal explorations rather than attempts to convince others, and that the measurements he takes are chosen to serve his own investigative process. Using a wind generator/capacitor project as a concrete example, he also identifies a key technical insight about energy loss caused by back-pressure on the diode (check valve) system as capacitors charge.
