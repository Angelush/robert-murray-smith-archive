## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the challenge of converting oscillating or reciprocating motion (like footsteps or buffeting wind) into unidirectional rotation for energy generation. He builds on the 1956 SEO "magic lever" — a clever ratchet mechanism that always outputs rotation in one direction regardless of input direction — combining it with a 3D-printed axial flux serpentine coil generator and an oscillating wind arm to demonstrate a working proof-of-concept wind energy harvester.
