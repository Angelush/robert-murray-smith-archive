## Overview

"Man In A Shed – Episode 6" is a wide-ranging episode in which Robert covers five distinct science and engineering topics: a 3D-printed dual-input planetary gearbox that acts as an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (CVT); a critical look at the Betaray spherical solar concentrator alongside hands-on optics experiments; a smart adaptive roofing tile that uses wax motors to switch between super-black and super-white surfaces; the surprising resurgence of vertical axis wind turbines (VAWTs); the conversion of HDPE plastic into spider silk via bacterial fermentation; and a humorous but sobering history of animals used as weapons of war. The overarching theme is that clever mechanical and biological simplicity often outperforms expensive high-tech solutions.
