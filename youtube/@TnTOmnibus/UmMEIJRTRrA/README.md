## Overview

Robert surveys a range of innovative vertical axis and alternative wind turbine designs, covering the O-Wind tetrahedron turbine, the Harmony furling Savonius turbine, crossflow turbines (including the Ridge Blade), Fibonacci spiral turbines, kite-based airborne wind generation, and triboelectric nanogenerators (TENG) for wind energy. He 3D-prints and bench-tests several of these designs himself. His central conclusion is that no single wind energy technology suits all environments or users — diversity of approach, matched to local conditions and resources, is the real path forward.
