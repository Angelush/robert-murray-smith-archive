## Overview

Robert builds a large human-powered hamster wheel generator inspired by medieval treadmill cranes and modern "active desk" exercising trends. Using cold molding to construct two large wooden hoops, he assembles the wheel on a rolling sled frame and couples it to a stepper motor via longboard wheels to generate electricity. The finished device produces roughly 50–120 watts — enough to power a games console — demonstrating that human-powered generation is surprisingly practical.
