## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the surprisingly broad range of practical applications for mushrooms and fungi beyond their traditional uses in food, medicine, and recreation. The video covers mycelium-based composite materials, supercapacitors, solar steam generation, and leather alternatives, demonstrating hands-on experiments throughout. The central takeaway is that fungi — particularly their mycelium networks — are emerging as a versatile, sustainable material platform with applications ranging from packaging to space habitats.
