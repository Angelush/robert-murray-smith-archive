## Overview

This episode of "Man In A Shed" is a wide-ranging survey of alternative vehicle propulsion technologies, covering the rise and fall of electric cars, hydrogen generation from aluminium and water, ammonia as a fuel, zinc-chlorine flow batteries, wind-powered cars, and the long history of "water car" claims. Robert argues that the death and rebirth of the electric car was driven purely by consumer interest rather than conspiracy, and he highlights several underexplored technologies — particularly the aluminium-water hydrogen system and Graneau's cavitation research — as genuinely promising directions that deserve more attention.
