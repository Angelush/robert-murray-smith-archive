## Overview

Robert demonstrates how conductive ink, applied freehand with a pen to a flat piece of scrap glass and then heated in a microwave, can cut complex curved shapes cleanly and easily. The video is a direct response to a viewer question (from Luke Turner) asking whether the technique works on flat glass. Robert concludes that this method makes traditional glass cutters effectively obsolete for hobby glasswork.
