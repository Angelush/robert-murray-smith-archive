## Overview

A short practical build video in which Luke (presenting on Robert Murray-Smith's channel) repurposes a collection of old lithium mobile phone batteries to construct a custom battery pack intended to power a wireless speaker for 2–3 hours. The build centres on wiring four cells in series and integrating a Battery Management System (BMS) to regulate output voltage.
