## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a cheap and simple method to boost solar panel power output by placing lenticular prism film (salvaged from LCD screens) in front of a mirror beside a solar panel. He replicates work by a Canadian inventor (later identified in comments as Guy Cramer), showing a 12–15% increase in a basic demo and noting the inventor reported up to 50% improvement with a full funnel arrangement.
