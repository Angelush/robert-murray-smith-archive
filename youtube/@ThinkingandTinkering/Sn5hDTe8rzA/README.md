## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the "aeromine" — a bladeless wind energy device based on NACA F1 airfoils positioned to create a low-pressure zone that draws air upward through pipes. He builds a prototype using 3D-printed airfoils from an Elegoo Neptune 3D Plus printer and tests it with a fan, concluding that while the concept works, it performs best at high wind speeds and is likely not worth the significant fabrication effort for typical wind conditions.
