## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith replicates Art Porter's "gapless motor" (gap motor) on a smaller, affordable scale suitable for home experimentation. Rather than using the large, expensive neodymium magnets from the original design, he substitutes small neo disc and bar magnets, and replaces the mechanical timing mechanism with a 555 astable timer circuit. The video demonstrates the motor running and concludes with ideas for improvements, including capturing back-spike energy and using the assembly as a linear alternator.
