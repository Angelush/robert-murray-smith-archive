## Overview

Robert demonstrates using his homemade EcoFoam carpet cleaner as an upholstery spot cleaner on his sofa. It proves highly effective at removing grease and graphite ink stains, but works so well on the spots that it makes the surrounding fabric look dirty by comparison — forcing him to clean the entire cushion.
