## Overview

A short Tinkercad tutorial demonstrating how to design eccentric cams using basic cylinder shapes and alignment tools. Robert explains that eccentric cams are a fundamental mechanical component used in crankshafts and steam/air engine slide valves, and shows step-by-step that the construction method scales to any size, thickness, or throw. The key takeaway is that the same alignment technique applies equally to gears, since gears are simply circles with indentations.
