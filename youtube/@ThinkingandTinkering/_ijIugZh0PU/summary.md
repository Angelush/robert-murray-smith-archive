## Overview
Robert recounts his Christmas trip to Milan to visit his son's newborn baby, describing the complex web of COVID-19 travel requirements in late 2021. He emphasizes that while the outbound procedures were manageable, the stress of potentially testing positive and being stranded abroad was the most difficult aspect of international travel at the time.
