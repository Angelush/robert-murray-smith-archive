## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the value of repurposing cordless drills, even broken or cheap secondhand ones, as sources of high-quality motors, battery packs, and mechanical components. He demonstrates disassembly and explains how each part can be salvaged and reused in DIY projects ranging from stirrers to e-bikes to boats.
