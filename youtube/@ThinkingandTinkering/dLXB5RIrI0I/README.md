## Overview

Luke dismantles a broken hard drive and repurposes its aluminium casing and spinning disc as the face of a functional wall clock. The motor housing is removed and replaced with a standard clock mechanism, producing a clean, industrial-looking clock. The finished piece is compact and visually striking, making clever use of the hard drive's original disc as the clock face.
