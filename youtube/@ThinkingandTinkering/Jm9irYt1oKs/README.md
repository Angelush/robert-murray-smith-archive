## Overview

This is a competition entry video by a fan demonstrating a PC fan converted into a small AC generator, inspired by techniques shown by Robert Murray-Smith. The creator modifies a standard computer fan by rerouting its coil wires to produce AC output, then conditions the power through a bridge rectifier and capacitor to light LEDs and a small LCD panel. The key takeaway is that a simple, cheap computer fan can be repurposed as a hand-spun or air-powered generator capable of producing 10–12V at ~100mA.
