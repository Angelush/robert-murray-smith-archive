## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives a walkthrough of his newly established graphene research lab, set up with funding from an Indiegogo campaign. The video documents his move from working at home and in garden sheds to a proper commercial unit near Canterbury, Kent, capable of handling chemical waste disposal at scale. He closes with an open invitation for anyone to visit, collaborate, or attend demonstrations at the lab.
