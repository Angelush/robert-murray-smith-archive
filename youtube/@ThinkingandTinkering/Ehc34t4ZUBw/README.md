## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith completes a multi-part project by assembling a working DIY concertina (bellows-based) pump from readily available plastic plumbing parts and gaffer tape. The pump uses two one-way valves to ensure unidirectional water flow, keeping the bellows completely dry. The successful proof-of-concept demonstrates that a functional water pump can be built cheaply and without power tools, making it viable in emergency or survival situations.
