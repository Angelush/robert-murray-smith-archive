## Overview

This short video demonstrates a wind pump mechanism featuring a 20:1 gear ratio that converts rotational motion into a pumping action. Robert shows the device operating with an artificial wind source, explaining how the gear reduction trades speed for force. The takeaway is that this mechanism could be directly coupled to a water pump to multiply the force available from a windmill.
