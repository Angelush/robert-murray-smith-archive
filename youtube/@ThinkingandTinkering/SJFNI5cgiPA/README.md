## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith begins building a small wooden automata inspired by driftwood collected near his coastal home. The sculpture will depict a rocking ship in front of cliffs, animated by a hand-turned crank mechanism. This is part 1 of the build, covering the initial design, cutting, and shaping of the main components.
