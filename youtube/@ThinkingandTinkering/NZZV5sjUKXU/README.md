## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates his homemade "Ecofoam" carpet cleaner, a product he developed after years of struggling to clean graphite-contaminated carpets. The cleaner is supplied as a dry powder (reducing plastic and liquid waste), mixed with hot water and scrubbed in, and visibly restores color to heavily soiled carpet in about 20 minutes. Robert presents it as a genuinely effective solution after trying every commercial alternative.
