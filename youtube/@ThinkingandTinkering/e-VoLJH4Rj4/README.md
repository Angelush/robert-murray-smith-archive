## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the classic American/Australian multi-blade wind pump, arguing that efficiency should not be the sole criterion when building small-scale wind-powered systems. He introduces the **Scotch yoke** mechanism as an elegant and simple way to convert rotary wind energy into linear reciprocating motion, demonstrating a 3D-printed prototype that could drive a water pump, compressor, or wind saw directly.
