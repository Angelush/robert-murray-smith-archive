## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to convert a cheap USB desktop fan (bought for ~£3) into a miniature wind turbine generator. He replaces the original fan blades with larger homemade pinwheel-style blades and adds a bridge rectifier to produce usable DC output. The project is presented primarily as an educational exercise for understanding the fundamentals of wind generation rather than as a practical power source.
