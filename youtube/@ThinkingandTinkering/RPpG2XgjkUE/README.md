## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith briefly documents large solar cookers in use at Dingboche, Nepal, a high-altitude village on the Everest trekking route. The cookers are being used primarily to heat water, and Robert is surprised by both their size and prevalence. His takeaway is that this remote location makes effective use of its abundant strong sunlight through multiple solar thermal installations.
