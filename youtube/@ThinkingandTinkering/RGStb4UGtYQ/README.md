## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to create a simple working LED circuit entirely on paper using a conductive ink of his own design. The video shows the construction of a complete circuit — including a spring-loaded paper switch, a battery holder, and an LED connection — using everyday materials like plastic packaging and masking tape. The conductive ink serves as both circuit traces and a "cold solder" to attach components.
