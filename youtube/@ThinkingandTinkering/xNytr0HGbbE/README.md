## Overview

This video demonstrates a bicycle-powered hammer mill setup used to process homemade biochar. The presenter, a gardener, explains their full biochar production workflow — from burning wood in a converted propane tank, quenching the coals, and then milling the dried charcoal (mixed with leaves) into a fine material used as chicken coop bedding. The activated biochar-manure mix is then added to compost or garden soil as a soil amendment.
