## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith acquires a secondhand Mini for £100 (trading in a scrapped Nissan Micra) and begins converting it to an electric vehicle. In this first part, he focuses on the initial acquisition and enlists the help of a local garage, Canterbury Automotive, to remove the original 1.6-litre petrol engine for another £100. The video sets up the project with a total outlay of £200 and previews the motor build to come.
