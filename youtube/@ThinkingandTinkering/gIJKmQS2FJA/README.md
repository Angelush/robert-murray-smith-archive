## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to repair a burnt-out solar panel that suffered an overload, damaging the internal contact tabs. Working from the back of the panel, he methodically disassembles it, cleans and reconnects the burnt tabs, and reseals it — confirming the repair works by reading 17 volts in the shade.
