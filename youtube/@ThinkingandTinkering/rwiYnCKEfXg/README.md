## Overview

This short video, presented by Luke from Robert Murray-Smith's channel, demonstrates connecting a dust extractor to a belt sander to reduce airborne dust during woodworking. The experiment shows that attaching the extractor to the sander's exhaust port significantly reduces dust spread, though it does not capture every particle.
