## Overview

Robert introduces triboelectric nanogenerators (TENGs) as a wind energy harvesting technology, explaining how rubbing or vibrating materials in the wind generates electricity. He argues this approach could complement traditional wind turbines — especially at low wind speeds — and that recent academic research makes DIY, large-scale versions feasible using simple everyday materials.
