## Overview

This video explores the theoretical foundations of electrochemical cells (batteries, supercapacitors, and fuel cells) through the lens of thermodynamics. Robert explains why electrochemical cells can exceed the efficiency limit of heat engines (Carnot efficiency) by introducing Gibbs free energy and the Nernst efficiency. Along the way, he offers an accessible and opinionated reinterpretation of entropy as the energy required to maintain a system's structural arrangement, rather than the conventional "tendency toward disorder."
