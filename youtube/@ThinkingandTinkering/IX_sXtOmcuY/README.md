## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reviews the Revopoint Inspire 3D scanner, sent to him free for review, giving his first-time user perspective. He unboxes it, explains how it works, and demonstrates it by scanning a fox skull and 3D printing the result. His conclusion is that it is surprisingly easy to use out of the box, produces good results for organic shapes, and the price point is reasonable.
