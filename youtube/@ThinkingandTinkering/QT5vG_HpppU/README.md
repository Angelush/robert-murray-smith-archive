## Overview

Robert tests whether the "forever wick" (also called the "magic wick," a carbon-based wick with a copper pipe surround) can serve as a direct drop-in replacement for a conventional wick in a garden heater. After initial failure and a 20-minute burn test using paraffin oil, he concludes it cannot be a straightforward replacement, but explains the underlying physics that make it workable under the right conditions.
