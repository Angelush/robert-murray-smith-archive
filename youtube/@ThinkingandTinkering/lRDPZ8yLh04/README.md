## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a home chemistry synthesis of graphene oxide (GO) using the modified Hummers method, working through the low-, mid-, and high-temperature stages of the process. He documents the full procedure from mixing graphite with concentrated sulfuric acid and oxidizers through washing, centrifuging, and obtaining a golden-brown GO suspension. The video concludes with the GO in solution and a preview of upcoming reduction steps to convert it into graphene.
