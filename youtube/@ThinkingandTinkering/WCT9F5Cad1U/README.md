## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the two fundamental categories of water turbines — reaction type and impulse type — and contrasts the Pelton wheel with the Turgo turbine. He covers how each design exploits different properties of water (mass vs. pressure) and which works best under different head and flow conditions. The video concludes by setting up a future project to build a Turgo turbine from spoons, noting its advantage over the Pelton wheel for DIY construction.
