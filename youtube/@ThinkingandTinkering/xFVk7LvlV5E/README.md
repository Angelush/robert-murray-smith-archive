## Overview

Part 3 of a series on CartaPesta (Italian papier mache) sculpture, demonstrating how to apply wet newspaper strips over a foam armature to create a figure with flowing fabric details. Robert shows the full finishing sequence — from paper application through gap-filling with polyfiller, to a final black paint and metallic wax finish — arguing that this accessible technique can produce impressive results even with artistically inexperienced students.
