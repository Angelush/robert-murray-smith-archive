## Overview
This video is a follow-up to video 1817, testing whether the Darwin wind turbine prototype can function when mounted at the end of a pipe. Robert demonstrates the turbine laid flat with a 110mm soil pipe and a PC fan at the bottom, successfully lighting an LED and confirming the concept works.
