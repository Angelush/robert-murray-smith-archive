## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the RidgeBlade wind turbine by first giving a thorough grounding in crossflow wind turbine technology — its history, design parameters, and trade-offs. He concludes that while crossflow turbines are not the most efficient, the RidgeBlade is a sensible choice for urban/domestic use because it mounts on an existing rooftop ridge, eliminating tower costs, and operates quietly at low wind speeds.
