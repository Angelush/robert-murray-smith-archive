## Overview

This video documents the construction of a DIY coffee table shaped and painted to resemble a retro floppy disk, complete with a functional aluminium sliding mechanism that mimics the floppy disk's metal shutter. The table's standout feature is a hidden secret compartment revealed when the sliding panel is pushed to one side. The build proceeds from raw MDF and wood through joinery, fabrication, and finishing, resulting in a green-painted, varnished piece with a toner-transfer label sticker.
