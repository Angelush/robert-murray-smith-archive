## Overview

Robert revisits two previously built devices — a copper thermopile and a magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) generator — both part of his "Rocket Stove Third Generation" project exploring ways to generate electricity from a rocket stove. Despite looking nearly identical (copper wire, applied heat), the two devices operate on entirely different physical principles, and the video exists specifically to clarify that distinction and dispel viewer confusion.
