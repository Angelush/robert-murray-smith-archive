## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith documents a week-long outdoor building project — constructing a deck, pergola, and summer house — and uses it as a framing device to showcase the Metmo multi-tool. After getting a deep splinter from saw-finished timber, he improvised a home surgery using the Metmo's scalpel blade attachment to remove it. The video doubles as a light endorsement of Metmo as a British engineering company, with a mention of their fractal vice Kickstarter.
