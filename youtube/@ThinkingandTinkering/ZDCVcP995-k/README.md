## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to build a friction (frictional) heater in under an hour using two stainless steel food canisters that happen to fit together with close tolerance. He fills the inner canister with oil and spins it with a drill, showing that at 2,600 RPM the device raises the oil temperature to 46 °C in just 3 minutes. The video reinforces his earlier observation that squat, broad heater geometries are more thermally efficient than long, thin ones.
