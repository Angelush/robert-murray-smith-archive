## Overview
This is an introductory/administrative video for Robert's "Heidi printer competition," in which community members submitted videos designing something related to lighting on LEDs for a chance to win a 3D printer. Robert explains the voting rules (YouTube likes over 14 days), the commenting policy, and asks viewers to approach each entry with generosity — judging participants on effort and learning rather than polished output.

## Key Topics
- Competition overview: entries are collected in a playlist, winner determined by most likes after 14 days
- Voting instructions and fairness expectations
- Comment moderation policy: comments disabled on all entry videos, centralised here; rude or hurtful comments result in an immediate ban
- Encouragement to view entries through the lens of personal achievement (first-timers, kids, engineers, teachers)
- Community values and the spirit of the channel
