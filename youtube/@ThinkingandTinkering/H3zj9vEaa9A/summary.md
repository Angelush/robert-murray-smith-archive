## Overview
This is a brief update video for participants in Robert's upcoming "FWG Graphene Making Live Event," where kits have been sold to attendees for making graphene via electrochemical intercalation. Robert corrects a wiring error in the relay-based timing circuit diagram included in those kits, clarifying which relay pins should be connected together. He reassures viewers that new kits ship with the corrected diagram and that the correct circuit will also be demonstrated live during the event.

## Key Topics
- Announcement of the upcoming graphene-making live workshop
- Correction of a wiring error in the timing circuit diagram included in sold kits
- Clarification of the correct relay pin connections (pins 1 & 4, and pins 8 & 5)
- How to obtain the corrected circuit diagram (via the end-of-video slide or by contacting Mandy)
- Availability of kits for those who haven't yet ordered

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Graphene (the material being produced in the live event)
- Two electromechanical relays (used to build the timing/switching circuit)
- Relay base block (housing for the relays)

## Techniques and Methods
- Electrochemical intercalation — the method used to produce graphene in the kit-based workshop
- Assembly of a relay-based timing/switching circuit to control the electrochemical process
