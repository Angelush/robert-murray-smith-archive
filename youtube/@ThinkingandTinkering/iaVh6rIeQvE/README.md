## Overview

Part 3 of Robert's electric kick scooter build series focuses on fabricating and fitting the metal panels — specifically floor pans, mudguards, and a front shield. He repurposes galvanized steel kitchen table tops, plans to sand off the galvanization and copper-plate the bare steel for a copper aesthetic with steel strength, then bends the panels using a vise and angle iron. By the end of the video, the scooter's bodywork is largely complete, with only the electrics and front control panel remaining.
