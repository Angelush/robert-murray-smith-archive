## Overview

Robert demonstrates a simple method for making custom-length drive belts of any size using a scarf joint technique borrowed from woodworking and metalworking. By cutting diagonal overlapping tapers in belt material down to the internal cloth reinforcement layer and bonding them with super glue, he creates a strong, reliable join. This solves the common frustration of finding correctly-sized belts for pulley-driven machines.
