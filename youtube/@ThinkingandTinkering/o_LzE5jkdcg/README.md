## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a DIY micro-hydroelectric turbine built from salvaged and repurposed materials at a riverside location in Scotland. Using a bicycle/wheelchair wheel, a hovercraft motor as a generator, and a garden hose with a makeshift nozzle, he generates enough electricity to power an LED strip light. The video is an informal proof-of-concept showing low-cost, improvised renewable energy generation.
