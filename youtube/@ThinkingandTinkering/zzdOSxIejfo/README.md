## Overview

Robert demonstrates a variable hypocycloid mechanism — an evolution of his earlier hypocycloid engine (video 2327) — where a worm gear allows the stroke length to be adjusted independently of rotation speed. By exploiting the Tan's couple property (inner gear diameter exactly half the outer), and adding a rotatable inner ring, the effective stroke can be varied continuously from zero to maximum, giving the mechanism the essential properties of a CVT (continuously variable transmission).
