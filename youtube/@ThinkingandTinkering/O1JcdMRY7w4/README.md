## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a DIY version of the commercial "Soundbug" device — a transducer that turns any flat surface into a speaker. Using just three components (an inductor, a neodymium magnet, and a rubber band), he shows how the device works by vibrating a surface in time with an audio signal, effectively using the surface itself as the speaker cone.
