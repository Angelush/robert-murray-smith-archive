## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith shares a brief tip for YouTube creators, highlighting the existence of free, royalty-free and copyright-free stock video and photo resources. He demonstrates two websites — Pexels and Pixabay — showing how creators can download and use stock footage in their videos without copyright strikes or royalty fees.

## Key Topics
- Royalty-free and copyright-free stock video resources for YouTube creators
- Distinction between free download sites and subscription-based stock media services
- How using stock footage can improve video quality and avoid copyright issues
- Brief mention of paid alternatives (Shutterstock, Storyblocks/Plus Five)
