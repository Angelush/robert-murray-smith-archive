## Overview

This is a progress update (Part 3) on Robert's budget wind turbine build, where he tests the electrical output of his homemade vertical-axis turbine for the first time. Using a microwave oven transformer coil and ceramic ring magnets, he demonstrates the generator produces up to 15V and around 60mA (roughly 600–800mW) when spun by hand, and can even illuminate an LED panel, showing the design has real promise before being mounted on his roof.
