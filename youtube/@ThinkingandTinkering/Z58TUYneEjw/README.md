## Overview

Luke repairs a salvaged Unilab Retro PSU variable power supply that was found without a plug and with an audible rattle. The repair turns out to be straightforward — a single loose wire needed resoldering and a new plug attached — and the unit is restored to full working condition. The video demonstrates that valuable lab equipment (worth £100–150) can often be saved with minimal effort.
