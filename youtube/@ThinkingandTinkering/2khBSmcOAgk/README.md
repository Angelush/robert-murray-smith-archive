## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to convert a mains-powered plug-in drill to run on DC batteries by removing the AC switch and reconnecting the motor terminals with a capacitor across them. He shows the drill running successfully on two 12V car batteries in series (24V), then argues that this approach generalises to a wide range of power tools since they all share the same universal motor design.
