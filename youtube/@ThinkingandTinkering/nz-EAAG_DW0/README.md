## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith addresses viewer questions about his unfinished projects, explaining the practical realities of running a YouTube channel as a small operation. He outlines how decisions about continuing or abandoning a project series are driven by a balance of viewer interest, income (advertising and memberships), and his own enthusiasm. The core takeaway is that projects are not abandoned arbitrarily — they are discontinued when viewership drops too low to justify the cost and effort relative to other content.
