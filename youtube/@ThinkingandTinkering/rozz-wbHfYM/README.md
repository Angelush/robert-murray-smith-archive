## Overview

Robert discovers a discarded microwave on a patch of parkland and decides to disassemble it on camera, identifying the reusable components inside. He then uses salvaged parts to build two practical projects: an electromagnet from the microwave oven transformer (MOT) and a hand-crank USB phone charger from the synchronous turntable motor. The video makes the case that broken appliances contain genuinely valuable materials and components worth salvaging before recycling or disposal.
