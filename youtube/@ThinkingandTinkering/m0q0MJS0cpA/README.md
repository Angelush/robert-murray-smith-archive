## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith upcycles a 1970s bedside table bought for £5 from a charity shop into a functional Dutch stove — a traditional foot/body warmer. He converts the furniture into an insulated wooden box with air holes, then creates a resistive heating element by painting conductive ink onto a kiln tile, powering it with a 24V DC supply to produce a safe, ~60W foot warmer.
