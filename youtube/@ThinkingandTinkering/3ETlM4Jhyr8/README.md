## Overview

Robert introduces JoinTruly, a platform created by his friend Richard Richardson that connects question-askers with domain experts who respond via personalised video answers for a small fee. The video explores the problem of unanswered questions on YouTube — where creators are overwhelmed by comments — and presents JoinTruly as a two-sided solution: giving questioners specific answers while allowing knowledgeable people to earn from their expertise.
