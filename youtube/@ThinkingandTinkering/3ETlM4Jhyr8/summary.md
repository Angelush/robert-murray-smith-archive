## Overview
Robert introduces JoinTruly, a platform created by his friend Richard Richardson that connects question-askers with domain experts who respond via personalised video answers for a small fee. The video explores the problem of unanswered questions on YouTube — where creators are overwhelmed by comments — and presents JoinTruly as a two-sided solution: giving questioners specific answers while allowing knowledgeable people to earn from their expertise.

## Key Topics
- The limitations of YouTube as a Q&A medium (passive consumption, unanswered comments)
- Robert's inability to respond to ~400–500 daily comments
- JoinTruly: a paid expert-answer platform founded by Richard Richardson
- The two-sided value proposition: getting answers vs. monetising expertise
- The idea that many people underestimate the expertise they have accumulated
- JoinTruly as an income opportunity during difficult economic times (COVID-era context)
