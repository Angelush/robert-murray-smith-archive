## Robert's Own Replies
Robert was active in the comments, adding several clarifications and words of encouragement:

- **The motor didn't need to work** — he explicitly says he never required the motor to function, encouraging someone who didn't submit because theirs failed.
- **You don't need to appear on camera** — hands and a voiceover are perfectly acceptable, addressing a likely barrier to entry.
- **It's about participation, not winning** — he emphasises the spirit of the competition over the competitive outcome.
- **Early days for the competition format** — he notes this was only the third competition he had run and expects things to improve as they become better known.
- **Brief aside on "red mercury"** — in an off-topic thread, he dismisses red mercury as likely nonsense, noting its implausibly high price (~£900k/kg) as a red flag.
