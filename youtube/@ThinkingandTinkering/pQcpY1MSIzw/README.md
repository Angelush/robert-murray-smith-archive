## Overview

A short video demonstrating a hand-crank phone charger built by Robert Murray-Smith. He frames human power as a reliable alternative to intermittent renewable energy sources (wind and solar), arguing that a crank mechanism is one of the most efficient ways to convert human effort into electrical power. The video concludes with a live demonstration of his phone successfully charging from the device.
