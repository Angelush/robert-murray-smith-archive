## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a DIY hot plastic stapler for repairing cracked plastic — particularly car bumpers and light clusters — using a salvaged microwave oven transformer (MOT). He walks through the full build, from preparing the transformer and fabricating the electrode connectors to testing the device on scrap plastic, concluding that it works remarkably well and is a practical, low-cost alternative to expensive professional repairs.
