## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds a hand-powered generator inspired by a humming top's twisted-bar mechanism, first with a custom 3D-printed serpentine-coil design, then by rewinding and converting a common brushed DC motor into a brushless generator. The two systems are combined — the pump/flywheel drive from the humming top mechanism driving the rewound motor — producing 3–4 volts with very little effort, enough to charge a mobile phone.
