## Overview

This short DIY tutorial demonstrates how to convert a standard soldering iron into a hot knife capable of cutting plastics. The presenter (Luke) shows that by removing the soldering tip, sawing a slit into it, and clamping an X-Acto or Stanley blade in its place, a cheap and effective plastic-cutting tool can be made. Testing confirms it cuts through both thin packaging plastic and 1.5 mm acrylic with ease.
