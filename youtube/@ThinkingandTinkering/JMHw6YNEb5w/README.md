## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith walks through the contents of his graphene supercapacitor kit (available via IndieGoGo campaign) and demonstrates how to build a simple symmetric graphene supercapacitor from scratch. He emphasises that this is a beginner's experimenter kit—success comes from iterative small-scale practice rather than expecting professional results immediately, and he demonstrates the finished device powering a small helicopter motor for 25 seconds.
