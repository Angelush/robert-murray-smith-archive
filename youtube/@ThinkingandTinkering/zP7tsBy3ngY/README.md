## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the concept of a hypocycloid differential — a type of differential gear based on hypocycloid geometry rather than the familiar ring-and-pinion found in cars. Building on his previous video (2282) about epicyclic gears, he adapts a hypocycloid gear design (sourced from Otvinta) by adding external cogs to its ring and output gears, demonstrating that the result functions as a true differential that can split or combine rotational input at variable speeds.
