## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith, a self-described battery expert, delivers a critical review of a viral YouTube video by the channel "Fabricated in Five Minutes" that claims to show how to build a giant battery from a 50-gallon drum, copper pipes, activated carbon, and rainwater. He systematically dismantles the device using the five fundamental components every battery must have, concluding the design cannot function at all. He also reflects on the broader problem of misleading clickbait science content on YouTube and the tension between honest, replicable science and spectacular but fraudulent demonstrations.
