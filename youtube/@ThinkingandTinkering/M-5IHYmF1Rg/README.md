## Overview

In this video (part of a series), Robert Murray-Smith takes the DIY push wheelchair he built in video 1198 — constructed from a car seat and shopping trolley — and motorises it using a salvaged e-scooter drive system. The result is somewhere between a powered wheelchair and a "crazy cart," and Robert tests it with a friend, finding it fun but tricky to control. He closes by explaining how a simple modification (welding the rear casters) could turn it into either a more conventional motorised wheelchair or a full crazy cart.
