## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to recover and restore the optical-quality acrylic light guide panel from broken LCD monitors. Using simple, cheap materials, he shows how to remove the micro-pit surface texture and polish the acrylic to a clear, optically usable finish. The main takeaway is that this salvaged acrylic is genuinely valuable — suitable for making lenses, prisms, or polished sheets — and can be turned into a sellable product with minimal investment.
