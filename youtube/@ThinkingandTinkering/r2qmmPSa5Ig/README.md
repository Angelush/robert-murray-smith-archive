## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds a simple rocket stove from 100×100mm square steel pipe purchased pre-cut online for £20, demonstrating how to weld the pieces together in roughly an hour. The video explains the efficiency principle behind rocket stoves — burning both the wood and the wood gas produced — which can save 30–50% of fuel compared to a conventional fire. He ends by outlining the next step: enclosing the stove in a mass heater using a larger cylinder and thermal mass material like concrete or fire bricks.
