## Overview

A guest presenter named Luke builds a simple DIY battery level indicator circuit designed to test 12V vehicle batteries. The device uses a voltage divider principle with five LEDs and a potentiometer to visually show remaining charge. The project is motivated by the frustration of a dead car battery and serves as a low-cost alternative to dedicated battery testers.
