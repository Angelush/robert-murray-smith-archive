## Overview

In this video, Robert demonstrates how ordinary non-magnetic graphite can be transformed into magnetic graphite through a simple mechanical ball-milling process. He contextualises the experiment within a broader philosophical point: that graphite — like graphene, like a chair — is a *category* of materials, not a single fixed thing. The magnetic result surprises even him, and he speculates that ball-milling may be rolling graphite sheets into multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs).
