## Overview

This video is a philosophical reflection on the nature of inventing, inspired by Robert's research into Starlite and its creator Maurice Ward. Drawing on a 1994 QED documentary ("Plastic Fantastic"), Robert argues that formal scientific training is far less important than curiosity, creativity, and the willingness to simply try things. The central takeaway is that trial and error — not academic theory — is the true engine of genuine innovation.
