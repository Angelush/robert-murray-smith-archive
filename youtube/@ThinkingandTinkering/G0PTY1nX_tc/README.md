## Overview

In this short Christmas advent calendar video (Day 15), Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a simple but striking science trick: bent toothpicks slowly straighten themselves out when a drop of water is placed at the bend. He explains that wood fibres absorb the water and the material tries to return to its original straight shape, calling it "science magic."
