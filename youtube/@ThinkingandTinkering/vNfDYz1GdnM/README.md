## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces Eric Luman and his "Game Changer Movement," a social initiative aimed at redirecting young people away from video games and toward collaborative, hands-on graphene science. Robert shares his enthusiasm for grassroots scientific education and encourages viewers to support Eric's work at dreamcatalyst.org. The video reflects Robert's belief that collective, practical experimentation — especially involving young people — is essential to unlocking graphene's potential.
