## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith documents the construction of a bamboo frame for an electric quad bike, reproducing the geometry of a previous metal quad built from welded children's bikes. He walks through his joinery method — socket-and-peg bamboo joints held with Gorilla Glue and reinforced with wrapped carbon fiber tow and resin — producing a remarkably lightweight frame.
