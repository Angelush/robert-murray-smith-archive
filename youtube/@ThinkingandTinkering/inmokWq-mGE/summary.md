## Overview
This video is a compilation of short comedic sketch videos Robert Murray-Smith made with a collaborator named Luke several years prior, saved here as the originals were being moved off his main channel during a channel reorganisation. The skits feature a fictional character called "Joe Below" — a lovably clueless everyman who delivers deadpan non-sequiturs about technology and tools. It's light-hearted entertainment rather than science content, shared by Robert as a way to preserve these comedy videos and give them a new audience.
