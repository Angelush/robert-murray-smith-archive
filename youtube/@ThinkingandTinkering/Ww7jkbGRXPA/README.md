## Overview

Robert examines the confusing state of UK e-bike legislation — the 250W motor limit, 25 km/h pedal-assist cap, and 5 km/h throttle cap that define a road-legal e-bike — and works through a personal scare after realising his ADO A20 might be the 350W international model, which would make it an illegal electric moped. He discovers the bike is in fact the EU 250W variant, confirmed by the legally-required wattage label on the motor, and concludes it is fully road-legal in the UK.
