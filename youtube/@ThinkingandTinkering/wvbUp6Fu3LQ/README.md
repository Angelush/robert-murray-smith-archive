## Overview

Robert demonstrates the extraordinary diamagnetic properties of a rare graphite sample (which he calls "VOCC graphite") supplied by RS International, a Sri Lankan mining company. Unlike ordinary natural graphite, this material levitates visibly above neodymium magnets and — crucially — can be moved across a magnetic bed by focusing sunlight onto it with a lens. The video lays out Robert's plan to build a spinning graphite disk driven by sunlight alone: a solar engine with no moving parts other than the levitating graphite itself.
