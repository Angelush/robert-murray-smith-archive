## Overview

Robert provides a status update on his range of conductive inks, explaining the trade-offs he has discovered between waterproofing and electroconductive properties. He concludes that no single ink can serve all purposes, and announces a plan to maintain separate formulations for different tasks — standard conduction, "swagging," and electroplating — while also working toward a cost-effective electroluminescent ink.
