## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith salvages a discarded cordless vacuum cleaner and repurposes its DC motor and impeller as a water-powered generator (hydro generator). The motor alone produces only ~1.2 volts — insufficient for an LED — so he pairs it with a Joule thief circuit to step up the voltage and successfully light an LED. The video is a quick, practical entry for his competition challenging viewers to build water generators.
