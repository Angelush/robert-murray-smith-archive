## Overview

Robert explores the application of tubercles — the bumpy ridges found on humpback whale fins — to water power generation. He 3D-prints a scaled-down tubercle turbine blade, attaches it to a converted PC fan acting as a generator, and tests it with a garden hose jet. The experiment confirms the design works as a turbine, and Robert speculates about its potential for broader water energy applications.
