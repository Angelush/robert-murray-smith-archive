## Overview

This short video introduces an idea for a hand-squeeze crank generator, inspired by a childhood memory of a squeeze-type torch and a Thingiverse design originally intended as a handheld fan. The host plans to adapt the squeeze mechanism by replacing the fan with a small motor and voltage regulator to create a compact, hand-powered phone or battery charger. The video also serves as a one-week reminder for an ongoing competition to win an LCD 3D printer.
