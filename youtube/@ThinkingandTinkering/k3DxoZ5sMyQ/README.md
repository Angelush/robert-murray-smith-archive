## Overview

In this short advent calendar video (Day 16), Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a simple robotic hand using drinking straws, string, and sticky tape. The hand mimics the tendon-driven mechanism of a real human hand, where pulling strings causes the "fingers" to curl and close. It serves as a fun, accessible science activity for children or families.
