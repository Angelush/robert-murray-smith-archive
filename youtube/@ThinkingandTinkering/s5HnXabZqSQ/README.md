## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith answers a frequently asked question: can his conductive ink be airbrushed? He demonstrates the process live, showing how to prepare a thinned, filtered version of the ink suitable for an airbrush with a 2.3 micron nozzle. The video concludes with a sheet resistance measurement confirming the airbrushed film is genuinely conductive.
