## Overview
"On The Trail" is a short, lighthearted video in which Robert Murray-Smith sings "On the Trail of the Lonesome Pine," a classic American folk song, apparently filmed outdoors. The video is not a science video — it was primarily a test of a new microphone (the Zoom H2N), shared with his audience in a playful spirit.

- Performance of the folk song "On the Trail of the Lonesome Pine"
- Audio/microphone testing in a real-world outdoor environment
