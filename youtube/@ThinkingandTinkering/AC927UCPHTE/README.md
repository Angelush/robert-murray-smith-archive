## Overview

Robert recounts his failed attempt to travel from the UK to Germany in June 2022, describing the chaos at St Pancras station — overcrowded halls, cancelled Eurostar trains, and a queue of 200 people for two information desk staff. He frames the experience as a "perfect storm" of overlapping crises (ferry shortages, airline over-booking, post-COVID staffing gaps, and a midterm school holiday), ultimately giving up and rescheduling his trip.
