## Overview

This video is the second in Robert Murray-Smith's series on brush electroplating, focused entirely on preparing the universal plating electrolyte solution from household chemicals. Robert demonstrates step-by-step how to mix ammonia, sulfuric acid, sodium hydroxide, and phosphoric acid to produce a versatile conductive plating solution. The key takeaway is that a fully functional, universal electroplating solution can be made at home for roughly £2–3.
