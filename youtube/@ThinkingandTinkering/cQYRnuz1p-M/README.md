## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith follows up on a previous video about a cheap electrolyzer for hydrogen generation, addressing viewer feedback — particularly around gas separation. He demonstrates two improvements: a H-bridge (Hofmann-style) split cell design that physically separates hydrogen from other gases, and the use of activated carbon felt wrapped around the electrodes to dramatically increase surface area and offset the efficiency loss caused by the increased electrode distance.
