## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores whether his homemade wind turbine — built from scrap materials for under £100 — can theoretically achieve 1 kilowatt of output. He walks through Betz Law, the fundamental physical limit governing all wind turbine designs, and applies it to his specific rotor dimensions to show that 1kW is achievable at high wind speeds with sufficient efficiency. The video is part of his ongoing learning-in-public series on building the turbine.
