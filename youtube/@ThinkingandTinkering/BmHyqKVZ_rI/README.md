## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith and his collaborator Luke each transform donated car seats (courtesy of Aiden from Canterbury Automotive) into two distinct motorized vehicles — Robert modifies an existing tricycle by mounting the car seat onto it, while Luke builds a quad entirely from scratch. The video culminates in a race across a car park, and closes with a reflection on two valid maker approaches: modifying an existing build versus building from the ground up.
