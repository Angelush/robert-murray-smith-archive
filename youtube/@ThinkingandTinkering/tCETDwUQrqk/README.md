## Overview

Robert revisits the Elegoo Neptune 3 Pro 3D printer (pre-release, ~£200) to explore its potential for fabricating vertical axis wind turbine (VAWT) components, specifically printing a Gorlov/Gorlovinsky-type helical turbine downloaded from Thingiverse. He prints five sections of the double-helix turbine, noting the printer's quiet operation and large build volume make it practical for producing complex, large-scale parts that would be very difficult to make by hand, with a view to assembling them into a "wind wall" array for testing.
