## Overview

Robert takes the orrery mechanism he built in video 2306 and repurposes it as a 3D-printed Moon Nightlight Mobile for children, featuring a lithophane moon globe lit from within by an LED and Apollo mission spacecraft orbiting it via a hand-cranked worm-gear drive. The key insight is that the mechanism is modular and holiday-agnostic — the central globe and orbiting figures can be swapped for Halloween (pumpkin, ghost, jack-o'-lantern) or Christmas theming, making it a versatile, interactive gift.
