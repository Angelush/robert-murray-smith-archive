## Overview

In this third installment of his rotating ventilator wind power series, Robert Murray-Smith constructs the stator — the fixed coil-bearing ring — for a small homemade generator. He details how to form a circular band from uPVC builders board, mount hand-wound coils inside it, add structural rings, and build a central guide block, producing a lightweight yet rigid stator ready to receive the rotor in the next video.
