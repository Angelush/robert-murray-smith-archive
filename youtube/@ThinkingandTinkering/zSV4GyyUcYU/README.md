## Overview

A short philosophical video in which Robert uses the "Parable of the Chimpanzees" — a behavioural experiment where chimps learn to avoid a ladder due to cold water spraying, and pass this avoidance on to generations who never experienced the spray — to illustrate how inherited assumptions can persist long after the original reason has disappeared. He applies this to a specific electronics debate: whether a resistive load is the only valid way to test a generator's output, arguing that charging a capacitor and measuring its voltage is equally informative.
