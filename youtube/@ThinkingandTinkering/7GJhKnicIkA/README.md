## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to make a wooden chessboard from inexpensive chopping boards, cutting them into precise squares and gluing them together in strips. The project costs just a few pounds in materials and results in a functional, attractive board finished with wax, shown off with a Doctor Who chess set.
