## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates an improved design for a large-scale copper coil alcohol burner that overcomes the key limitation of traditional coil burners — their inability to handle large fuel reservoirs. By using two concentric copper pipes with an activated carbon felt wick between them, he creates a self-heating vaporizer that burns ethanol cleanly and at high heat output. The device is then adapted into a practical space heater using a stainless steel mantle and a terracotta plant pot.
