## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents a 2024 paper from UC Santa Barbara researchers who designed a passive, power-free roofing tile that automatically switches between heat-absorbing and heat-reflecting modes using a wax motor actuator. The system combines a super-black coating, a radiative-cooling white paint, and a wax-powered mechanical linkage to cut household heating and cooling energy use by up to 2.5× compared to static alternatives. Robert highlights the elegance and robustness of the design and notes it is accessible enough for DIY experimentation.
