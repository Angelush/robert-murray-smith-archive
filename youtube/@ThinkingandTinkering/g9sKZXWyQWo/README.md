## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a glass speaker he built by attaching a "soundbug" transducer (driven by an audio amplifier) to a pane of glass, following up on an earlier video where sound quality was difficult to convey. The video plays music through the device to showcase the sound quality achievable, and Robert discusses plans to scale up using a long, thin bath shelf of toughened glass to improve treble and bass response.
