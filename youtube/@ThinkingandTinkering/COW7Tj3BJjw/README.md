## Overview

Robert introduces "builders board" — a cellular PVC foam board commonly used in roofline construction (fascias and soffits) — as an exceptionally versatile and easy-to-work model-making material. He covers its physical properties, available sizes, suppliers, and adhesive options, then demonstrates its use by building a functional lab beam balance from scratch.
