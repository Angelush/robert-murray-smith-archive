## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to repurpose old smartphone batteries by pairing them with inexpensive USB charge/protection boards to make battery-powered household devices rechargeable. Building on his earlier videos (1272 and 1273) about testing and preparing used phone batteries, he shows that a single bare phone battery with an 83p charge board can replace disposable AA batteries in devices like fairy lights, radios, torches, and remote controls. The core takeaway is that this simple, cheap modification eliminates the need for disposable batteries across a wide range of everyday devices.
