## Overview

This video is a competition entry submitted by Gerald Dupuis from Michigan, USA, for Robert Murray-Smith's water wheel challenge. Gerald demonstrates a small water wheel generator built almost entirely from recycled and reclaimed components, inspired by Robert's earlier videos on water turbines and stepper motor generators. The build successfully generates enough electricity to light an LED.
