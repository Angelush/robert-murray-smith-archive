## Overview

Robert demonstrates low-cost hydrogen production via water electrolysis, exploring two enhancements: replacing standard stainless steel electrodes with carbon felt (activated carbon) to increase surface area, and applying an external magnetic field to boost hydrogen output. The key takeaway is that both techniques significantly increase the rate of hydrogen gas production, with the magnetic field effect — reportedly doubling output based on a 2020 paper — being the more surprising finding.
