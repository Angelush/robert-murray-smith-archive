## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith makes the case that the Dynapod — his human-powered, pedal-driven mechanical platform — is an essential piece of emergency preparedness equipment. He argues that directly coupling pedal power to mechanical tools is far more efficient than converting it to electricity first, where 40–70% of energy is lost. He calls for the Dynapod to become a commercial product and envisions it as a versatile, off-grid solution for washing, refrigeration, communication, and more.
