## Overview

Robert uses this video to introduce the general principles behind electrical generators, arguing that a PC fan, a car alternator, and a washing machine motor are all fundamentally the same device. He incorporates excerpts from a 1960s U.S. Army training film, which he regards as the clearest explanation of generator fundamentals he has ever encountered, covering EMF generation through to DC output and winding configurations.
