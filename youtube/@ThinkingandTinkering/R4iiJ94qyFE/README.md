## Overview

This is Part 2 of a bike roller build project, presented by Luke, in which he responds to viewer feedback by fabricating sturdy upright angle supports to hold the bike steady during use. The uprights are built using a composite layering technique and are demonstrated successfully, allowing hands-free cycling on the roller. A further part is teased at the end.
