## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith combines two previously built components — a push-button mechanism and a modular generator — into an integrated "Magic Button Generator." By adding compression springs and reprinting adapter plates to fit a bearing, he completes the assembly and demonstrates it generating 4–6 volts from repeated button presses. The key takeaway is that any source of vibrational or mechanical energy (footsteps, sitting, wind) can drive this button-rotation-generation chain to harvest useful electricity.
