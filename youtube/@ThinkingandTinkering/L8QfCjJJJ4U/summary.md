## Overview
A festive Christmas poem written and narrated by Robert Murray-Smith, set in his laboratory on Christmas Eve. It is a playful parody of "A Visit from St. Nicholas" (Twas the Night Before Christmas), in which Santa's sleigh crashes and Robert rescues the situation by providing a wood-gas quad bike from his lab. The poem is a lighthearted seasonal greeting blending his scientific world with holiday tradition.

## Key Topics
- Christmas Eve narrative poem set in Robert's laboratory
- Parody of "A Visit from St. Nicholas" (Twas the Night Before Christmas)
- Santa's overloaded sleigh crashing and being repaired
- A wood-gas powered quad bike as a substitute vehicle
- A personal, humorous greeting to his audience

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Wood gas (used to power the quad bike gasifier)
- Wood (feedstock for the gasifier)

## Techniques and Methods
- Gasification (wood gasifier powering a quad bike, referenced as something Robert previously demonstrated on YouTube)
- Welding (mentioned as part of the quad bike build shown in a prior video)
