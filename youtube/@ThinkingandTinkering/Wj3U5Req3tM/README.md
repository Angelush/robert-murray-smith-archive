## Overview

Robert introduces his plan to build an electric trike with a paper frame — a follow-up to his earlier paper bike project. He shares free trike plans (based on the Catrike Expedition's geometry), explains his design choice of two rear wheels and one front wheel, and previews the upcoming build series. The key takeaway is that redesigned paper "sticks," a cheap rear axle, and a 500W motor will combine into a trike he intends to actually keep rather than recycle for parts.
