## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith teaches his friend Dave (a complete beginner) how to MIG/stick weld in roughly 15 minutes, using a simple modern welder on thick and thin steel plate. The video demonstrates that welding is far more accessible than most people assume, with the biggest barrier being psychological rather than technical. By the end, Dave has successfully welded two pieces of angle iron together.
