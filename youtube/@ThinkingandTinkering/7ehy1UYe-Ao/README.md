## Overview

Robert delivers a short editorial rant arguing that personal responsibility for safety is being eroded in modern society. Using two darkly comic anecdotes — a man who scratched himself with a chainsaw and a Canadian who sued an RV manufacturer after leaving cruise control to make tea — he contends that individuals, not content creators, bear the primary obligation to understand the risks of their own actions. He applies this directly to his chemistry videos, pushing back on viewers who demand more safety warnings.
