## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a simple hack for making gears by cutting up toothed timing belts (the kind used in car engines and 3D printers) and gluing them onto circular objects. He presents this as a practical solution to a longstanding frustration with gear-making in DIY motor and machine projects, showing how a strip of belt glued to a flywheel creates a functional, durable cog with very little effort.
