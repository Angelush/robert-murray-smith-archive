## Overview

Robert demonstrates why he uses a cement mixer in his lab — specifically to produce large batches of "UltraSand," a carbon-coated sand material used for water filtration. The process scales up a home experiment shown in a previous video, allowing him to produce up to 100 kg of UltraSand per batch by coating pre-washed sand with sugar solution, then carbonizing it with sulfuric acid and heat.
