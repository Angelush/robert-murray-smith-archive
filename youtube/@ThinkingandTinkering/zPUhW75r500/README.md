## Overview

Part 8 of Robert's DIY gasification series, documenting the first live test of his home-built gasifier unit. He fires it up using charcoal, successfully produces combustible syngas, and demonstrates it burning at a striking distance from the output pipe. The main takeaway is that the unit works well — in fact, too well — producing more gas than needed, requiring future tuning to reduce airflow and output volume.
