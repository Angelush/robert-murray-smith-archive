## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith compares three different sandblasting media for use with a pressure washer sandblasting attachment: glass frit, Kärcher professional black grit, and ordinary kiln-dried builder's sand. All three performed similarly in cleaning power, but builder's sand won decisively on cost and ease of use. His main takeaway is that cheap kiln-dried sharp builder's sand is the best practical choice for this application.
