## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores a major breakthrough in thermoelectric generation: research from Duke University and Montana State University found that magnesium-based alloys (specifically magnesium antimony / magnesium antimonate) perform three to four times better than conventional thermoelectric materials like bismuth compounds. He demonstrates a simple live experiment using a magnesium ribbon in point contact with copper, measuring voltage and charging a capacitor, confirming real energy generation from the junction.
