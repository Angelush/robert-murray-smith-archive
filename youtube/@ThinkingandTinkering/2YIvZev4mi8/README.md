## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores research from Australia showing that biochar made from spent coffee grounds can be added to concrete to make it up to 30% stronger. He explains the science behind pozzolanic materials dating back to Roman concrete, then demonstrates his own pyrolysis process for converting coffee grounds into biochar. The video highlights both the strength benefits and the broader environmental advantages of this technique.
