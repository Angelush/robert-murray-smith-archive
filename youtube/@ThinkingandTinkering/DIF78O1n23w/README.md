## Overview

A short Advent Science Magic video (Day 9) in which Robert demonstrates the "glitter finger" trick: placing glitter on water and then touching it with a soapy finger causes the glitter to rapidly flee to the edges. He uses this simple visual to explain the molecular structure of soap and why it is effective at removing dirt and oil from skin.
