## Overview

A short personal video in which Robert Murray-Smith shares the story of his cat going missing for approximately a week — likely trapped in a neighbour's shed — before returning home thin and exhausted. Relieved by her return, Robert fitted her with a radio tracking device so she can be located if she ever disappears again.
