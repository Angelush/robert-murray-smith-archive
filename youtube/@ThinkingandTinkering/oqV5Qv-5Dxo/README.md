## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds a hand-cranked crystal generator (electricity generator) entirely with traditional tools — no laser cutter — to prove a point: anything made on a laser cutter can be replicated with conventional workshop tools. He walks through each of the five main components step by step, introducing a clever "instant gear" technique using power transmission belt. The finished device successfully lights a bulb, demonstrating over 20 volts of output.
