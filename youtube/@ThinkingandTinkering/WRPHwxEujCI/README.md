## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith announces the launch of a new secondary YouTube channel called "Welcome to the World of TnT" (Thinking and Tinkering). The channel is a humor-focused offshoot of his Twitch stream, created with his collaborator Luke, featuring skits, absurd day-to-day situations, and comedic content rather than experiments. He encourages viewers to visit, engage, and even argue in the comments for the sake of engagement metrics.
