## Overview

Robert demonstrates a simplified approach to building a Joule Thief circuit by replacing the traditional ferrite toroid core with a hand-rolled paper tube wound with copper wire. He then extends the design by adding a second bifilar coil over the first, enabling wireless near-field power transmission to a second LED with no direct electrical connection.
