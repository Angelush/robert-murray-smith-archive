## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith revisits EcoFoam, a versatile cleaning foam he developed and previously featured in earlier videos. In this update, he introduces Sam, a collaborator who has taken on the task of developing and promoting EcoFoam commercially. The video showcases before-and-after footage of the foam cleaning a wide variety of surfaces and issues a public challenge to find something it cannot clean.

## Key Topics
- Re-introduction of EcoFoam after a hiatus from earlier videos
- Introduction of Sam as the person leading EcoFoam's development and outreach
- Demonstration of EcoFoam's cleaning ability across multiple surfaces (cookers, carpets, upholstery)
- Community challenge: find something EcoFoam cannot clean
- Call to action for viewers to contact Sam directly with cleaning challenges

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- **EcoFoam** — a foaming cleaning product invented by Robert Murray-Smith; described as capable of cleaning a wide range of surfaces and stains

## Techniques and Methods

Robert's comment replies are mostly brief and conversational, but a few notable points emerge:
