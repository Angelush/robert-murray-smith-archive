## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces and demonstrates a mechanical curiosity: a gear system using two identically-sized gears that nonetheless produces a 2:1 reduction ratio — something long considered impossible. He explains gear ratio fundamentals before revealing and assembling a 3D-printed mechanism that defies the conventional assumption that same-size gears must yield a 1:1 output. As a bonus, the mechanism simultaneously splits power across two axles.
