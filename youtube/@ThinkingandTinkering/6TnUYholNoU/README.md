## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces Oogoo, a DIY alternative to the commercial moldable rubber product Sugru, made from silicone sealant and cornflour. He explains the chemistry behind both products and demonstrates how to make Oogoo at home for a fraction of the cost. The main takeaway is that a near-identical substitute to Sugru can be made for roughly £15–20/kg versus Sugru's ~£550/kg, with the only trade-off being a vinegar smell during curing.
