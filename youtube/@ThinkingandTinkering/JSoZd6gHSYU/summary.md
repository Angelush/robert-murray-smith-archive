## Overview
A short Christmas advent calendar video (Day 21) in which Robert demonstrates how to make a decorative glitter ball using two balloons and water. By nesting a water-filled transparent balloon inside a red balloon with cut holes, the result is a visually striking glitter-filled orb that looks like a magic trick.

## Key Topics
- Christmas advent science magic series (Day 21)
- DIY glitter balloon construction
- Simple household science craft

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Red balloon — outer shell with holes cut into it
- Transparent balloon — inner balloon filled with water and glitter
- Water — used to fill the inner balloon
- Glitter — added to the water inside the transparent balloon
- Rubber band — used to secure both balloons together

## Techniques and Methods
- Cutting ~1 cm holes in the outer (red) balloon
- Stuffing the transparent balloon inside the red balloon
- Filling the inner balloon with water
- Using a funnel to add glitter into the transparent balloon
- Wrapping a rubber band around both balloons to seal the assembly
