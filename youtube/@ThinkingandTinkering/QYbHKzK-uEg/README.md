## Overview

This video explores the classic "drinking bird" (dippy bird) toy as the inspiration for a new clean energy generator, based on research from China and Hong Kong. Robert explains the thermodynamic principles behind the toy, then demonstrates triboelectric generation as the key innovation that makes the concept viable. The central takeaway is that water evaporation — which absorbs roughly 50% of all solar energy hitting Earth — represents a vast untapped energy source that a simple toy has helped unlock.
