## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith, drawing on 30 years of experience developing batteries and supercapacitors, argues that making your own batteries for home energy storage is practically infeasible for most people due to the complexity, cost, and precision required. Rather than defaulting to conventional batteries, he encourages viewers to explore the wide range of alternative energy storage technologies that are far better suited to home use.
