## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores gravity batteries as a practical, low-cost alternative to electrochemical batteries for energy storage. He demonstrates a 3D-printed escapement mechanism — borrowed from pendulum clock design — as a key control component for regulating the controlled fall of a weight, showing it working live. The video was inspired by a high school student in the Philippines and all design files are released to the public domain.
