## Overview

Robert uses a bicycle gear cluster as a jumping-off point to argue against consumer over-engineering and advertising-driven purchasing decisions. He contends that most cyclists only ever use 3–4 gears regardless of how many they have, and that chasing the "best" spec is largely a response to marketing rather than genuine need. The video ties into his ongoing paper bike build project, where he plans to fit the gear cluster to a recumbent bike made from rolled newspaper.
