## Overview

Luke (a collaborator on Robert Murray-Smith's channel) repurposes a bamboo quad bike — originally built by Rob in video #1155 — by converting it into a reverse-configuration electric trike with two wheels at the front and one at the back. The first part of the build focuses on fabricating and attaching the rear wheel assembly using modified bicycle back stays, welding, and structural support brackets.
