## Overview

This is a short advent calendar video (Day 8) in which Robert demonstrates the classic "missing square" or "extra piece" dissection puzzle. By cutting a 5×4 rectangle, rearranging the pieces, and reassembling them, he appears to produce an identical 5×4 rectangle with a leftover spare piece — seemingly creating matter from nothing. He cheekily notes the trick also works well with chocolate.
