## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains his approach to the review/sponsorship products he receives, and what happens to them once he's done. After a costly incident where a competition prize went missing en route to Australia (costing him £400 compensation), he stopped running giveaway competitions. He now simply gives reviewed equipment away — primarily to local schools, colleges, and his local church — and films this video from the churchyard where his wife Patti is buried.
