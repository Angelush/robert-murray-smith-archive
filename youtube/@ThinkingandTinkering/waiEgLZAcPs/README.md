## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith walks through how to design and 3D print a ratchet and pawl (pole) mechanism using Tinkercad. He demonstrates the step-by-step construction of a 12-tooth, 40mm ratchet and its corresponding pawl, then slices and prints both parts using Cura. The video fills a gap from earlier videos where he showed ratchets in use but had not yet explained how to model them from scratch.
