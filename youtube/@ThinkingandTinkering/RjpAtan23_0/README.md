## Overview

Robert demonstrates his newly completed waterproof conductive ink by using it to electroplate copper onto a painted plastic surface. The video shows the full acid copper electroplating process — from setting up the bath chemistry to depositing copper — and introduces the concept of electroforming as an alternative use case. The key takeaway is that the conductive ink performs well as a plating base even without industrial surface preparation.
