## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains how rocket stoves work by tracing their principles back to the 1780 Argand lamp, which demonstrated that a chimney dramatically improves combustion efficiency. He walks through the three stages of wood combustion and shows how a rocket stove's design captures and burns the secondary gases that would otherwise escape in an open fire, achieving a much more complete burn.
