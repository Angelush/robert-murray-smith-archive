## Overview
This short video announces the winner of a giveaway draw for 250ml of Robert Murray-Smith's conductive ink. A numbered ticket was drawn from a coffee pot by Luke, with ticket number 39 (belonging to a viewer named Marcel) winning. Robert briefly highlights the many uses for the ink and points viewers to related project videos on the channel.
