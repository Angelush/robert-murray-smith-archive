## Overview
A short advent calendar magic trick video in which Robert demonstrates how to fold and cut a small square of paper so that a red circle — seemingly too large to fit — can be passed through it. The trick relies on a clever origami-style folding technique that transforms the square hole into a larger opening.

## Key Topics
- Advent calendar science magic (Day 19)
- The classic "squaring the circle" paper puzzle
- How folding can increase the effective size of a hole in paper

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Blue piece of paper (square)
- Red circle (cutout)

## Techniques and Methods
- Folding paper diagonally to cut a triangle, creating a square hole
- Making a series of strategic folds (folding edges to the center, then over)
- Pinching and twisting the folded paper to open a larger aperture
- Sliding the circle through the expanded opening
