## Overview

A short, lighthearted video in which Robert revisits his earlier human-powered generator concept and pivots to exploring the long history of animals — dogs, goats, pigs, donkeys, oxen, and horses — being used as motive power "engines." He surveys historical examples from the turnspIt dog to Victorian "pooch mobiles," concluding with a tongue-in-cheek suggestion that animal (or child) power could serve as an unconventional answer to the energy crisis.
