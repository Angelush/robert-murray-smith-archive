## Overview

A short maker tutorial showing how to convert a standard kitchen rolling pin into a rubber-surfaced DIY roller. By removing the handles, threading the central bar, wrapping the barrel in rubber matting (held with spray adhesive), and fitting a custom wooden handle, the result is a versatile workshop roller suitable for the many applications demonstrated in the companion video #1360.
