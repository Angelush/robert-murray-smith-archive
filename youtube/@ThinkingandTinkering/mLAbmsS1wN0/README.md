## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how a 12V car winch (rated for 2-ton pulls) can be repurposed as an electrical generator, since DC motors and generators are functionally identical devices. He disassembles the winch to examine its planetary gearbox, then reverses the gearbox orientation to turn the previously driven motor shaft into the output drive shaft. The experiment produces a small but measurable voltage and current (~0.7V, ~50mA), with the main limitation being an impractical gear ratio inherited from the winch's original purpose.
