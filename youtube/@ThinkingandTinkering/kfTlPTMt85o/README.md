## Overview

Robert redesigned a heat pipe and fitted it inside a commercial evacuated solar tube to test its thermal transfer efficiency. After exposing the assembly to sunlight (limited by England's weather), he measured temperatures at four points and found the heat pipe able to transfer nearly all of the tube's collected heat to the heat pipe head, achieving over 95% efficiency. He notes the results are promising despite the improvised filming conditions.
