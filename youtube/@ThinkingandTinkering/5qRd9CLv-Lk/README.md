## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces and demonstrates his graphene-based conductive ink, highlighting a packaging change from plastic bottles to recyclable tins for environmental reasons. The ink combines graphene with graphite to achieve excellent conductivity at a practical price point, and is suitable for applications including electroplating, EMF shielding, and silkscreen printing. The main takeaway is that this thin, conforming ink preserves surface detail while delivering high conductivity.
