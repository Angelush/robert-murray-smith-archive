## Overview

This video is a community competition entry submitted to Robert Murray-Smith by a viewer named Fosse, who attempted to build a water-driven turbine housed inside a 4-inch PVC pipe (repurposing a plant pot concept) as part of Robert's "generate electricity with water by means of motion" competition. Fosse's goal was to integrate the turbine into a hydroponics system, using the existing water pump to spin the turbine for aeration, visual status indication via an LED, and personal mental stimulation. While individual components worked, the full combined system was not fully operational by the submission deadline.
