## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith shares a quick update recommending a commercially available uPVC cleaner (Cesto Type 10) as an effective solvent for liquid phase exfoliation of graphene. While acetone-water mixtures are safer for home use, Type 10 — predominantly ethyl acetate with co-solvents — produces stable graphene colloidal dispersions after extended low-power sonication. The video is a practical tip for DIY graphene makers, with a note on volatility hazards.
