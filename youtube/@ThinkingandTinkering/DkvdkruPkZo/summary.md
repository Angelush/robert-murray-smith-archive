## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith builds the Moyustore DM13-1 inline 4-cylinder model engine kit, walking through the full assembly process step by step. The video showcases the high build quality of the kit — machined aluminium and stainless steel components with tight tolerances — and concludes with the completed engine running under its own electric motor drive.

## Key Topics
- Unboxing and parts inspection of the DM13-1 model engine kit
- Step-by-step assembly of a 4-cylinder engine model
- Quality assessment of machined aluminium and stainless steel components
- Integration of electric motor ("starter motor") and generator assemblies
- Chain drive, belt drive, and tensioner assembly
- Final powered demonstration of the completed model

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Anodised aluminium (engine block, pistons, body components)
- Stainless steel (various fittings and inserts)
- Rubber O-rings (used on exhaust plate fittings)
- Lubricating oil (applied to pistons, crankshaft, and connecting rod assemblies)

## Techniques and Methods
- Laying out components by sub-assembly before building (Robert's habitual method)
- Pre-oiling moving parts (pistons, crankshaft big-ends) before assembly
- Installing circlips by hand/fingernail without specialist pliers
- Gear timing mark alignment (matching marks on crankcase and timing cog before fitting chain)
- Belt tensioner adjustment via tensioning screw
- Angled snap-fit technique for fitting the crankcase cover
