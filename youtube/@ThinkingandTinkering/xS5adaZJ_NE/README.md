## Overview

In this video, Luke builds a DIY wireless Bluetooth speaker using salvaged components, including a battery pack made from old mobile phone batteries (covered in video #1542). The build uses an acrylic enclosure, a pre-built control board with Bluetooth/aux input, and a voltage regulator to step down the battery voltage to 5V USB. The final result is a compact, functional portable speaker he plans to keep in his car.
