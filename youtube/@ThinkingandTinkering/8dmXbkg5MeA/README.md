## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith offers practical advice on managing a YouTube channel's community, arguing that the comments section is the primary space for creator-audience interaction and must be actively moderated. His central conclusion is that self-policing never works — it is the creator's responsibility to "weed" the community just as one would a garden, removing bad actors while nurturing the genuine audience at the center.
