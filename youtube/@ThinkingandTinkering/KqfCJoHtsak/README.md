## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a simple pinwheel-style wind generator from recycled LCD screen plastic (or paper/card), a repurposed PC fan motor, and PVC builders' board. The project is presented as a quick, cheap, and educational homeschool activity covering basic principles of wind and electricity generation. Even in near-calm conditions, the finished generator produces around half a volt.
