## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds a "Bellows Drive" — a composite machine that combines his previously designed hyper cycloid drive with a dual-bellows system and check valves to produce a directed airstream. The airstream is intended to spin a blisk turbine and generate electricity. Along the way, he discusses why bellows fell out of favour compared to centrifugal fans and makes the case for creative, exploratory machine-building.
