## Overview

In this concluding video of his Wimshurst machine series, Robert Murray-Smith finishes the build by constructing and fitting the neutralizer brushes and current collectors, then successfully tests the machine. The video opens with a clear explanation of electrostatic induction — the physical principle behind how the machine generates high voltage — before walking through the final fabrication steps. The machine works as intended, producing visible sparks, and Robert notes the output voltage surprised him given the modest disk size.
