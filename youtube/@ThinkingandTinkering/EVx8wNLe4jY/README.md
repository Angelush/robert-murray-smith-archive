## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith revisits the classic childhood cotton reel tank toy, identifying its core flaw: the rubber band powering it creates axial clamping force that makes the mechanism harder to turn the more it is wound. He designs a 3D-printed replacement using TinkerCAD, incorporating a thrust bearing to absorb those axial forces, resulting in a smooth, free-spinning rubber band motor module he intends to use as a foundation for future projects.
