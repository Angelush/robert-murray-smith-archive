## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to build wind turbine blades entirely from bamboo, splitting a 4-meter pole into three blade sections using basic hand tools. He mounts the finished blades on a radiator fan motor atop a bamboo pole and tests the turbine outdoors, producing around half a watt in very light winds. The video argues that bamboo is an ideal, genuinely renewable material for DIY wind turbine construction.
