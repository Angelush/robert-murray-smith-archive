## Overview
This is a short announcement video by Patty Murray-Smith (Rob's wife) and Emily from FWG, previewing an upcoming live event on 24 August 2019. The event will cover the differences between batteries, capacitors, supercapacitors, and fuel cells, with live demonstrations from Rob (plant fuel cell) and Steve (hydrogen fuel cell). The video also briefly showcases new electroplating solution kits and a forthcoming children's lab kit.

## Key Topics
- Upcoming FWG live event: batteries, capacitors, supercapacitors, and fuel cells explained
- Rob's plant battery kit demonstration (technically a fuel cell producing energy from garden plants)
- Steve's hydrogen fuel cell demonstration, referencing its role in the Apollo moon landing as a British invention
- New all-in-one electroplating solution kits (zinc, copper, nickel) designed for beginners
- Planned children's starter lab kit (ages 6+) with basic lab and safety equipment
- Teaser of a second live event on electroplating for crafts

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- **Plant-based fuel cell** — used in Rob's demonstration kit to generate energy from garden/backyard plants
- **Hydrogen** — used in Steve's fuel cell demonstration
- **Zinc, copper, nickel** — the three electroplating solutions offered in the new kit
- **Cyanides** — mentioned as typical hazardous chemicals found in traditional electroplating solutions (used as a contrast to justify the simpler, safer FWG product)

## Techniques and Methods
- **Plant/microbial fuel cell operation** — extracting electrical energy from plant material in a backyard setting
- **Hydrogen fuel cell demonstration** — building and running a hydrogen fuel cell
- **Electroplating** — simplified all-in-one solution process for depositing zinc, copper, or nickel onto objects; contrasted with conventional multi-chemical approaches
