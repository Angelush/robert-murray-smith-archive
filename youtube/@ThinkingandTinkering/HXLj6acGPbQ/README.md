## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a curious phenomenon shared by a friend: a neodymium hard-drive magnet suspended from a waxed cotton thread begins to spin spontaneously and accelerates on its own. Rather than claiming perpetual motion or free energy, Robert presents this as a prompt for open-minded scientific investigation, paired with a companion video on that theme.
