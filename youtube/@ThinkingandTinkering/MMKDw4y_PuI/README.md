## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith visits the Deal Time Ball in England as a thematic starting point for exploring mechanical power transmission via chains. He demonstrates a 3D-printed chain of his own design, remixed from Thingiverse, and shows a surprising phenomenon: when spun at speed on a cog, a free-hanging chain appears to levitate and self-align to drive a second cog — a curious emergent mechanical behaviour he calls the "floating or levitating transmission."
