## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith follows up his earlier "giant wall radiator" video by addressing the legal and regulatory landscape for anyone wishing to commercialise the conductive heating ink technology. He explains that while building one-off installations is relatively straightforward, selling or professionally installing such devices requires navigating electrical competency requirements, product safety certification, and materials safety documentation — a process he describes as the "other half of the job" when bringing something to market.
