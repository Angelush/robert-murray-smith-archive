## Overview

In this short Christmas advent video, Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a simple paper chromatography experiment using kitchen towel and washable markers. As water travels up the paper by capillary action, it separates the ink colours by particle weight, creating a rainbow effect. The video is aimed at families looking for fun, safe science activities to do with children over Christmas.
