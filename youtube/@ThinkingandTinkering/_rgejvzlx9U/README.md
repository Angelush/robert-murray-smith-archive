## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to reduce graphene oxide into graphene using green tea extract — a safe, non-toxic process aligned with the principles of green chemistry. He prepares a green tea extract by steeping tea bags in water, mixes it with a dilute graphene oxide solution, and heats it at 90°C for 10 hours with stirring. The result is a jet-black graphene suspension produced without any harsh chemicals.
