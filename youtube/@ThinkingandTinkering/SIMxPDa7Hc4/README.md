## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates building a DIY Fresnel lens by coiling 7 metres of clear polypropylene tube, filling it with water, and testing whether it can focus light. The experiment successfully focuses sunlight enough to burn paper, confirming the coiled water-filled tube functions as a Fresnel lens. He credits viewer "Iz pilot land" for suggesting the idea.
