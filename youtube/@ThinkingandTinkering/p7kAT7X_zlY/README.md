## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith replicates and explains the Harmony Wind Turbine, a design by Christopher Moore inspired by the classic Savonius (Cavonius) turbine concept from 1922. The turbine's key innovation is self-furling scoop blades that open and close to adapt to wind speed, allowing operation across a wider range of conditions without the risk of over-spinning. Robert designed and 3D printed a working model to demonstrate the mechanism.
