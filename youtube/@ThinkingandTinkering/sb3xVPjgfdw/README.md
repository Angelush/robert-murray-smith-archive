## Overview

Robert demonstrates a template-and-tape method for making bellows of any size, using a printed card template and PVC-fabric tape. The resulting bellows are airtight, light-tight, springy, and durable — with a leatherette feel. The key advantage of this approach over his previous methods is that once a template is designed on a computer, it can be reprinted to produce identical bellows repeatedly, and the geometry can be adapted to virtually any shape.
