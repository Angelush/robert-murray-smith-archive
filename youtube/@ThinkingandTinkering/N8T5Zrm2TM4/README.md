## Overview

Luke continues building his DIY scooter by addressing a structural flaw (the front end sitting too low) and begins the electrification process. He modifies the frame through welding, redesigns the footrest to clear space for a motor, and fabricates a custom drive sprocket system using a salvaged bicycle cassette cog. Part 1 concludes with a successful test of the motor spinning the rear wheel via a motor controller.
