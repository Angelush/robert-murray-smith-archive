## Techniques and Methods
- **Painting resistors**: varying line width, cross-sectional thickness, or path length to control resistance value
- **Painting a potentiometer**: using a movable contact arm across a painted resistive track
- **Painting parallel-plate capacitors**: applying conductive ink squares to opposite faces of card/paper
- **Stacking capacitors in parallel**: layering painted card sheets and connecting plates to increase total capacitance
- **RC timing circuit design**: pairing a painted capacitor and resistor to set a time constant
- **555 astable configuration**: connecting external R and C components to pins 2, 6, 7, 8 of the 555 to generate a square-wave output at pin 3
- **Frequency calculation**: f = 1.44 / (Ra + 2Rb) × C
- **Duty cycle adjustment**: modifying the ratio of Ra and Rb
