## Overview

Robert provides a brief constructional walkthrough of two prototype Tesla (bladeless/disc) turbine generators he has built, comparing their designs side by side. He explains how magnet placement, disc size, and the geometry of the air-inlet lip directly affect efficiency, and notes that a third prototype is already in development with further improvements planned.
