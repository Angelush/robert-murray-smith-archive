## Overview

Robert unboxes and reviews the Creality CR Scan Ferret Pro 3D scanner, walks through its setup with a wireless bridge connected to a Surface Pro laptop, and uses it to scan the life-size bronze statue of Amy Johnson at Herne Bay. The resulting 3D model was cleaned up, exported as an STL, scaled down, and printed on a Mingda Magician Pro 2 — demonstrating a complete scan-to-print workflow with high praise for the scanner's ease of use.
