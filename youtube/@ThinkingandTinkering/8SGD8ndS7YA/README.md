## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains why solid-state batteries are a major focus of research — enabling lithium metal anodes while avoiding dendrite formation — and covers a landmark collaboration between Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL) and Microsoft. Using Microsoft's Azure Quantum Elements AI tools, researchers filtered 30 million theoretical battery material candidates down to 18 viable options in just 80 hours, identifying a mixed metal chloride compound using 70% less lithium while maintaining energy density. Robert uses this result as a springboard to argue that AI is fundamentally a tool that accelerates human-directed work, not a replacement for human creativity.
