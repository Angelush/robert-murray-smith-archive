## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the motivation behind his bellows-and-piston build, revealing it was inspired by an existing wave power design that uses an oscillating water column to drive a turbine. He argues that replacing the specially designed bidirectional turbine with bellows linked to a standard generator could make wave power affordable and globally accessible. The video is a short addendum clarifying the real direction of his experiments for viewers who were too focused on the vacuum cleaner demo.
