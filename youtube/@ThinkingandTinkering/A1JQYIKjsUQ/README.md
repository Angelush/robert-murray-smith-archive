## Overview

Robert demonstrates a simplified approach to solar water desalination using carbon felt coated with graphene ink, presented as an accessible alternative to his previous MIT-inspired method using carbonized Oasis foam. By painting graphene ink onto the top surface of carbon felt and floating it on saltwater under a heat lamp, he achieves rapid steam generation that can be condensed into pure water. His key takeaway is that this method eliminates the need for dangerous chemicals or complex fabrication, making it potentially viable for low-cost, large-scale deployment in water-scarce regions.
