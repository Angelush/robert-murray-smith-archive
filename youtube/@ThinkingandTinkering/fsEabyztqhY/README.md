## Overview

This short update video shows Robert testing an iteration of his chlorophyll-based solar cell outdoors in direct sunlight for the first time, after previous tests had only been conducted under fluorescent lighting. The key finding is that the cell produces roughly three times more current in sunlight (~300 mA equivalent reading) compared to indoor fluorescent light (~100 mA), though absolute output remains modest at approximately 11 mW per square meter.
