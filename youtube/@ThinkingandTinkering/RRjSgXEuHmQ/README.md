## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reviews the Vevor 12V 8kW diesel heater, priced at around £102.99, by fully disassembling it to examine its construction and engineering. He explains how it uses a convection-based design to isolate the combustion chamber from the living space, then reassembles it and runs it successfully. The video concludes that it represents good value and also serves as a study in how to construct similar heaters yourself.
