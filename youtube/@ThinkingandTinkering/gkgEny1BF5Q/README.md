## Overview

Robert waterproofs the internal electronics of a PC fan (previously repurposed as a wind turbine generator) using Plasti-Dip rubber coating, then tests it under a running tap to see if it can function as a water turbine. The experiment succeeds, generating approximately 1.7 volts and lighting an LED, demonstrating that a simple dip-coat waterproofing treatment is sufficient to convert a PC fan into a working water turbine without affecting the mechanical tolerances.
