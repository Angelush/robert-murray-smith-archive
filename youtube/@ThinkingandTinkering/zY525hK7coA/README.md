## Overview

Robert examines the WWII-era GN44 (GM44) hand-cranked military field generator, explaining how its alternator, commutators, and electromechanical voltage regulator worked together to produce dual voltage outputs for field radios. He then replicates the concept using a modified exercise bike as the mechanical frame, fitting neodymium magnets to the flywheel and a salvaged microwave oven fan coil to demonstrate DIY electricity generation.
