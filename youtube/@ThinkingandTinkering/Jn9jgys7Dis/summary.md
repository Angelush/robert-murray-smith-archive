## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith recruits friends to test-ride the ESKUTE Voyager electric mountain bike on his behalf, openly admitting he felt unsuitable as a rider due to age and arthritis. Multiple riders — including Russ from the "Intelligent Tinkering" channel — give hands-on impressions of the bike's handling, brakes, and build quality. The overall verdict is positive: the bike is well-built, responsive, and accessible to a broad range of riders.
