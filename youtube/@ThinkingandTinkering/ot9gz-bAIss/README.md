## Overview

This video is a foundational introduction to the science of making inks and paints, explaining that both share the same three essential components: a functional material, a carrier, and a binder. Robert uses these principles as a framework for his broader work on functional inks — particularly conductive inks for batteries, solar cells, and printed electronics. The video establishes the conceptual vocabulary needed to understand his more advanced follow-up videos.
