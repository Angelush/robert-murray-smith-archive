## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to convert a PC fan into a hand-crank generator by building a simple gear system from everyday materials. The key insight is not the generator itself, but the cog-making technique: cutting circles from builders board (PVC-coated foam board) and gluing toothed belt around the edges to create functional gears with controllable gear ratios. The finished device produces measurable AC voltage (~2.6V) just from hand-cranking.
