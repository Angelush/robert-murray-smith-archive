## Overview

In this father-daughter project video, Robert and his daughter convert a ceiling fan motor into a small water turbine generator. They fabricate turbine blades from coat hangers, install magnets around the motor to generate electricity, and test whether spinning the turbine with water can light up LEDs. The project successfully produces blinking lights during the water spray test, demonstrating basic principles of electromagnetic induction in a hands-on, DIY setting.
