## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates three bright metal plating solutions he developed — zinc, nickel, and copper — which produce shiny, finished-quality coatings with minimal post-processing. The video covers both wand/brush plating and bath plating techniques, highlighting how the solutions work robustly across a wide voltage range (2–20V) and deposit bright, adherent metal in seconds. Robert's main takeaway is that these solutions dramatically simplify electroplating, making it accessible without the usual expertise or finishing work required.
