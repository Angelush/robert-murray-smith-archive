## Overview

Robert tries out a foldable ADO A20 e-bike for his 8-mile commute to work, prompted by the 2021 Great British Petrol Crisis — a panic-buying event where rumours of a shortage became self-fulfilling despite no actual fuel shortage existing. He completes his first commute and finds it slower than driving but genuinely enjoyable, leaving open the possibility it could become his regular mode of transport.
