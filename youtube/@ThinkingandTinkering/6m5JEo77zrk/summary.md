## Overview
In this video, Robert Murray-Smith builds a physical 3D-printed model of a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) he designed in TinkerCAD, following the theoretical introduction in video 2090. The CVT uses a moving fulcrum (lever) principle to vary torque and distance output, combined with a Scotch yoke mechanism to convert rotation to push-pull motion and a dual ratchet mechanism to convert it back to rotation. The video demonstrates the full design-to-build process as a proof-of-concept model.

## Key Topics
- Moving fulcrum principle as the basis of the CVT
- Design process and the importance of making deliberate mechanism choices
- Scotch yoke mechanism for converting rotational input to push-pull motion
- Dual ratchet mechanism for converting push-pull back to rotation
- 3D printing the design using TinkerCAD files shared on Thingiverse
- Work-in equals work-out principle (mechanical advantage via lever)

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- 25 mm × 6 mm stainless steel rods (sold as shelf supports)
- Small bearings: 12 mm OD, 6 mm ID, 4 mm thick (×8, bought in bags of 10)
- Skate bearings: 22 mm OD, 8 mm ID, 7 mm thick (×2)
- Additional bearing: 17 mm OD, 9 mm ID, 4 mm thick (×1, for ratchet assembly)
- 8 mm diameter steel bar (~100 mm long for base, ~30 mm long for Scotch yoke)
- 8 mm washers
- Superglue (for fixing components in place)
- 3D-printed PLA parts (implied, printed from TinkerCAD/Thingiverse files)

## Techniques and Methods
- CAD design using TinkerCAD; files shared freely on Thingiverse
- 3D printing of structural and mechanical components
- Assembly of a sliding pivot/fulcrum mechanism using bearings and capped slides
- Scotch yoke construction: gear with offset hole driving a slotted yoke for push-pull conversion
- Dual opposing Scotch yokes set 180° apart to balance motion
- Dual ratchet mechanism assembly to rectify push-pull into approximate continuous rotation
- Spot-gluing with superglue to fix rotational alignment of yoke components
- Linkage arm added to connect the pivot assembly to the ratchet mechanism
