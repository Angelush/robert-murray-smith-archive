## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reviews two thermal battery technologies — Antora Energy and Brenmiller Energy (B-Gen) — both recognised by Time magazine as among the best inventions of 2023. The video explains how each system stores and retrieves heat energy at industrial scale, and argues that thermal storage is cost-competitive with lithium-ion and pumped hydro solutions, making it a compelling option for both industry and grid storage.
