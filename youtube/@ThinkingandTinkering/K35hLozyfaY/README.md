## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds on his previous video (1007) by experimenting with an improved McKibben pneumatic artificial muscle design. By repositioning a smaller balloon at the top of the braided/mesh sleeve — mimicking how a bicep muscle bunches up with tendons below — he increases contraction from 20% to 30%. His key insight is that concentrating expansion at the top of the muscle, rather than across its full length, is the path to improving McKibben muscle performance.
