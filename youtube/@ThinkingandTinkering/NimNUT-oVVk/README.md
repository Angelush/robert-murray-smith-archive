## Overview

In this short meta-video, Robert Murray-Smith outlines the community guidelines for his YouTube channel, explaining why he moderates comments and what kinds of behaviour will result in deletion or banning. The core message is that the channel is a supportive, exploratory space — not an open forum — and that rudeness, off-topic posts, and attempts to "hijack" the discussion will not be tolerated. Robert emphasises that disagreement is welcome, but manner and tone matter enormously.
