## Overview

This video is the second and final part of Robert's build of an electric scooter using a bamboo frame, salvaging mechanical and electrical components from a defunct kids' scooter. He walks through installing the hub motor, fitting replacement lead-acid motorcycle batteries in a stitched-on battery box, wiring a DC motor controller, and adding controls — then takes the finished scooter for a test ride that impresses everyone in the yard.
