## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build the frame of an electric trike using rolled-up newspaper tubes as the primary structural material. He walks through constructing a wooden jig based on previously published plans, assembling the frame pieces with wood glue, and wrapping the joints with carbon fiber or fiberglass to provide structural strength. The video is part of a larger series on building a functional electric trike entirely from paper-based materials.
