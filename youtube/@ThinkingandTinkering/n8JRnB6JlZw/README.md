## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith reviews the Anycubic Kobra S1 Combo 3D printer, sent to him as part of Anycubic's 10th anniversary celebration. He unboxes and sets up the machine, highlighting its standout feature: a combined automatic filament feeder and dryer (the ACE unit) that he considers a genuinely novel innovation. He concludes it looks very promising out of the box but reserves final judgment for a follow-up review after 3–6 months of heavy use.
