## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith completes and tests his "rowing cycle" — a human-powered vehicle built by combining rowing machine mechanics with a children's bike drivetrain. The proof-of-concept succeeds in that the vehicle moves, though it currently travels slowly due to over-gearing, and Robert discusses plans to tune the gear ratios or add proper gears for more speed.
