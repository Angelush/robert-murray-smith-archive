## Overview
Robert and his collaborator Luke receive a free ride-on lawn mower from a friend named Ben, and in this first part of the series, Robert tears it down to its core components. The goal is to strip away the diesel engine and bodywork, assess the chassis, steering, and drivetrain, and convert the platform into a DIY electric quad bike (themed after "Bessie," the iconic yellow car from *Doctor Who*). The video ends with the mower reduced to its essential mechanical skeleton, ready for cleaning and electric conversion.
