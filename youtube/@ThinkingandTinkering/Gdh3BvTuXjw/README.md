## Overview

In this short video, Robert responds to recurring comments accusing him of "comparing apples and oranges" — specifically regarding his comparisons between batteries and supercapacitors. He argues that the phrase is meaningless without defining the metric of comparison: when a clear metric such as weight, energy, or power is specified, comparing two very different things is entirely valid and meaningful.
