## Overview

This video covers the practical sealing requirements for a DIY gasification unit, focusing on how to choose the right sealant based on joint permanence and operating temperature. Robert walks through several sealing methods suited to different parts of the gasifier — from high-temperature permanent joints near the reaction chamber to removable gaskets on filters and traps. The key takeaway is that matching the sealant material to both the thermal environment and maintenance frequency is essential for a functional gasifier build.
