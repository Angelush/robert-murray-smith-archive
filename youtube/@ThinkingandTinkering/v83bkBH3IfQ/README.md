## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the concept of EZ (Exclusion Zone) water — a structured, ion-free layer of water theorised by researcher Gerald Pollack to form at hydrophilic surfaces. The video presents a preliminary experiment designed by collaborator Steve, using a zinc plate and resistance measurements to look for evidence of the EZ zone. Initial results are intriguing but inconclusive, and Robert commits to a more rigorous follow-up experiment.
