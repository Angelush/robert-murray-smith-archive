## Overview

Robert presents a hastily assembled water wheel generator as a competition entry, acknowledging it is incomplete and rough. The concept involves attaching a washing machine motor assembly to a fabricated angle-iron water wheel and placing it in a stream to generate electricity. He demonstrates it can power an LED and suggests it could realistically provide emergency LED lighting.
