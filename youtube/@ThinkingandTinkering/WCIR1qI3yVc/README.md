## Overview

Robert demonstrates a modified batch of hemp plastic — this time made without graphene or plasticizer — which, while it cannot be injection moulded, can be hot-press moulded into dense, hard sheets and rods. He shows examples of a turned rod (machined on a lathe) and a 3mm plate, both remarkably tough, and flags that the next step is finding a plasticizer blend that enables injection moulding while retaining this hardness.
