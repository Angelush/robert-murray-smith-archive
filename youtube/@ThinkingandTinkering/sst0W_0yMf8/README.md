## Overview

Robert explores the use of spiral geometry — particularly the Fibonacci spiral — in wind turbine design, tracing the concept from academic research papers through to the commercially available Liam F1 turbine. He reviews two key studies showing significant efficiency gains from spiral-blade configurations and demonstrates his own 3D-printed replication of the conical spiral turbine design.
