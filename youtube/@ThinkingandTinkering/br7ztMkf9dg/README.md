## Overview

This short update video documents Robert Murray-Smith's experiment with diamagnetic graphite sourced in Sri Lanka, specifically addressing a practical problem: the material arrives as awkward chips. He demonstrates that grinding the chips in a food blender with water, drying, and pressing the result into a flat disc preserves the diamagnetic property, with the compressed chip floating visibly above magnets.
