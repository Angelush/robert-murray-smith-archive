Robert Murray-Smith shares a video made by his brother David (Dave Smith), who lives in County Durham and was loaned an Eskute Polluno e-bike for a couple of months as a real-world trial. David reports on his experience using the bike as a car replacement for daily errands and longer leisure rides, covering approximately 450 miles over the period. The overall verdict is strongly positive, with David saying he intends to buy one when he has to return it.
