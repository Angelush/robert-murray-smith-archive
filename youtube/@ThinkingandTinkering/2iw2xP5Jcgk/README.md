## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a compact DIY emergency rainwater generator built around an Archimedes screw motor mounted on a pipe. He explains his preference for an open pipe design over a closed one, as it can be fed from a bath, toilet, or rainwater without risk of clogging. The video is a short teaser; a full build video was posted on his main channel.
