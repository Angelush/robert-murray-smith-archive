## Overview

Robert introduces a conceptual solution to a fundamental problem with Savonius-type wind turbines built from oil drums: they perform well in low winds when open but risk overspeeding in high winds. He proposes a centrifugal, pulley-based sliding mechanism that automatically closes the turbine blades as rotation speed increases, and opens them again as it drops — self-regulating without electronics.
