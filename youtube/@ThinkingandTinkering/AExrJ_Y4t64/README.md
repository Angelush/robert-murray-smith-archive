## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to significantly increase the strength of FDM 3D printed parts using two key strategies: orienting the print grain in the direction of applied force, and infiltrating PLA prints with cyanoacrylate (super glue) and an activator to create a composite material. The video concludes that even cheap PLA prints can be made surprisingly difficult to break with these simple techniques.
