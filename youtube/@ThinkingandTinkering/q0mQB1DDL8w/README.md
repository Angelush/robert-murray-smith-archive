## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the concept of a self-charging solar battery using Prussian Blue, a photochromic/electrochromic material. He demonstrates how Prussian Blue on filter paper can store energy when bleached white by sunlight, then release it as electrical current while reverting to blue. The video combines a brief survey of solar cell efficiency history with a hands-on construction of a simple Prussian Blue cell that charges a supercapacitor.
