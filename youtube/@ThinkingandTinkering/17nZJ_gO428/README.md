## Overview

This is a Christmas advent calendar short (Day 18) in which Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a quick, playful craft: making toy "rolling feet" from straw and sticks attached to a roll of tape. He then briefly explains the biomechanics that distinguish walking, running, and jogging in terms of foot-ground contact time.
