## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores alternative methods of generating electricity and mechanical work from chemical heat differences, filmed partly at the Science Museum in London. He demonstrates that both exothermic reactions (zeolite + water) and endothermic reactions (urea + water) can drive heat engines and thermoelectric devices. The video concludes with an optimistic argument that forgotten technologies, new materials, and human ingenuity mean the energy crisis is solvable.
