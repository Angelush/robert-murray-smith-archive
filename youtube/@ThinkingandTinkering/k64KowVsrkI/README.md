## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith examines Toyota's 2023 announcement of an ammonia-powered internal combustion engine car, exploring whether it could realistically challenge electric vehicles. He focuses on a new University of Illinois Chicago paper describing a lithium-battery-based electrochemical process for producing ammonia that is green and roughly six times cheaper than competing green ammonia methods, concluding that while challenges remain, this advances both the ammonia car concept and the broader hydrogen economy.
