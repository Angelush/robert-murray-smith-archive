## Overview

This is a short update video in which Robert demonstrates an improved version of his leaf-based solar cell, made using a solution with approximately twice the chlorophyll content of his previous attempt. The improved cell shows a significantly higher voltage reading (76.2 vs ~40) and a much faster charge/discharge response, leading Robert to conclude that chlorophyll is the active component responsible for the photovoltaic effect.
