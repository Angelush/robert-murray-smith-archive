## Overview

This video is a follow-up to Robert's saltwater generation experiment, focusing on the anisotropic properties of his graphene-based waterproof ink. He demonstrates that placing the ink-coated material across a temperature differential (hot water) generates a measurable voltage, consistent with thermoelectric behaviour seen in Peltier devices. The experiment is framed as early proof-of-concept, with future work planned to characterise actual power output in amps.
