## Overview

Luke rebuilds a DIY push scooter from scratch, motivated by dissatisfaction with his previous version and improved skill level. He strips down a donor bicycle, fabricates a custom steel frame using welding and cutting techniques, and assembles a finished scooter with suspension forks, a repurposed footplate, and a traditional rear brake. The final result is a functional, blue-and-red painted scooter he rides in a car park to test the braking.
