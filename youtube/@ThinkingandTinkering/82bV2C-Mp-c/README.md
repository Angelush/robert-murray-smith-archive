## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to convert an automotive glow plug into a cheap, simple pyrography (wood-burning) tool. The build requires minimal parts and basic tools, and the finished tool can be powered by a small battery or low-voltage power supply. He concludes that for just a couple of pounds, anyone can make a functional pyrography tool to burn patterns, names, or artwork onto wood.
