## Overview

Robert stumbles upon 20 brand-new emergency lights being discarded by a local electronics store, salvaging nearly £400 worth of NiMH batteries and other useful components for free. He then demonstrates extracting electromagnetic ballasts from fluorescent light fittings and using them as small inductive generators to charge the salvaged batteries as part of his ongoing wind turbine project. The video concludes with Robert making the case for a modular, redundant energy storage system inspired by RAID arrays in computing.
