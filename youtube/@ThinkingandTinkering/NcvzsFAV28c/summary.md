## Overview
This video features Carl, an 11-year-old inventor, demonstrating a water motor he built from scratch for a competition. The motor uses a jet of water to spin a platter, using a repurposed electric motor housing as a bearing — with no electricity involved. Robert appears to be sharing this young maker's work as an example of hands-on ingenuity and the spirit of independent invention.
