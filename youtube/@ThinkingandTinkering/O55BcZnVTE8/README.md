## Overview

Robert unboxes and does an initial assembly review of the AlgoLaser Alpha MK2, a 20W diode laser sent to him for review. He compares it favourably against more expensive CO2 lasers (like the xTool X2 P2) as a more affordable entry point into laser cutting at around £700. After assembling the machine and connecting it via Wi-Fi, he runs a quick test cut on 3mm basswood using the AlgoLaser phone app, concluding it offers good value out of the box with a few minor assembly quirks.
