## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith takes the alternator-style generator from a cheap ceiling fan (explored in video 1717) and pairs it with a DIY Savonius vertical-axis wind turbine rotor built from leftover materials. The experiment demonstrates that the ceiling fan's generator section is actually quite efficient — the problem in the previous video was the poor wind-capture blades, not the generator. His main takeaway is that this low-cost alternator design could be a viable path to cheap, accessible wind power generation.
