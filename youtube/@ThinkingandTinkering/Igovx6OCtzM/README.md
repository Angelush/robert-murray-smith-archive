## Overview

A concise explainer on the five functions of gears — transferring motion, reversing direction, changing angle, and trading off speed against torque. Robert debunks the common misconception that adding gears increases power output, clarifying that gears always introduce losses and their real purpose is matching the speed and torque requirements of different parts of a machine.
