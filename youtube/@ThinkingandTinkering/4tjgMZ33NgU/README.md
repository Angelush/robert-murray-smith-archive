## Overview

This video continues Robert Murray-Smith's ongoing "Darwin wind turbine" project, reviewing two community-submitted wind pipe designs. He 3D-printed and tested both designs — one by Tony Jackson and one by Emile Davis — evaluating their hyperbolic funnel geometry and practical buildability, concluding that both work well but each has distinct trade-offs.
