## Overview

Robert demonstrates the microwave exfoliation of sulfate-intercalated graphite, showing how a quarter teaspoon of the material expands dramatically in volume after 20 seconds in a microwave. The video explains the modified Hummers method used to intercalate rather than oxidize graphite, and shows that the resulting exfoliated material can be pressed into a flexible, electrically conductive graphite foil.
