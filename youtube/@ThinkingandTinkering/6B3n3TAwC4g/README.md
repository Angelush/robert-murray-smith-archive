## Overview

Robert demonstrates a conductive ink heating pad he previously made, showing it can generate enough heat to fry an egg. Running at 24 volts and drawing 1.7 amps (~42 watts), the thin flexible pad reaches around 68°C — sufficient to cook a fried egg, proving the practical heating capability of the ink in a fun, informal experiment.
