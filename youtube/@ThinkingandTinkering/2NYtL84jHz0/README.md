## Overview

This video documents the physical disassembly of a Renault Twizy electric vehicle as part of Robert's "FWG powered by paper" project, which aims to drive the Twizy from Land's End to John O'Groats using a self-developed paper-based battery. Robert walks through the full stripdown procedure step by step, culminating in the removal of the Twizy's original lithium-ion battery pack from beneath the vehicle.
