## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to convert his exercise bike generator (built in video 1519) into a water turbine by attaching the "tablespoon turbine" he fabricated in video 1476. The modification involves removing some components, cutting, and welding the turbine head onto the generator. The result works effectively as a water turbine, and Robert notes it could also function as a wind turbine with the appropriate rotor attached.
