## Overview

This short theory video explains the scientific basis for Robert's "SWAG generator" (Salt Water And Graphene generator), drawing on two Chinese research papers. Robert outlines two competing mechanisms by which salt water moving over a graphene surface might generate electrical current, and discusses how ion charge and size affect performance. The video pairs with his practical experiments and serves as a conceptual grounding for why the system works.
