## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith refurbishes a commercial deep fat fryer acquired cheaply by his neighbours who are setting up an industrial kitchen. He replaces the original worn-out mechanical temperature controls with a modern PID controller, K-type thermocouple, and solid-state relay, while also adding improved safety features including a thermal cutout switch and a relay latching circuit for the start/stop buttons.
