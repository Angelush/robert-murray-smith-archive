## Overview

A short build-and-demo video in which the creator constructs a small water-powered generator using a custom rotor, neodymium magnets, and coils. The key design innovation is a revised turbine housing that keeps the rotor spinning in the same direction regardless of which way the unit is oriented, allowing a fixed reservoir of water (stored in repurposed Coca-Cola bottles) to be reused simply by flipping the entire system upside down.
