## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith uses this video as an interlude in his ongoing solar system installation project to address a recurring question from his audience: why he doesn't provide exhaustive component specifications. He argues that understanding the *function and structure* of a system is far more valuable than accumulating raw information, and that knowledge — defined as information with structure — is what enables people to adapt any system to their specific circumstances.
