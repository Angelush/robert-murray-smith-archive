## Overview

This video explores three distinct methods for harvesting electrical energy directly from plants: microbial fuel cells driven by root microorganisms, direct electrochemical access to photosynthesis within leaf cells, and transpiration-based electricity generation. Robert highlights recent research (including a 2022 paper and a paper published just before filming) demonstrating that plant-based power is a credible and advancing field, capable of practical feats like charging a mobile phone.
