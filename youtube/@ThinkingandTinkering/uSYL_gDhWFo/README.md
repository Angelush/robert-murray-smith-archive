## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith critically examines electric composters (such as the Lomi, Vitamix, and Pela), arguing that they are fundamentally food dehydrators rather than true composters. He concludes that they are expensive, not genuinely eco-friendly across their lifecycle, and solve a problem that is better addressed simply by buying and wasting less food.
