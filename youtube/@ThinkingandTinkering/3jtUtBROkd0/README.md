## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores gravity as a cheap, clean, and simple energy storage medium, demonstrating several prototype mechanisms that convert falling weights into rotational electrical generation. He walks through his 3D-printed designs — including a worm gear derived from an Archimedes screw and a spiral ball-bearing drop mechanism — before spotlighting an impressive emergency gravity generator built by students at the Technological University of the Philippines, which he considers a near-perfect realisation of the concept.
