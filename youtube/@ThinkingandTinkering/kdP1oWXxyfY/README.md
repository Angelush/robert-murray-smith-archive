## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith traces the history of "water fuel car" claims from Jules Verne's 1874 prediction through a string of fraudulent inventors up to the present day, clarifying what a water car actually is versus hydrogen/HHO systems. He concludes that the real reason we don't have a true water-fuel car isn't government conspiracy but rather that nobody has seriously pursued the correct approach: using water itself as a fuel via cavitation and high-voltage arc phenomena, rather than converting it to hydrogen first.
