## Overview

Robert repurposes a plaster-of-Paris hand cast (made in earlier videos using sodium alginate dental mold material) by mounting it on a breadboard wood base to create a sculptural phone/camera tripod holder for his iPhone. The video focuses primarily on achieving an attractive aged-copper finish through layered painting and waxing techniques, turning a leftover project piece into something both functional and decorative.
