## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a simple but effective laboratory balance scale, inspired by an idea from his Australian friend Pete. The balance uses water as a known reference weight (1 g/mL) to measure unknown quantities, and is constructed primarily from PVC foam board ("builder's board"). The finished balance can measure up to approximately 50 g with an accuracy of around ±0.1 g.
