## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith converts a salvaged river bike's rear wheel into a hubless, spokeless wheel driven by a custom cog system made from a gear-making material introduced in video 1084. The cog is glued inside the wheel rim so that caster-wheel drive units engage the rim directly, eliminating the need for a centre axle. By the end of the video the prototype supports his weight and rolls freely, with only the chain drive still to be added.
