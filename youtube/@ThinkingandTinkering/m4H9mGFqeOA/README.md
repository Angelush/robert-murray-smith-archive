## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith converts a PC fan motor into a water-powered generator by replacing the fan blades with a 3D-printed Pelton wheel sourced from Thingiverse. After cleaning the print with IPA and removing the original blades, he tests the assembly with a water jet and achieves approximately 160 milliamps and 1–1.5 watts of output. He presents this as a simple, ultra-low-cost proof-of-concept for small-scale hydro generation.
