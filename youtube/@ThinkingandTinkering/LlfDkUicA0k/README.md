## Overview

This video is part 6 of Robert Murray-Smith's series on building a Van de Graaff generator, focusing specifically on constructing the charge-collecting combs (brushes). Robert demonstrates multiple methods for making brushes, then shows his preferred approach using brass strip and pins soldered together, and explains the correct positioning of the combs relative to the belt and rollers.
