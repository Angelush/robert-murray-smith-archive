## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives his final review of the ADO A20 folding electric bike after using it for over a month in real-world daily commuting conditions. He evaluates it from the perspective of an older, heavier rider with hip arthritis rather than as a performance cyclist, concluding it's an excellent value for money as a practical transport aid.
