## Overview

Robert demonstrates vacuum distillation of artificial sea water, boiling it at just 50°C using a vacuum pump to lower the pressure. He then proposes a pump-free, solar-powered version using the barometric head principle — a 34-foot pipe in a bucket — to create a natural vacuum, enabling low-energy desalination with nothing more than a tall pipe, a bucket, and sunlight.
