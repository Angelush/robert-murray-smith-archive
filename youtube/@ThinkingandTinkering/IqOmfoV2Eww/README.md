## Overview

This short tutorial demonstrates how to use dowel joinery as an alternative to wood screws when joining pieces of raw wood, preserving the natural appearance of the material. The presenter (Luke) walks through the full process of cutting, gluing, and installing dowels, then sanding them flush. The result is a strong, visually clean joint that is nearly invisible once sanded.
