## Overview

Robert salvages two discarded vacuum cleaners found on the roadside and extracts their series-wound universal motors. He demonstrates that these motors function on DC power, then builds a simple proof-of-concept hovercraft by mounting one motor in a large mixing bowl to act as a plenum chamber. The experiment succeeds, showing that a vacuum cleaner motor alone is sufficient to lift and float the platform.
