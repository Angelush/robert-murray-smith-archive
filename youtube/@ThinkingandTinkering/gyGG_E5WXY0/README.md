## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to combine weatherproof glass bearings (introduced in video 2001) with a 3D-printed hypoid gear to create a rust-proof gear train suited for outdoor use. The assembly is designed with wind turbines and water wheels in mind, offering both a built-in gear ratio and weather resistance without needing precise alignment.
