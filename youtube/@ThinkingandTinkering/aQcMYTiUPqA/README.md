## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents an updated, improved version of the Hummers-based method (Thomas method) for synthesising graphene oxide, replacing his earlier lower-quality videos. The process is a three-stage temperature-controlled reaction — cold, medium, and high — that converts graphite powder into a filterable graphene oxide cake in approximately two hours. He emphasises that while the procedure is accessible to motivated home chemists, it involves genuinely dangerous chemicals and must be followed carefully to avoid explosive or runaway reactions.
