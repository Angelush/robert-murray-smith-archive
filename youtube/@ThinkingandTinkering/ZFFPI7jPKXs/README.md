## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces his plan to build a large Van de Graaff generator using a stainless steel garden ornament ball as the dome. He explains the basic operating principles of a Van de Graaff generator using his existing small model as a reference, noting that voltage output scales with dome size. The video serves as a preview/introduction to a build series targeting 300,000–500,000 volts.
