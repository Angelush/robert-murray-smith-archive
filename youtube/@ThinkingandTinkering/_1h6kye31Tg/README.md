## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives a tour of his lab (FWG) to address fans wondering why videos had slowed down — the team has simply been too busy with expansion and projects. The video introduces the growing team, the physical lab buildout (including a newly acquired adjacent unit), and provides status updates on three active research streams: graphene-reinforced concrete, coal-to-graphite conversion, and biopolymer thin films, alongside the ongoing battery plate work. The key takeaway is that the lab has clarified its business model: they create intellectual property and develop ideas to manufacturing-readiness, rather than manufacturing products themselves.
