## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the application of biomimicry — specifically whale tubercles (bumps on humpback whale flippers) — to vertical axis wind turbine (VAWT) blade design. He 3D prints and assembles two identical small VAWTs, one with smooth blades and one with tubercle-patterned blades, then tests both under a fan to compare RPM. The tubercle version spins noticeably faster (149 RPM vs ~100 RPM), supporting the hypothesis that tubercles meaningfully improve VAWT efficiency.
