## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith shares a quick, elegant tip for drawing large circles without a traditional compass. The method uses a nail (or screw) as a pivot point and a lockable tape measure as the radius arm, with a pen inserted at the desired distance. He considers it one of the simplest and most practical workshop tricks he's encountered.
