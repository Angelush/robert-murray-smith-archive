## Overview
This is Robert Murray-Smith's 1,000th YouTube video, marking a milestone after roughly 8–9 years of content creation. To celebrate, Robert announces a giveaway of 250 ml of high-purity graphene oxide (valued at approximately £1,000 from Sigma-Aldrich), to be awarded via a random lottery drawn two weeks after publication. The video is primarily a thank-you to his community of viewers, members, and commenters.

Robert's comment replies are largely logistical, directing lottery entrants to email him at robertmurraysmith64@gmail.com. A few substantive points emerge:

- **Uses of graphene oxide:** In one reply he explains his personal uses — supercapacitors, batteries, graphene-based inks for circuits, and as a reinforcement additive for plastics ("stronger than steel").

- **Video count clarification:** He notes that members-only videos aren't numbered, and he has deleted a few over the years, but reached 1,000 numbered videos.
