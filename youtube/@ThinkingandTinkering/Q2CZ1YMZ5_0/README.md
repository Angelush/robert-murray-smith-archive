## Overview

Robert introduces a multi-part series project to build a DIY multi-fuel home generator (gen set) by combining his existing rocket stove work with a steam boiler and a converted steam engine. The core concept is inspired by WWII-era Stuart Sirius steam engines used to power radio equipment, adapted for modern energy resilience. The project aims to produce electricity from a wide variety of fuels — including wood, vegetable oil, biodiesel, and even twigs — making it a practical emergency power solution.
