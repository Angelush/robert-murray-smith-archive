## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds a physical chain-driven model of an AirLoom-style wind turbine, drawing parallels between his own previous ring-generator designs and the commercial AirLoom (Airloom) concept. The video demonstrates that the AirLoom's approach — airfoils on a flexible loop generating power at the rim rather than via a central shaft — is conceptually identical to work he has been doing for years. The prototype successfully spins under fan-driven airflow, validating the concept at model scale.
