## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith compares his custom Pelton wheel wind turbine design against a standard Archimedes spiral turbine, demonstrating that the Pelton wheel spins effectively in very light winds. He tests it by powering a 6-LED emergency light at roughly 3 m/s wind speed, successfully lighting it both indoors and outdoors, showcasing wind power as a viable nighttime alternative to solar.
