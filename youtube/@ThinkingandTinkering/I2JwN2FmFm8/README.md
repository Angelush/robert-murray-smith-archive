## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith describes his proprietary solution-based method for producing graphene from graphite and proposes that this same method could be integrated into commercial graphite mining operations to improve both yield and product purity. He walks through conventional graphite mining and processing techniques before explaining how his cheap, safe, and reusable solvent system could serve as a post-mining refining step or even produce graphene as a value-added product at the mine head. He closes by actively seeking investors or commercial partners for this application.
