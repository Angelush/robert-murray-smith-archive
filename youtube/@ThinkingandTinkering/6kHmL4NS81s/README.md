## Overview

Robert demonstrates his DIY large rotary tumble cleaner in action, showing how it cleans rusty and dirty metal parts salvaged from a lawn mower being converted into an electric vehicle. The cleaner uses a food-grade 30-litre drum filled with gravel, metal parts, and a homemade acidic cleaning solution, tumbling for about an hour to produce clean, bright metal.
