## Overview

This video is presented by Luke, a younger collaborator (likely Robert Murray-Smith's son or assistant), who builds a simple wind turbine based on Robert's earlier "easiest wind generator" video. Using a donated car radiator fan with a brushed DC motor as the generator and 3D-printed blades made by Robert, Luke constructs and tests the turbine outdoors, achieving performance comparable to a commercial wind turbine of the same blade length at the same wind speed.
