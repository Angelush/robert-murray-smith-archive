## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a hand-cranked emergency phone charger built around a custom generator, filmed at the Science Museum. Using the premise that renewable sources like wind and solar are unreliable, he argues that human power is always available — and shows that a crank mechanism is one of the most efficient ways to transfer that power into electrical energy. The device successfully charges a phone via USB after about 20 minutes of cranking.
