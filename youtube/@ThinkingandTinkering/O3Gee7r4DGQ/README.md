## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a method for copper plating aluminium — a process normally considered impossible due to aluminium's high reactivity. The key is first applying a sodium molybdate-based passivation (conversion) coating that turns the aluminium surface golden, after which standard electroplating can proceed. He is motivated by supercapacitor applications, where a corrosion-resistant, conductive aluminium current collector is needed.
