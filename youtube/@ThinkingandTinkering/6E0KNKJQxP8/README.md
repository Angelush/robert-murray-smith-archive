## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith provides a follow-up to a previous video demonstrating the reduction of graphene oxide to graphene using hydrochloric acid and aluminum foil. In this update, he scales up to a more concentrated version (10 mg/mL in 35% HCl) to produce higher-concentration graphene ink. He primarily uses the video to warn viewers about the vigorous, exothermic nature of the reaction that he neglected to mention previously.
