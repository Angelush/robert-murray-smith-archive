## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates cleaning a dirty steering connecting rod by hand using his "miracle cleaning solution" and stainless steel scouring pads. The video serves as a practical comparison to the machine-based cleaning methods (shot blasting, tumblers, shakers) he had been exploring for an electric lawnmower conversion project. His main takeaway is that for small jobs, hand-cleaning with this solution works surprisingly well and requires no special equipment.
