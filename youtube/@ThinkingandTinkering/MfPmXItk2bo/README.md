## Overview

This is a planning video in which Luke and Robert Murray-Smith discuss how they will convert a scrap ride-on lawnmower into an electric vehicle inspired by "Bessie," the yellow car associated with Doctor Who's Third Doctor. The two collaborators divide responsibilities — Robert handling design and visualisation while Luke brings mechanical expertise — and sketch out a practical plan for adapting the existing wheels, adding a larger tyre, and fitting a dual-disc brake system per wheel.
