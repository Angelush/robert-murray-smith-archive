## Overview

In this video, Robert Murray-Smith explains why he works on many projects simultaneously rather than focusing on one at a time. He uses the waiting time forced by long 3D print jobs as a jumping-off point to argue that being a "jack of all trades" develops a generalist problem-solving mindset that is essential when facing novel, unseen problems — something a deep specialist ("master") cannot do. His core takeaway is that both mindsets have value, but for invention and exploration, breadth of experience is at least as important as depth.
