## Overview

Robert demonstrates "hot ice" — a supersaturated sodium acetate solution that crystallises instantly when seeded with a tiny crystal, releasing heat in the process. The video shows how to prepare and trigger this reaction as a Christmas science trick, highlighting that the same material is used in reusable hand warmers.
