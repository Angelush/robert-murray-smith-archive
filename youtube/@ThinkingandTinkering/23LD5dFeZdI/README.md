## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith inspects, jet-washes, and strips down a lawn mower chassis in preparation for converting it into a miniature electric vehicle styled after "Bessie," the Third Doctor's iconic yellow Edwardian tourer from Doctor Who. The video focuses on cleaning and disassembly, revealing the surprisingly simple mechanical components — steering linkage, gearbox, drivetrain, and rear axle — that form the foundation for the build. His main takeaway is that a large part of any restoration or upcycling project is simply pulling things apart and cleaning them properly.
