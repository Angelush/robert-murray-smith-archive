## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith repurposes brass gears and steel components salvaged from a donated car gearbox to build a dieselpunk-styled freestanding clock, incorporating a Doctor Who pocket watch as the clock face. The project combines automotive salvage, fiberglass resin casting, and welding to create a personalized Christmas gift for his sister. The result is a functional, removable-pocket-watch clock with a striking steampunk/dieselpunk aesthetic.
