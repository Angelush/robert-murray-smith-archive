## Overview
Robert presents the completed rolling chassis of "Bessie," a lawn mower he is converting into an electric vehicle inspired by the Doctor Who character's iconic Edwardian-style car. The video is a brief tour of the modifications made to reach this milestone, with a nod toward the next steps of fitting body panels and the motor. The chassis now sits on repurposed car tires and features several structural additions that give it a rugged, mini-jeep aesthetic.

## Key Topics
- Reveal of the completed rolling chassis as a project milestone
- Overview of all modifications made since the previous episode
- Wheel hub and tire upgrades
- Steering column relocation
- Outrigger and running board fabrication
- Transmission options (belt vs. chain drive)
- Design intent: Edwardian tourer / "Bessie" Doctor Who theme
