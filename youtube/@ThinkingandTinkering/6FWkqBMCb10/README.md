## Overview

Robert tests the Elegoo Orangestorm Giga large-format 3D printer by printing a large wind turbine blade — a part he plans to use in a massive wind turbine build. The print took 14 hours (vs the slicer's estimate of ~12 hours), used 800g of filament, and produced an acceptable surface finish with minor jitter and ghosting only at the very top of the tall print. His conclusion is that the Orangestorm is a purpose-specific machine best suited to large-part fabrication, and it performed well straight out of the box.
