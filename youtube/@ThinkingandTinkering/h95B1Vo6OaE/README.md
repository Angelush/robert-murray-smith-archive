## Overview

In this second part of his DIY gasification series, Robert Murray-Smith explains the design and construction of the "fire pipe" — the gas-generation core of a downdraft stratified gasifier. He demonstrates that the system is remarkably simple: the reactor is just a steel pipe sized to the engine, dropped into a stainless steel beer keg that acts as a collection jacket, with almost no critical tolerances to worry about.
