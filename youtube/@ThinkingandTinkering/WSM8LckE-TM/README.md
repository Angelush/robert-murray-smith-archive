## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to make urea-formaldehyde resin — a hard white plastic used in kitchen countertops, cupboards, and MDF — from just three readily available ingredients. The video responds to criticism of a previous video by showing a simpler, reproducible process. He concludes by hinting at future experiments mixing the resulting plastic with graphene to test strength improvements.
