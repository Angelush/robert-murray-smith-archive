## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith makes a chess set as a Christmas gift for his older brother — a former chess master — using acrylic salvaged from broken LCD screens. He laser-cuts the pieces from acrylic of varying thicknesses, constructs the board from the reflective backing material found inside LCDs, and sandwiches it between recovered tempered glass. The video emphasises that no laser cutter is required, as he offers the free cut-file plans to anyone who emails him.
