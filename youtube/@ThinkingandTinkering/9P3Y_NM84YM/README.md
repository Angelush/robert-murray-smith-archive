## Overview

A short practical tip video showing how to remove and reinsert incorrectly wired pins in plug/wire connectors without cutting and re-soldering. Robert demonstrates that the retaining spring on each pin can be compressed to release it from the plastic housing, allowing wires to be moved to the correct positions. The video was prompted by a miswired connector on his electric trike build.
