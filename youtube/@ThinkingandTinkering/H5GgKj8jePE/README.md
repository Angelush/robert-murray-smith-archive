## Overview

Robert investigates the composition of commercial aluminium "welding" rods (specifically Durafix brand), which are more accurately brazing rods. By feel alone he suspects the rods are primarily zinc, and he confirms this by rolling zinc foil into a rod and successfully brazing aluminium pieces together — achieving joints stronger than the base metal. His main takeaway is that cheap zinc wire can substitute for expensive branded rods, at least for cosmetic or non-structural fabrication work.
