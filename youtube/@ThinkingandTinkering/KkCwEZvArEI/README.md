## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the concept of contra rotation in wind turbines, where two sets of blades on the same axle spin in opposite directions to potentially extract more energy from the wind. He designs and 3D-prints a working model using bevel gears and a chain drive to demonstrate and test the principle, concluding that the model serves as a valid test framework for others to experiment with blade spacing, bearing configurations, and output comparisons.
