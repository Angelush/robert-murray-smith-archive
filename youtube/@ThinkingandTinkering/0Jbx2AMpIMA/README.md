## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith shares practical advice on sourcing materials for inventing and making, using a scavenged treadmill as the central example. He demonstrates that old treadmill DC motors are powerful generators, producing around 3.5V and 0.5A when spun by hand. The video emphasises building relationships with local businesses and scrap collectors to obtain useful components for free or cheaply.
