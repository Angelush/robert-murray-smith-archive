## Overview

Robert explores the business potential of FDM 3D printing using the Elegoo Neptune printer, inspired by a seller who made over a million pounds printing and selling chess sets. He walks through the complete workflow — from finding a Doctor Who chess set on Thingiverse to slicing in Cura and printing — to demonstrate that a 3D printer can function as a "business in a box" for almost anyone.
