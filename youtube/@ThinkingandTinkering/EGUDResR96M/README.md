## Overview

This video demonstrates converting a TENG (Triboelectric Nanogenerator) — previously featured in episode 1485 — into a wind-powered energy harvester. The presenter builds a simple enclosure using a food storage container and rubber band suspension, tests it outdoors, and achieves a peak of ~1.8 volts in real wind conditions. The experiment shows the concept is viable, with room for further optimisation.
