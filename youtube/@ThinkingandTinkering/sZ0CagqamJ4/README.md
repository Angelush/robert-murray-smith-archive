## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the three methods of heat transfer (convection, conduction, and radiation) and demonstrates how to build a simple nano carbon infrared heater from carbon fiber weave. He debunks the "nano carbon" marketing claim, clarifying that "nano" refers to the infrared wavelength rather than the carbon structure itself, while still affirming that infrared heaters are genuinely useful for heating people rather than spaces.
