## Overview

Robert builds a portable, foldable, near-silent DIY air conditioner by combining a USB PC fan motor fitted with a 3D-printed toroidal propeller with an insulated cool bag filled with ice. The key innovation over similar "bucket AC" builds is using a collapsible cool bag (instead of a rigid cool box) and a toroidal propeller to dramatically reduce noise. Testing shows the unit outputs air at 9°C against a 21°C ambient temperature.
