## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores airborne wind power — specifically kite-based energy generation — as a compelling alternative to conventional wind turbines and solar. He covers the ancient history of kites, their modern engineering principles, and the growing industry of companies developing megawatt-scale kite generators. His key takeaway is that an 11,000-year-old technology may prove critical to 21st-century renewable energy, particularly for remote or offshore locations where traditional turbines are impractical.
