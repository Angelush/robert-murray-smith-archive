## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores unconventional wind energy harvesting beyond traditional turbines, focusing on four wind-effect mechanisms: vortex shedding, galloping, wake-induced, and flutter. He demonstrates a 3D-printed flutter device with an integral hinge — costing about 15p to make — that can harvest energy from intermittent wind bursts, vibrations, and foot traffic, arguing that simplicity and low cost matter far more than peak efficiency for this class of device.
