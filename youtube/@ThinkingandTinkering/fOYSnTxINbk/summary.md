## Overview
This is a short announcement video in which Robert Murray-Smith reveals custom-designed coffee mugs featuring artwork submitted by a community member named Cat. The mugs are available through YouTube's merchandising platform (powered by Teespring) and carry the slogan "Be a maker, not a taker." The video serves as both a thank-you to Cat and a heads-up to fans about the new merchandise.

## Key Topics
- Reveal of custom merchandise mugs featuring fan-submitted artwork
- Credit and thanks to community member "Cat" for the designs
- The "Be a maker, not a taker" slogan on the mug logo
- Availability of the mugs via YouTube's Teespring-integrated merch shelf
- Brief note that Robert evaluated alternatives to Teespring but found them comparable
