## Overview

"Graphene 101" is a foundational explainer video in which Robert walks through the structure of graphene, why it has its unusual properties, and the various strategies for producing it — with a central focus on making graphene accessible to home experimenters using cheap, obtainable materials. His main argument is that contrary to forum wisdom, you do not need an expensive professional lab to make graphene; persistence and creative use of basic chemistry can achieve it.
