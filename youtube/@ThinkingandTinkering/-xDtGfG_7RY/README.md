## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith documents a failed electrolysis experiment where a glass vessel used to split water into hydrogen and oxygen gas exploded after a single press of the button. The video serves as an honest account of experimental failure, noting that while the result was a mess, the force of the explosion itself demonstrated significant stored energy. He plans to rebuild the setup using thicker glass or polycarbonate before continuing.
