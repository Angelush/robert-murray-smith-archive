## Overview

This video is a viewer competition entry featured on Robert Murray-Smith's channel, demonstrating how to build a simple voltaic pile battery using US pennies and salt-and-vinegar potato chips as the electrolyte separator. The builder stacks alternating copper and zinc penny discs with potato chip spacers to produce over 2 volts, sufficient to light white, green, and red LEDs.
