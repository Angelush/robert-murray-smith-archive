## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to make homemade soldering flux after failing to find any in his lab. Using just three common ingredients mixed with deionized water and heated to consistency, he produces a flux suitable for use on a range of metals. The video serves as a quick practical guide ahead of a follow-up video where he attempts to solder copper wire to stainless steel.
