## Overview
Robert unboxes the Eskute Wayfarer, an electric city bike sent to him by Eskute following his positive review of their earlier Voyager model. He walks through the packaging, minimal assembly process, key components, and specs, concluding that it's an excellent value e-bike — and personally ideal for him due to its step-through frame, which accommodates his arthritis.

## Key Topics
- Unboxing and initial inspection of the Eskute Wayfarer e-bike
- Comparison to the earlier Eskute Voyager model
- Assembly process and included tools
- Key specifications (motor, battery, gears, range)
- Step-through frame as an accessibility feature
- Throttle installation procedure
- Removable battery and charging options
- Rear cargo carrier

## Techniques and Methods
- Unboxing and packaging assessment
- Basic bike assembly (attaching front wheel, handlebars, seat adjustment, kickstand)
- Throttle control installation (removing original handle, sliding on throttle unit, connecting cable, tightening with Allen key)
- Battery removal and separate charging
