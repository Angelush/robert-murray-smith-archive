## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how he "cloned" himself multiple times in a single video frame using the free video editor OpenShot, appearing as three simultaneous versions of himself. He explains that the key to convincing visual trickery lies not in complex editing but in careful scene setup — keeping the camera fixed and using simple crop/layer techniques. The video serves as a playful lesson in how easily video can deceive, echoing the craft behind professional film illusions.
