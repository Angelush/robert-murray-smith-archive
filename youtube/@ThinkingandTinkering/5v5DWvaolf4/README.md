## Overview

This video (part two of a two-part series, following video 1490) demonstrates Robert's DIY low-head water vortex turbine, built from a hyperbolic-curve bowl, an aluminium cover, and a PC fan motor. He tests both an impeller blade and a boat propeller to see which generates more electricity from the vortex flow, concluding that the impeller outperforms the boat propeller in this configuration.
