## Overview

Robert explores whether fluorescent 3D printing filament can act as a light guide to improve solar cell output, building on his earlier work with fluorescent acrylic panels (video 2198). He prints a right-angle prism using a fluorescent yellow filament and demonstrates over a 10% improvement in solar cell voltage output. The experiment is presented as an exciting early result, with Robert encouraging others to refine it further by making the printed plastic transparent.
