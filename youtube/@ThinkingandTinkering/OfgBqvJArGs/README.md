## Overview

This is a competition entry video by Gary Slade (Australia) for Robert Murray-Smith's channel, demonstrating a Coca-Cola can wind turbine connected to a zinc-bromide battery that stores power to light an LED. The video expands into an in-depth exploration of zinc-bromide battery design, focusing on a novel HDPE/carbon separator technique that doubles the per-cell voltage, and Gary's many experimental attempts (most of which failed) to build a practical gravity battery for home energy storage.
