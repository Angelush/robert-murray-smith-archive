## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents an upgraded version of his Archimedes screw wind turbine, now integrated with an axial flux coreless generator he designed in TinkerCAD. The build uses only five 3D-printed parts and is tested both as a bare generator (spinning by hand) and outdoors in low wind conditions (~1–2 m/s), demonstrating that it successfully self-starts and generates voltage in real-world conditions.
