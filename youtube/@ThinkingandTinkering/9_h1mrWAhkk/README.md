## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith takes a brief break from lab work to share a handcrafted owl pendant he received as a gift from Alan Ardiff, an artist in Ireland, in exchange for a couple of Edison pens. The video is a personal reflection on the overwhelming positivity of his community, using the gift as a prompt to encourage viewers to recognise and appreciate the positive interactions in their lives rather than fixating on the negative.
