## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tests a motorised quad vehicle he built, driven by a 12-volt motor connected through a gearbox (adapted from a car winch). The test run is chaotic and hilarious, but the main purpose was to learn what would be needed for future electric vehicle conversions — specifically projects called "Bessie" and "the Mini."
