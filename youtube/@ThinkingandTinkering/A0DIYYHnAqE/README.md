## Overview

Robert unboxes and sets up the Elegoo Saturn 4 Ultra 16k resin 3D printer, sent to him by Elegoo for review. After initially resisting the idea (mirroring his earlier resistance to filament printers), he comes around to giving resin printing a fair try, motivated by its exceptional resolution potential for precision engineering parts. The video concludes that the machine is impressively easy to set up out of the box, with a follow-up video planned to cover the wash station and final print quality.
