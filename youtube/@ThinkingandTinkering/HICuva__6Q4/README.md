## Overview

This video demonstrates Robert's method for washing and purifying graphene oxide (GO) after synthesis, aimed at hobbyists who lack centrifuge equipment. Rather than the standard filtration approach (which clogs filter paper), he developed a diffusion-based dilution method followed by slow air-drying, grinding, and a final HCl/deionized water wash. The process takes about a week but requires minimal active time and produces a usable brown GO powder from roughly 5g of graphite starting material.
