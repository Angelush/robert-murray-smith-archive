## Overview

Luke, presenting on Robert Murray-Smith's channel, builds a functional "gaming" PC at zero cost by sourcing all components through scavenging and asking friends. Despite the age and low spec of the parts, the finished machine successfully runs Windows 10 and several games at playable framerates. The video demonstrates that a usable gaming PC can be assembled without spending any money.
