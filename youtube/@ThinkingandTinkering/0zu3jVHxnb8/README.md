## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith revisits his earlier Da Vinci flying pendulum investigation and asks whether the complex upper mechanism is actually necessary. He designs a stripped-down 6-part version in TinkerCAD powered by a rubber band, assembles it, and demonstrates that the simplified concept works — concluding that the original clock mechanism was over-engineered.
