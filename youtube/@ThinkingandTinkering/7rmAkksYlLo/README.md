## Overview

A short practical tip on how to make a perfectly fitting blanking plate without careful measuring. Robert demonstrates using super glue to temporarily attach a steel plate to the existing fitting, drilling through it to transfer the hole positions exactly, then removing the plate with heat. The result is a blanking plate with holes in exactly the right positions.
