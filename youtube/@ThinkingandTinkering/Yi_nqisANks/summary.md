## Overview
A short clip from Robert's Twitch stream in which a friend unexpectedly donates a ride-on lawnmower to the workshop. Robert and his collaborator inspect it, diagnose a broken plastic fuel tank nipple, and brainstorm plans to convert it into a small off-road vehicle — with a "mini jeep" being the leading idea.

- Asking the stream audience for suggestions
