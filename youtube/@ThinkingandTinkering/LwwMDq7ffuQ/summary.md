## Overview
Robert shares his delight upon discovering fan artwork sent to him by a community member known as "Cat's Pajamas," who created illustrated designs he can use freely. He announces plans to put the artwork on merchandise (mugs and t-shirts) via Teespring, while humorously acknowledging that, as a self-effacing Brit, having his own image on a mug feels a little strange but wonderful.
