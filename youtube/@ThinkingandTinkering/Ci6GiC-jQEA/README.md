## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the surprisingly broad range of approaches for building a parabolic reflector to concentrate sunlight for solar thermal applications. He walks through materials, construction methods, and a live burning demonstration, concluding that precise parabolic geometry matters far less than maximising reflectivity — making this an accessible DIY project achievable in roughly half an hour.
