## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a cheap, functional microwave kiln using a basic terracotta plant pot, graphite-based paint, and ceramic fiber blanket insulation. The video explains the science behind microwave kilns — how a susceptor material converts microwave energy into heat — and shows how to use the DIY kiln to fuse glass. It positions the build as a low-cost entry point for hobbyists wanting to try glass fusing or low-temperature ceramics before committing to commercial equipment.
