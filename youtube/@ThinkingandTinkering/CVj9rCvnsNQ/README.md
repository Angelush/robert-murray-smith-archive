## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith examines the KBY DISP KD21C controller fitted to the ESKUTE Voyager e-bike, walking through its multi-level menu system in detail. He demonstrates how to navigate the controller's Nokia-style hierarchical menu to access settings including speed limits, throttle control, and various system parameters. The video serves as a practical guide for understanding and configuring the controller, with a note that modifications to speed limits or throttle are intended for off-road or non-UK-road use only.
