## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to convert a broken electric fret (scroll) saw into a pedal-powered attachment for his "dino pod" — a muscle-powered device he built in earlier videos (1097 and 1099). The motor is removed, replaced with a custom steel spindle and pulley, and connected to the dino pod via a flexible coupling belt. The conversion proves simple and effective, reinforcing his broader theme of salvaging discarded electric tools by replacing their motors with human power.
