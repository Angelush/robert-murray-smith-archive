## Overview

This is a memorial tribute video posted by Robert Murray-Smith in memory of his wife Patti (Patti Murray-Smith), who passed away shortly before the video's publication. It features existing footage of Patti — a teacher by profession — demonstrating a simple bottle rocket experiment for home use, using the acid-base reaction between vinegar and bicarbonate of soda to propel a corked bottle into the air. The video serves both as a science tutorial aimed at encouraging children and families to experiment at home, and as a loving remembrance of Patti's warmth and talent as an educator.
