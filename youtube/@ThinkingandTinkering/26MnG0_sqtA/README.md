## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tests the Elegoo Centauri Carbon 3D printer by running a series of practical prints, comparing it head-to-head against his Bambu printer. The video covers a single-layer "transfer" printing technique developed with a collaborator, a carbon fiber PETG print of a custom latch hook needle, and a general assessment of the machine's speed, noise, and print quality. His main conclusion is that the Elegoo Centauri is a compelling alternative to the Bambu, consistently faster and producing indistinguishable results.
