## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tests his "forever wick" — a carbon felt material — as a direct wick replacement in a brass miner's lamp purchased from eBay. The wick works beautifully in this lamp, which is both exciting and frustrating because it previously failed in a greenhouse heater. The video's central lesson is epistemic humility: thinking you understand something doesn't mean you do.
