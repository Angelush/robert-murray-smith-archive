## Overview

On Boxing Day 2021, Robert makes an improvised wooden catapult toy entirely from kitchen items "stolen" from around the house — a rolling pin, a breadboard, and a salad spoon. The build is spontaneous and unplanned, loosely inspired by a quick glance at Roman catapult designs online, and results in a functional toy capable of firing chocolates across a room.
