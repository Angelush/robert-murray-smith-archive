## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith buys two cheap children's bikes from a local store with the intention of welding them together to create a rideable quad bike. The video covers the disassembly of the frames, the welding process to join them, fabricating a simple push-rod steering system, and mounting a seat. The build is incomplete by the end — the rear drive and motor still need fitting — with a follow-up video promised.
