## Overview

Robert converts a low thermal difference Stirling engine (purchased from Amazon, previously featured in video 1616) into a small electrical generator by attaching magnets around the rim and positioning a solenoid coil nearby. He demonstrates the principle by generating ~0.44V from a hot cup of coffee and successfully lighting an LED using a tea light candle, then reflects on why Stirling engines remain underused in home energy applications.
