## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith announces a reorganisation of his YouTube presence across three channels to make navigation easier for viewers. Content will be split by type: the main channel for hands-on builds and investigations, the subsidiary "Welcome to the World of TnT" for whimsical and humorous content, and the members channel for in-depth serious topics. The goal is to help viewers quickly find the kind of content they're looking for.
