## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives a concise history of solar panel inverter technology, tracing the evolution from simple string inverters to modern microinverters. He explains the electrical and safety problems that drove this evolution, and concludes that microinverters represent a beneficial shift toward plug-and-play solar installations, though they come with their own trade-offs.
