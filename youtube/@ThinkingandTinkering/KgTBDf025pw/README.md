## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces the spherical coulisse (coulisse) power transfer mechanism as an underexplored alternative to conventional mechanical drives. He demonstrates a 3D-printed version of the design, originally sourced from YouTube channel th1146 and redrawn in Tinkercad, showing how it transfers rotational motion at a 2:1 gear ratio without using gears. The key takeaway is that this non-back-drivable sliding mechanism has shown efficiency advantages over traditional drives in some applications (e.g. pumps), though its optimal use cases are still being explored.
