## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to disassemble a broken LCD monitor and reclaim valuable components — particularly the ITO (indium tin oxide) coated conductive glass — for use in DIY solar cell experiments. The key takeaway is that the conductive glass panels recovered from a discarded screen have a commercial value of roughly £150–$200, obtainable for free with about 20 minutes of careful work.
