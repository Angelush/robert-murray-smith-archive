## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents an evolution of his powdered foam soap formula, now compressed into a convenient tablet form. The tablet dissolves in water (hot water speeds up the process) to create a clear foaming handwash solution. He demonstrates its effectiveness by cleaning graphite-stained hands without the scrubbing and soreness associated with concentrated washing-up liquid.
