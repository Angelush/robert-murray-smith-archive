## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a simple, low-cost solar evaporation system for purifying or desalinating water, inspired by university research using graphene films. His solution replaces expensive graphene with florist's Oasis foam and activated carbon felt, which together wick water to a heated black surface and dramatically increase evaporation rate. Over a nine-hour partially cloudy test, the modified setup evaporated 52% more water than a plain bowl of water in the same conditions.
