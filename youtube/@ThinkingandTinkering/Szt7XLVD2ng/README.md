## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the Seiko "Magic Lever" mechanism — invented in 1956 and patented in 1957 — which converts oscillating or bidirectional rotary motion into unidirectional rotation. He 3D-printed a scaled-up version in Tinkercad to demonstrate how this elegant, minimal three-part design could be applied to human-motion energy harvesting, such as powering electronics while walking.
