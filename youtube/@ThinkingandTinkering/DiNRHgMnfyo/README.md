## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a 3D-printed vane motor (a simple pneumatic rotary motor) that he designed in Tinkercad and published on Thingiverse. He attaches a serpentine coil generator — wound with copper wire and fitted with neodymium magnets — to create a compact air-powered generator. The proof-of-concept successfully produces 3 volts simply by blowing into it.
