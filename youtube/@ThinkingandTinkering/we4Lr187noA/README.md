## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith documents his decision to move his workshop from a rented studio unit into his home garage, prompted by realising that 3D printers take up far less space than he assumed. Using the move as a practical opportunity, he walks through how to set up an effective tinkerer's workspace on a budget, arguing that the essential toolkit is simpler and cheaper than most people expect.
