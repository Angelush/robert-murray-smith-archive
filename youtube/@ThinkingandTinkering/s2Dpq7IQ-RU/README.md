## Overview

Robert tests four different adhesives for bonding polyurethane (PU) foam boards (Kingspan, Celotex, Recticel) as part of a larger project to build a tribute replica of the Sinclair C5 electric vehicle. After leaving overnight bonds and performing break tests, he ranks the glues by bond strength and recommends gap-filling foam or gorilla glue as the best options depending on surface conditions.
