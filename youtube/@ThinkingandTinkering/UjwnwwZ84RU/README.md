## Overview

Robert demonstrates a 3D-printed mechanical device that behaves as the physical analogue of an LC tank circuit: a flywheel (representing an inductor) and a spring (representing a capacitor) exchange energy back and forth, producing a sustained oscillation. The device was inspired by a YouTube creator he credits as "Barley Week One" and is presented as an interesting mechanical curiosity, not a free-energy or perpetual motion machine. It will eventually stop due to friction.
