## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith uses the Aufero Laser 2 diode laser cutter/engraver to design and cut parts for a functional air engine from black and yellow acrylic, then assembles them. The video doubles as a real-world capability test of the machine, pushing it beyond its rated specifications — cutting 5mm acrylic and yellow acrylic, neither of which it was officially rated for. The finished engine runs successfully off a vacuum cleaner.
