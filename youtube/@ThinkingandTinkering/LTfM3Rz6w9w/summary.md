## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith announces he is giving away the recipe for his homemade cleaning product, "EcoFoam," for free due to the COVID-19 lockdown. He describes the product's development history — originally created to clean graphene residue from his kitchen — and demonstrates how the powder sachet dissolves in water to produce a foaming cleaner. The full recipe is directed to his members channel, while this video covers the background, capabilities, and packaging evolution of the product.

## Key Topics
- Announcement of the free EcoFoam recipe giveaway (full recipe on members channel)
- Development history: formulated to clean graphene and grease from kitchen surfaces
- Evolution of product format: tablet press → powder sachets
- Demonstration of dissolving the powder sachet in 250 ml of water to produce foam
- Cleaning capabilities: degreasing, descaling, upholstery, carpet spot cleaning
- Regulatory compliance: MSDS and TDS completed, submitted through the UK government cosmetics portal
- Shipping and weight advantages of powder format over liquid (5 g vs. ~300 g)

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- **EcoFoam powder** — the proprietary cleaning product being given away; dissolves in water to form a foaming cleaner
- **Graphene** — the material Robert works with in his kitchen, which prompted the product's development; mentioned as something EcoFoam can clean from surfaces and pores
- **Grease** — mixed with graphene in initial test on kitchen cupboard doors

## Techniques and Methods
- **Tablet pressing** — early production method using a tablet press with a custom 20 mm × 6 mm insert, producing several thousand tablets per hour
- **Sachet/powder packaging** — adopted as a simpler alternative to tablets; easier to dose and package
- **Foam preparation** — adding powder sachet to ~250 ml of water, waiting 5–10 minutes until clear, then dispensing as foam via a spray/foam bottle
- **MSDS/TDS testing** — Material Safety Data Sheet and Technical Data Sheet commissioned; product submitted through UK government cosmetics safety portal
