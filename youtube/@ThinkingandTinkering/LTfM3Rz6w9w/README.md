## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith announces he is giving away the recipe for his homemade cleaning product, "EcoFoam," for free due to the COVID-19 lockdown. He describes the product's development history — originally created to clean graphene residue from his kitchen — and demonstrates how the powder sachet dissolves in water to produce a foaming cleaner. The full recipe is directed to his members channel, while this video covers the background, capabilities, and packaging evolution of the product.
