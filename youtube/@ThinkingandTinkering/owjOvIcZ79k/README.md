## Overview

Robert demonstrates a mold-free method for fabricating fiberglass body panels, adapted from boat-building techniques, as part of his lawnmower-to-electric-vehicle conversion project. He uses recycled sheet PVC as a structural core, fiberglass over it, and finishes with filler and paint. The key insight is that a foam or sheet core can substitute for a traditional mold, enabling one-off bodywork without the expense of mold-making.
