## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith takes on the restoration of a badly neglected potter's wheel that had been sitting outside for roughly 10 years, covered in rust, moss, and snails. He disassembles it, cleans the metal frame, replaces rotten wooden components, and reassembles it with a working motor — demonstrating that even seemingly ruined equipment can be fully recovered. The video's closing moral is a characteristic Robert note: don't be too quick to give up on things, even when they look fit for the bin.
