## Overview
Robert Murray-Smith visits Milan on Christmas Eve to see his son, who has just had a new baby. He takes viewers on a brief walking tour of the city's main attractions, including the ancient canals, Roman ruins, the Castello Sforzesco, the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and the Christmas markets around the Duomo. The video is a warm, informal travelogue capturing Milan's festive atmosphere and layered history.

## Key Topics
- Milan as a working city with weekend exodus culture
- The Navigli canals, dating to the 13th century
- Roman-era ruins integrated into modern buildings (including the 3rd-century circus)
- The Castello Sforzesco (construction began 1368) and its exhibitions
- Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II and luxury shopping district
- Christmas markets, street food, and festive lighting around the Duomo
- Street art and compact electric cars spotted around the city
