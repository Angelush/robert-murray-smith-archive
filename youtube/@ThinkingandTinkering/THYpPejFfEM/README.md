## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to convert a PC fan motor into a small water-powered generator by 3D printing and attaching turbine wheels. He tests both a Pelton wheel and a Turgo turbine (sourced from Thingiverse) on the same PC fan motor, comparing their output. The Turgo turbine proves more capable, producing an estimated 3–4 watts from a small water jet.
