## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith takes a walking tour of Faversham, Kent, to explore the real historical murder that inspired "Arden of Faversham" — a Elizabethan play whose authorship is contested, with Shakespeare among the leading candidates. The video weaves together the town's history (gunpowder manufacturing, its role as a Cinque Port) with the dramatic story of Thomas Arden's murder by his wife Alice and her lover Mosby, concluding that textual analysis supports Shakespeare writing at least the central section of the play.
