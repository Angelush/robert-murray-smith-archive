## Overview

Robert demonstrates a simple water motor inspired by an 1901 design from *Model Engineering and Amateur Electrician*, updated using modern plastic and nylon materials. The motor works similarly to a Tesla turbine — a water jet strikes a textured disc to spin it — but is far easier to build. He runs it as a generator, powering an LED, and argues it represents a practical way to harvest energy from household water pressure.
