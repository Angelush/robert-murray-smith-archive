## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith examines a brand-new Lego worm gear released in April 2023, reverse-engineers its geometry, and 3D prints his own replicas in multiple sizes. Unlike traditional worm gears, this design can be back-driven and arranged in a cross formation where each gear drives the other — a property Robert successfully replicates with his printed versions. He shares the files on Thingiverse and invites the community to explore applications he hasn't yet thought of himself.
