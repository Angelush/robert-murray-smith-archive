## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a "Daisy Solar Motor" — a simple heat engine made from zinc-coated conductive HDPE plastic cut into a daisy/flower shape. When heat is applied, the bimetallic-like material curls and uncurls, causing the rotor to spin continuously. The video is a step-by-step build guide inspired by a design he encountered online, showing the concept is achievable with minimal materials.
