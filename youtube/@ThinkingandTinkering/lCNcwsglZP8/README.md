## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces the Poncelet wheel, a 19th-century undershot water wheel design featuring curved blades that eliminate the energy-wasting "bounce back" of traditional flat-blade wheels. The curved blades allow water to enter smoothly, rise briefly, and drain away slowly — extracting up to 80% of the water's kinetic energy compared to roughly 30% for conventional designs. Robert presents it as a compelling option for small-scale home power generation for anyone fortunate enough to have a stream on their property.
