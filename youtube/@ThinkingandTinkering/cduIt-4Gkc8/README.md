## Overview

This short video explains the geological process by which crude oil forms over millions of years from the remains of microscopic marine organisms. Robert walks through the sequential stages from organism death to oil reservoir formation, covering burial, pressure, temperature, and rock permeability. The key takeaway is that oil is a product of ancient biology and deep geological time.
