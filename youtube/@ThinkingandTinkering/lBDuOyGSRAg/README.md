## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith examines the claims of Holcomb Energy Systems (HES), a company that has promoted a purportedly over-unity magnetic generator for over a decade. Using foundational physics concepts — force, work, energy, and magnetic reluctance — he methodically demonstrates why the device's core claim (that electrical steel produces four times more magnetic flux) is a misunderstanding of flux density versus flux quantity, and concludes there is no credible basis for the generator producing more energy than conventional machines.
