## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith visits Draper's Windmill in Margate to mark a landmark achievement: in the first quarter of 2023, wind power overtook gas as the UK's leading electricity source for the first time. The video contrasts the public's affection for traditional windmills with their resistance to modern wind turbines, arguing that aesthetics are a primary barrier to expanding onshore wind capacity. Robert concludes that making wind turbines more visually appealing could be key to accelerating the UK's renewable energy transition.
