## Overview

This video (episode 2100) builds on the previous Samara wind turbine project by adding tubercles — the bumpy nodules found on humpback whale pectoral fins — to the blade's leading edge. Robert investigates whether this biomimetic modification improves turbine performance, inspired by the research of marine biologist Frank Fish and engineer Philip Watts, who demonstrated a 15–20% improvement in lift and drag reduction. He 3D-prints a modified blade and conducts a side-by-side field comparison on a windy hill, concluding the results are promising but somewhat equivocal given real-world conditions.
