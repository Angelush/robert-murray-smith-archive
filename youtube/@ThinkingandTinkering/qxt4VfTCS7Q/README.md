## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith disassembles a broken strimmer (string trimmer) salvaged from a friend and repurposes its parts into a prop gun inspired by the Mandalorian blaster from Star Wars. Rather than aiming for an exact replica, he takes a Lego-inspired interpretive approach — suggesting the gun's silhouette with readily available scrap materials. The finished prop is wall-mounted and built entirely from salvaged and hardware-store components.
