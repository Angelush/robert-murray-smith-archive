## Overview
Robert takes his ADO A20 foldable e-bike out for a real commute — an eight-mile ride to work — motivated by the 2021 "Great British Petrol Crisis," in which panic buying caused fuel shortages at service stations. The video is a casual first-person account of trying the e-bike as a practical daily commuter alternative to the car.

## Key Topics
- The 2021 UK petrol panic and its self-fulfilling nature
- Using an e-bike as a car replacement for commuting
- First impressions of the ADO A20 foldable e-bike on an 8-mile commute
- The appeal of electric vehicles (and e-bikes) during fuel disruptions
