## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives a hands-on walkthrough of how to level the print bed on the Elegoo Neptune Max 3D printer, explaining the physical mechanism in detail before demonstrating the manual leveling procedure step by step. He clarifies the counter-intuitive relationship between tightening/loosening the adjustment knobs and bed movement, and explains how manual leveling must precede the printer's auto-leveling routine. His main takeaway is that bed leveling is simpler than it seems once you understand the mechanics and just get hands-on with it.
