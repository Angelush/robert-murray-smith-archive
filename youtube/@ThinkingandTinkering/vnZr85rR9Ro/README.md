## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith unboxes and sets up the Aufero Laser 2 (model LU2-4-LF), a diode laser cutter/engraver sent to him by Arturo for review. He provides a brief history of laser cutting technology from 1960 to the present, then walks through the machine's assembly, laser head options, focusing procedure, and key design features. The main takeaway is that modern diode laser cutters like this one are dramatically simpler, cheaper, and more user-friendly than earlier CO2 systems.
