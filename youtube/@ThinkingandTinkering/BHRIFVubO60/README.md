## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith combines two DIY concepts — a saltwater bucket used as a resistive power supply (inspired by a Ugandan maker) and a miniature arc furnace (inspired by The King of Random) — to melt aluminium chips using nothing more exotic than a bucket of salted water connected to a mains socket. The video demonstrates that both the power supply and the furnace can be built from inexpensive, widely available materials, successfully melting aluminium in roughly five minutes.
