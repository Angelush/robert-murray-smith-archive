## Overview

Robert visits a replica Edwardian workshop at the Doha Transport Museum to introduce the concept of drive trains and power transmission. He then presents a novel friction drive he designed and 3D-printed, combining ideas from the National Ignition Facility's (NIF) friction drive and the Archimedes drive — using angled TPU cylinders in a cross-roller-bearing-style arrangement to achieve a ~2.5:1 gear ratio with no backlash and full reversibility.
