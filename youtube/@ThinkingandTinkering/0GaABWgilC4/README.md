## Overview

Robert reviews the Revopoint Miraco, an all-in-one handheld 3D scanner/camera sent to him by Revopoint. He uses it to scan an outdoor sculpture from a local art walk and then scans himself to create a printable bust, demonstrating the full workflow from capture to 3D print. His conclusion is that the device's integration of tablet, battery, and scanner into a single unit makes it exceptionally easy to pick up and use with minimal learning curve.
