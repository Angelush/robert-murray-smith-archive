## Overview

This video demonstrates a DIY bike roller trainer built entirely from scrap materials at zero cost. The presenter (Luke) salvaged a heavy-duty printer roller, scrap wood, and steel shelf angles to create a functional indoor cycling trainer comparable to commercial products like Peloton or bike rollers. The build is straightforward but noted to have a couple of issues — base slipping and requiring the rider to hold on for stability.
