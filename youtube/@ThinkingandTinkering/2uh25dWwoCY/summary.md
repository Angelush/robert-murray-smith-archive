## Overview
This is a community-made video presented by "Luke," introducing the Discord server created by community members for Robert Murray-Smith's channel. It walks viewers through how to join the server and tours its various channels and features. The main takeaway is an invitation for fans to join the Discord community to share projects, exchange ideas, and interact during livestreams.

- How to join Discord (browser-based, no installation required)

- Tour of voice channels: lounge, community hangout, streaming room, staff/members, and stream guests
- The "Project Share" channel highlighted as a standout feature for community collaboration
