## Overview

This video concludes the multi-part project of electrifying a homemade scooter (begun in video 1106). Presenter Luke fabricates and installs a steel battery carrier, corrects the motor drive direction, adds a derailleur for chain tension, and completes a successful outdoor test ride. The final build achieves an estimated top speed of 10–15 mph using a 42:12 tooth gear ratio and nickel metal hydride batteries.
