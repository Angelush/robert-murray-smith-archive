## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates his DIY copper plating kit, available from his Working Ink shop, designed to let fans create a Harry Potter-style copper-plated magic wand. The kit includes nearly everything needed — from wand wood and string to conductive ink and copper sulfate — making it accessible for beginners and science enthusiasts alike. The full plating process takes around 30 hours to achieve a complete copper coating.
