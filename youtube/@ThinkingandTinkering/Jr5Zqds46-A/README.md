## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith documents his very first experiences with arc/stick welding, purchased specifically to support upcoming fabrication projects like a gasifier unit and a DIY quad bike. Rather than offering a technical tutorial, he focuses on the psychological and practical barriers that beginners face — particularly the fear of striking an arc — and shares what actually helped him get started after just a day or two of practice.
