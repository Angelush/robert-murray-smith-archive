## Overview

This video is Part 2 of Robert's series on making solar cells from broken LCD monitors, focusing on recovering valuable materials from the front panel. He demonstrates how to mechanically scrape indium tin oxide (ITO) from the glass, then extract pure indium using a dilute oxalic acid process — a scaled-down version of an industrial reclamation method that achieves ~99% indium recovery.
