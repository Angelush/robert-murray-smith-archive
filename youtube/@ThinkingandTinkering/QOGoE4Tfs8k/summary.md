## Overview
Luke takes the ESKUTE Voyager electric mountain bike (27.5", 250W) through a series of real-world off-road tests in rural England, including muddy fields, country lanes, debris-strewn farm tracks, and an orchard slalom. The bike handles all terrain impressively, and notably retains full battery after 20–30 minutes of high-assist riding, sparking a new hobby for the presenter.

## Key Topics
- Introduction and overview of the ESKUTE Voyager e-MTB
- Off-road field test in wet, muddy conditions
- Country lane riding on narrow English roads
- Navigation through farm debris and rough terrain
- Orchard slalom at 11–15 mph
- Battery life performance under sustained high-assist use
- Fitting the bike into a small hatchback using quick-release front wheel
