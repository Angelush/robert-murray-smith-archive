## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith shares a practical tip for successfully using aluminium welding (brazing) rods on large or heavy aluminium pieces, a task he found difficult until discovering the preheating trick. By placing the aluminium workpiece on a hot plate to raise its temperature close to the rod's melting point, then adding a small amount of blowtorch heat, clean and strong welds become achievable. He demonstrates the technique in the context of fabricating an aluminium blower housing for his gasifier project.
