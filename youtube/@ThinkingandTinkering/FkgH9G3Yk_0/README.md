## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents an improved, simpler method for synthesizing graphene oxide (GO) at home, addressing complaints that his previous method was too complex and required strict temperature control. The new approach — essentially the Tour/Marcano method — requires only about 5 minutes of preparation, no heating, and just 3–4 days of passive stirring, with an approximate 100% yield of graphene oxide.
