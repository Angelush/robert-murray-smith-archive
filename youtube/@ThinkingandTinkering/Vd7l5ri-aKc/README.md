## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith visits Reclamet, described as the largest scrapyard in Kent and one of the largest in Europe, to source parts for upcoming projects including a steam engine build and a generator conversion. The video is a short tour/vlog showcasing the value of scrapyards as a resource for DIY inventors and tinkerers, highlighting specific finds like alternators and a catalytic converter that will feed into future lab projects.
