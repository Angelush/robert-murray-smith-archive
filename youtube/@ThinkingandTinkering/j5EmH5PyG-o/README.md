## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith follows up a previous video criticising the patent system by tackling the harder question: what should actually be done about it? Using the story of Andre Geim (co-discoverer of graphene) being warned off patenting by a large electronics company, he argues the system has become fundamentally corrupt. His proposed solution centres on openness, shared stakes, and accepting a "fair return" rather than chasing monopoly wealth — a philosophy he practises himself by sharing his work publicly.
