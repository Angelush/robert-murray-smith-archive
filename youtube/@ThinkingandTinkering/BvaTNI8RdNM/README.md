## Overview

Robert demonstrates using the "Strange Engine" — a compact thermoacoustic/oscillating-diaphragm device — as an electrical generator via electromagnetic induction. He builds a simplified two-container version of the engine, mounts N42 magnets on its balloon diaphragm, and couples it to a salvaged microwave turntable coil to successfully light an LED panel, achieving up to 51V peak unloaded. The video also previews several other generation approaches planned for future videos.
