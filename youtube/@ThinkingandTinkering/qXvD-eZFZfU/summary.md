## Overview
Luke disassembles a discarded pressure washer to explore its internal components, identifying the pump mechanism and universal motor. Through hands-on teardown, he gains a basic understanding of how the pump uses a spring-and-bearing assembly to generate pressure, and demonstrates that the salvaged motor still works by running it from a DC power supply.

## Key Topics
- Teardown of a consumer pressure washer
- Identification and explanation of the pump mechanism
- Overview of the universal motor and its gear coupling to the pump
- Demonstration of the universal motor running on DC power
- Potential reuse/repurposing of salvaged components

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
None mentioned.

## Techniques and Methods
- Mechanical disassembly (unscrewing casing, removing pump and motor components)
- Bench testing a salvaged universal motor using a DC power supply at 12V
