## Overview

Robert demonstrates a working artificial muscle — a pneumatic soft actuator — attached to a simple arm-like rig. When inflated with air (blown by hand), the device contracts and bunches up, pulling on a tendon and lever in a motion closely analogous to biological muscle function. The video is a short proof-of-concept showing the principle in action.
