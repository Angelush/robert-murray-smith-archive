## Overview

Robert demonstrates a 4-tier version of Heron's Fountain that improves on his previous 3-cylinder design by allowing continuous operation with a simple flip — no waiting for water to transfer between cycles. The device works by essentially combining two 3-cylinder Heron's fountains so that one side charges while the other discharges. While he uses the word "perpetual" tongue-in-cheek, he is clear that the user inputs energy each time they flip the device.
