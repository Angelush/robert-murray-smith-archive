## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores Leonardo da Vinci's iconic catapult design, discussing Da Vinci's legacy as a Renaissance polymath before walking through his own 3D-printed interpretation of the machine. He demonstrates assembly of the printed model and then shows how he designed it in Tinkercad using simple primitive shapes, encouraging viewers to try drawing it themselves.
