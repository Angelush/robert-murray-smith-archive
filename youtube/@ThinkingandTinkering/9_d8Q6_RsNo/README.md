## Overview

Robert continues developing his CD4 longboard prototype (first seen in video #1181), correcting the truck orientation based on viewer feedback, removing the bottom skids to reduce ride height, and laminating the underside with carbon fiber reinforced Kevlar. The result is a functional "bulletproof" longboard that receives positive test rides from both Robert and a friend named Luke/Matt.
