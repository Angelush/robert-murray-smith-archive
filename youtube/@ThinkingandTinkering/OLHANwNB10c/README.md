## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith constructs a small wind turbine using pairs of men's underwear ("tighty-whities") as the sail elements, mounted on a DIY frame. The structure is assembled from a music stand tripod, wooden discs with bearings, timber uprights, and wooden stair sections. The result is a novelty demonstration that the underwear-sail concept can generate rotation in the wind.
