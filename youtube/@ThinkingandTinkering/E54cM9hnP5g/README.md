## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith announces planned changes to his YouTube channel after eight years and over 1,100 videos. He explains that the sheer volume of content has made it difficult to navigate, and outlines a reorganization effort: removing low-quality videos, re-editing multi-part series into single concise videos, and organizing content into better playlists. The goal is to make information — particularly on battery-building — easier to find and use.
