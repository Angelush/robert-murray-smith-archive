## Overview

This video explores the historical use of animals in warfare, from ancient times through the mid-20th century. Robert surveys a range of real military programs that attempted to weaponise animals as delivery systems for explosives or as guided weapons, most of which ended in failure. The central takeaway is that while necessity drove many creative and desperate ideas during wartime, animals proved too unpredictable and unreliable as military assets, and technology ultimately offered a more practical alternative.
