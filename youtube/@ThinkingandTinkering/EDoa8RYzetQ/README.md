## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to repurpose a scrap catalytic converter (donated by Rick Clement's scrapyard) into a flameless catalytic heater. He cuts a section from the ceramic honeycomb core, layers it with a wicking material, stainless steel mesh, and ethanol fuel, then ignites it to show that once the catalyst reaches operating temperature, it produces significant heat without any visible flame.
