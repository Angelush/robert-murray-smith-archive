## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith debunks the "Liberty Engine," a free energy machine that has been circulating as clickbait on YouTube. He argues that the fundamental error behind all such claims is a misunderstanding of the word "generator" — a generator does not create electricity, it converts mechanical energy into electrical energy. Using a memorable carrot-slicing analogy, he explains why no arrangement of motors and generators can produce more energy than is put in.
