## Overview

Robert 3D prints Pelton wheel buckets using an Elegoo Mars 2B Pro resin printer and mounts them on a pseudo-levitating magnetic bearing (built in a prior video) to create a miniature hydroelectric generator. Running water through the wheel at partial tap pressure, he generates approximately 6 volts and 100 milliamps from a small coil, demonstrating a fast-spinning, effective water motor. The video is part of his ongoing water motor series.
