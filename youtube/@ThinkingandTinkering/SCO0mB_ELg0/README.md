## Overview

Robert explains the three types of heat transfer (convection, conduction, radiation) and argues that convection — the dominant method used in UK homes — is the least efficient. He builds on his earlier carbon-felt wick oil burner (from video 1680) by replacing the ceramic plant pot with a stainless steel saucepan, demonstrating it reaches 53°C in 5 minutes and 77–78°C in 10 minutes, making it a more effective radiant heater.
