## Overview

Building on his earlier rainwater turbine project (video 1886), Robert explores the fundamental difference between mass-based and momentum-based water turbines. He compares a Stirling-style turbine against a Kaplan turbine blade on the same generator, demonstrating that matching turbine type to water flow characteristics (high mass/low head vs. low mass/high momentum) produces meaningfully better electrical output. The key takeaway is that there is no universal turbine — the right choice depends entirely on your specific water supply conditions.
