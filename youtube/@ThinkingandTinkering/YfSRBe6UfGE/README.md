## Overview

This video demonstrates how to transfer an inkjet-printed image onto a piece of wood using varnish as a transfer medium. The presenter (Luke) walks through the full process step by step, including a mid-video correction after encountering air bubble problems on the first attempt. The final result is a rustic Spitfire print mounted in a silver frame, made as a Christmas gift.
