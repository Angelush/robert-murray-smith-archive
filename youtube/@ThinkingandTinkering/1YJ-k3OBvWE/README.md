## Overview

This video presents computational fluid dynamics (CFD) modeling work done by community member Trey Rousey using the open-source tool FreeFLOW, analyzing the airflow behavior of the "Darwin Waters Wind Turbine" — a squirrel-cage-style wind turbine with a passive wind-capture sock. The modeling confirms that baffle plates and a hyperbolic cone are effective at directing airflow through the turbine's vanes, validating the design direction. Robert shares this as an example of community collaboration driving the project forward.
