## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith converts an electric tricycle into a pedal-powered emergency generator ahead of Storm Eunice hitting the UK in February 2022. By attaching a hand-crank generator (built in a previous video) to the trike's gear cluster via a sprocket and chain, he demonstrates a reversible setup that produces usable power for charging phones and batteries — without permanently modifying the bicycle.
