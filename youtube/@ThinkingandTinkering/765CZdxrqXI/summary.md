## Overview
In this short companion video to episode 1403, Robert Murray-Smith announces that his friend Paul Burbeck has created a detailed set of technical drawings for the portable rocket stove/camping stove built in that earlier video. The plans are being offered completely free of charge — viewers simply need to email Robert to receive them.

- Reference to the original portable rocket stove build (video 1403)
- Paul Burbeck's contribution of detailed technical drawings with measurements
- Slideshow walkthrough of the plans
- Free distribution of the plans via email request

## Robert's Own Replies
Robert's comments are mostly brief affirmations and casual exchanges, but one reply stands out as a substantive clarification: when a commenter apparently questioned the design's safety or whether it truly qualifies as a rocket stove, Robert pushed back good-naturedly — acknowledging that no design is entirely foolproof, disagreeing that it isn't a rocket stove, and noting that ultimately the choice of whether to build it is the viewer's own. He also confirms in another reply that a larger/heavier version would work but would be difficult to carry, suggesting the portable form factor was a deliberate design priority.
