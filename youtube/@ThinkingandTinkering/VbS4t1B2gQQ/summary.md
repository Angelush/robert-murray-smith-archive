## Overview
Robert visits an ancient Cambodian temple built around the 9th–10th century AD and points out a stone carving that strikingly resembles a stegosaurus. He poses the playful but thought-provoking question of whether such creatures could have still existed in the Cambodian jungle at the time of the temple's construction. The video is a short, wonder-filled observation inviting viewers to consider the mystery.

## Key Topics
- Stone carvings at a Cambodian temple (circa 900 AD)
- A carving interpreted as depicting a stegosaurus
- The question of whether dinosaurs or large unknown creatures may have survived into historical times in remote jungles
