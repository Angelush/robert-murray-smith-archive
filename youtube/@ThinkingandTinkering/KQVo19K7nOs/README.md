## Overview

A short, lighthearted video in which Robert discovers he needs a single wall plug to rehang a curtain rail, but is 40 minutes from the nearest store. Rather than making the drive, he downloads a model from Thingiverse and 3D prints a replacement wall plug in about 10 minutes, demonstrating how having the right mindset about your tools can save both time and money.
