## Overview

This video is a follow-up to Robert's previous hemp casein bioplastic-making video, demonstrating how to take the dried bioplastic material and form it into a solid molded block using heat and pressure. He packs the dry, breadcrumb-like material into an aluminium soap mold, heats it to around 150°C, and applies compression via a vise and heat press. The result is a formed bioplastic block, with Robert emphasising that much of his process is exploratory guesswork and that viewers should treat this as a starting guide rather than a definitive recipe.
