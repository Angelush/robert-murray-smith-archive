## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith traces the history of the spirit burner from its origins in alchemical laboratories of ancient Egypt and China through medieval Europe and into modern chemistry, arguing it symbolises the transition from mystical to empirical science. He then demonstrates how to build one yourself using simple materials like a glass bottle, a wick, and a brass plumbing cap. The video concludes with a working alcohol burner filled with methylated spirits.
