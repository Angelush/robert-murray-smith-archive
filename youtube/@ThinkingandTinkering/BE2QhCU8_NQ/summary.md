## Overview
This video features Luke, a collaborator on Robert Murray-Smith's channel, using a 3D printer for the very first time. He prints the final component of his steampunk lamp build — a decorative cage to display the exposed bulb — following setup guidance from Robert's earlier tutorial videos. The print succeeds on the first try after roughly 3.5–4 hours, completing the long-running lamp project.
