## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to adapt a cheap £5 circlip tool into a miniature circlip pliers capable of removing tiny metal circlips from small electronic components. Rather than paying £66 for a specialist tool, he files down the pins of a standard circlip tool to fit the smaller clips. The broader takeaway is a general philosophy for DIY toolmaking: identify the job, find the nearest existing tool, and adapt it.
