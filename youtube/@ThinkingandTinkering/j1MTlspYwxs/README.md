## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build an ultra-simple gasification stove (or mini rocket stove) using just two supermarket tin cans — one of mushy peas and one of new potatoes — costing 55p total. The build requires only drilling and filing holes in specific patterns before nesting the cans together, and the result burns twigs effectively. He then fits a gas mantle (from a previous video) to transform the flame from a simple fire into a bright, efficient heat and light source.
