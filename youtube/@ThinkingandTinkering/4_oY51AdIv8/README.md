Robert Murray-Smith follows up on his previous vortex stove build (video 1736) by adding a stainless steel mesh mantle, an idea suggested by viewers and inspired by his earlier methanol heater project (video 1702). He demonstrates the mantle's dramatic effect on the flame, producing an impressively intense vortex fire, while acknowledging it needs better fuel control for practical use as a space heater.
