## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith follows up on a previous DIY air conditioning video by explaining the modular "logical block" approach to building a custom AC unit, addressing viewer comments about design flexibility. He walks through each component — bellows, valve chest, and radiator — emphasising that the specific geometry and materials are secondary to understanding the function each block performs. The key takeaway is that a 12V DC-powered, bellows-driven system can be efficient, off-grid-compatible, and adaptable to many form factors.
