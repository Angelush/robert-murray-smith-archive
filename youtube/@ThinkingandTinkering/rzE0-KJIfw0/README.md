## Overview

This short video, filmed at the "Pink Ink Factory," demonstrates conductive ink being used to hand-paint a circuit that successfully lights an LED at approximately 9 volts. It serves as a quick proof-of-concept showcasing the practical capability of the ink for simple electronics fabrication. The video highlights the accessibility of conductive ink as a tool for building circuits without conventional PCB processes.
