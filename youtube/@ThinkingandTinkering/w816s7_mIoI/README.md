## Overview

Robert demonstrates a surprisingly simple and free method for creating time-lapse videos of 3D prints using only a USB webcam, the built-in Windows Camera app, and a free Microsoft Store app called "Timelapse Creator." He walks through the full workflow — from capturing ~3,800 photos over a 12-hour Triceratops skull print to stitching them into a finished video — concluding that when technology just works this effortlessly, it's worth sharing.
