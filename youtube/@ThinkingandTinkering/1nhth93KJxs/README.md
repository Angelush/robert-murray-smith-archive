## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith traces the full journey from crude oil formation to plastics manufacturing, then examines what it would take to reverse that process — converting plastic waste back into useful fuel or oil. The central challenge is not the chemistry itself (pyrolysis is well understood), but reducing the energy input required so the process yields more energy than it consumes. He concludes that the real "secret" lies in proprietary catalyst formulations, exemplified by companies like Canadian startup Aduro.
