## Overview

In this short opinion piece, Robert Murray-Smith responds to a comment suggesting he has a duty of care as an influencer to model strict health and safety practices for young viewers. He argues that over-protection and instilling fear in children is a primary cause of the "snowflake generation," and that allowing kids to take risks, make mistakes, and earn "war wounds" is essential to developing confidence, pride, and resilience.
