## Overview

Robert demonstrates photonic (flash) sintering of two types of copper-based inks — copper nanoparticles and an organometallic copper ink — applied as dried spots on paper. He shows that brief exposure to a high-intensity flash unit dramatically improves the conductivity of the copper nanoparticle sample (from non-conductive to ~184 kΩ), while the organometallic ink requires significantly more flashing to achieve the same effect.
