## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to build a seat for his "paper trike" recumbent bike project, breaking the build into three components: a structural cradle, a thin plywood seat backing, and foam/leatherette upholstery. The process uses a free downloadable template and simple woodworking techniques, and Robert notes the finished seat shape is attractive enough to work as a standalone contemporary chair design.
