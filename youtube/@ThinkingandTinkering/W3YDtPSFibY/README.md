## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to convert a cheap 12-pound heated car seat cover into a mains-powered heated chair by rewiring it to a 12V 5A power supply. The hack is simple, low-cost, and produces a 36-watt resistive heater for back and seat warming. He treats the heating element as a basic resistor and notes the wiring polarity doesn't matter.
