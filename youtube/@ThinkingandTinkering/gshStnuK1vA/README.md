## Overview

Robert demonstrates using a previously built Tesla turbine with an involute aluminium housing as an air pump for his wood gasifier project. He tests it at both low (60V) and full mains voltage (240V DC), successfully drawing air as shown by cigarette smoke. He also presents a disassembled handheld cordless vacuum cleaner as a cheaper backup alternative, concluding that both are viable options for supplying air to the gasifier during startup.
