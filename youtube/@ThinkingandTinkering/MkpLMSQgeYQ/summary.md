## Overview
A short "Advent Science Magic" video in which Robert demonstrates how two paper clips placed on a Z-folded banknote will interlink when the bill is pulled apart. He then explains that the apparent magic is actually topology — the S-shaped fold of the bill transfers its shape to the paper clips, causing them to join.

- Rapid pull of both ends of the bill to cause the clips to interlink
