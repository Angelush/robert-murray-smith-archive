## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith introduces the EcoFoam Hard Surface Cleaner, part of his growing "EcoFoam" product line concept. The core idea is to eliminate the waste and expense of shipping water-heavy liquid cleaners by instead providing concentrated powder or tablet form that the user dissolves in 250ml of water at home. He demonstrates the cleaner's impressive ability to remove graphite and molybdenum grease from surfaces and hands with minimal effort.
