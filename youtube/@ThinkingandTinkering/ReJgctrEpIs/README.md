## Overview

Robert shares his philosophy on design, sparked by a conversation with Luke about designing a computer with a distinctive feel. His central argument is that good design provides just enough information to suggest a style — no more, no less — allowing the viewer to mentally complete the image and become engaged. He illustrates this with steampunk PC examples and his own project recreating "Bessie" (an Edwardian-style car) on a lawn mower.
