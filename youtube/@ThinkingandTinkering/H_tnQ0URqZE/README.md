## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith builds on his previous maglev turbine video (1877) by exploring how the squirrel cage rotor works in multiple wind directions due to its aerodynamic wing-shaped vanes. He then proposes an exciting combination: mounting a Darwin wind turbine (a conical wind concentrator) on top of a Waters turbine, arguing this pairing could produce one of the most efficient small wind turbines possible.
