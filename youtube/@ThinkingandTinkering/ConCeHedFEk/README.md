## Overview

In this short meta-video, Robert Murray-Smith directly addresses the recurring question of what his YouTube channel is actually for. He outlines a four-fold purpose — to inspire, inform, educate, and entertain — and emphasises that his videos are intended as jumping-off points for viewers' own experimentation, not as prescriptive recipes to follow. The central message is that the channel exists to spark curiosity and independent thinking, not to prove claims or hand-hold viewers through step-by-step instructions.
