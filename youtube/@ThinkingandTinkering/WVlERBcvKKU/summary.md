## Overview
A short video in which Robert Murray-Smith combines two earlier projects — homemade bellows and a PC fan repurposed as a generator — to create a simple bellows-driven generator. By pumping the bellows, he forces air through the fan, generating enough electricity to light an LED. The video is a playful proof-of-concept demonstrating mechanical-to-electrical energy conversion using salvaged household components.

## Key Topics
- Combining two previous DIY projects into one demonstration
- Bellows originally built for a homemade air conditioning unit

## Techniques and Methods
- Repurposing a PC fan as an electrical generator by spinning it with forced airflow
- Mounting the fan at the air outlet of a bellows assembly
- Hand-pumping bellows to produce an airflow sufficient to drive the fan and generate current
