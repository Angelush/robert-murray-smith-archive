## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith visits Locomotion, the National Railway Museum's satellite site in Shildon, County Durham — the birthplace of the railways. He tours the collection of early steam locomotives, including a pre-Locomotion No.1 Stephenson engine, a model of Locomotion, the Sans Pareil (1829), and part of the Royal Train exhibit. The video celebrates the ingenuity of early steam engineering and the surprising power generated by simple piston mechanisms.
