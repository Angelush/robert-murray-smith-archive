## Overview

Robert demonstrates a simple, low-cost jig for making straight cuts on metal cylinders, built from four casters and a block of wood with an angle grinder mounted on a hinged block. The video covers sourcing scrap metal from a local scrapyard, building the jig, and using it to cleanly cut steel fire extinguisher bodies, gas cylinders, and plastic soil pipe.
