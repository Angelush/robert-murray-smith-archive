## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents a FEMM 4.2 magnetic simulation of the Flynn device, demonstrating that pulsing a single side coil instead of the two center coils produces the same flux effect at a fraction of the power — approximately 2 watts versus 16 watts. He concludes the video by announcing his intention to build a physical prototype to test the simulation findings.
