## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents the completed homemade ukulele he has been building, walking through the final assembly details and fret spacings. He demonstrates how to correctly measure fret positions (always from the nut, not fret-to-fret) and gives a brief playthrough to show how it sounds. The video serves as a practical conclusion to his ukulele-building project.
