## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to make a bioplastic composite panel by impregnating a hemp fiber mat with a liquid seaweed-based resin, then curing it in an oven or kiln. The result is a rigid, fully natural, biodegradable hard panel. He argues this material is ideally suited to artisanal production rather than industrial manufacturing, and could replace conventional plastics in flat-panel applications like furniture, boxes, and enclosures.
