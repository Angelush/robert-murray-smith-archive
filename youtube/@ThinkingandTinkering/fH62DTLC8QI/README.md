## Overview

A ~3-hour sponsored live stream in which Robert Murray-Smith and his colleague Luke unbox, assemble, and review the new Elegoo Neptune 4 (filament) and Saturn Ultra (resin) 3D printers, walk through software installation, and host a 33-prize giveaway. The main takeaway is Robert's enthusiastic endorsement of Elegoo machines as reliable, affordable tools after eight months of near-continuous use, with particular excitement about the Neptune 4's dramatic speed improvements and the Saturn Ultra's 0.01mm resolution.
