## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tears down a £30 mini ceramic PTC heater to examine its components and evaluate the marketing claims made about these products. He demonstrates that PTC heaters offer no efficiency advantage over any other electric resistance heater, debunks common advertising claims, and raises significant safety concerns about the specific unit examined — which lacked any fuse or thermal cutout.
