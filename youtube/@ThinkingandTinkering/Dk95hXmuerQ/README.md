## Overview

Luke demonstrates how to build a DIY resistive cup warmer using conductive ink and copper strips. The cup is painted with conductive ink between two copper contact strips, and placed on a custom "warming plate" with spring contacts salvaged from a battery box. The completed device successfully heats liquid in the cup to around 50°C.
