## Overview

Robert tests a Chinese diesel heater running on a homemade fuel blend — used cooking oil clarified with gelatin and mixed with 20% paraffin — as a cheap alternative to diesel. He runs the heater for approximately half an hour, demonstrating a clean, odour-free burn with good heat output. The main takeaway is that this DIY fuel mix (costing roughly 30–50p per litre vs. £1.80/L for diesel) works well in these simple heaters, making them a viable and affordable heating solution.
