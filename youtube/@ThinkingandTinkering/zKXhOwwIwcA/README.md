## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a wood tongue drum using salvaged oak floorboards and a minimal toolset (chop saw and drill). The video focuses on simplicity and accessibility, showing how individual wooden keys can be mounted separately on a basic box frame — making the instrument easier to build, tune, and repair than traditional single-block tongue drums. The finished instrument is gifted to his brother.
