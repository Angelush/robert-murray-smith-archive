## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith gives a walkthrough of his lab after recently downsizing from two units into one, explaining the layout and showing the range of equipment, ongoing projects, and materials on hand. The video reveals the breadth of his work — spanning electrochemistry, energy storage, fabrication, and video production — all operating out of a single compact industrial unit. Despite the tighter space, Robert presents the setup as functional and even improved in some respects (better audio due to the mezzanine ceiling).
