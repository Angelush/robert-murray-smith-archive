## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith walks through the fundamental mathematics behind wind turbine power generation, starting from first principles (force, mass, kinetic energy) and building up to the real-world efficiency losses that reduce a turbine's practical output. The video demystifies the Betz limit and tip speed ratio, showing that the physics behind wind energy is straightforward once you break it down step by step.
