## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a miniature Flynn motor using salvaged transformer parts, neodymium magnets, and soft iron laminations. He explains that scaling the motor down improves efficiency due to the more favorable volume-to-surface-area ratio at smaller sizes. The video walks through assembly step by step, with plans to test the device and measure current output.
