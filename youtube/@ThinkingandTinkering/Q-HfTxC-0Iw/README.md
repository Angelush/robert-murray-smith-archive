## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents his completed "Crystal Antikythera" — a laser-cut replica of the ancient Greek Antikythera mechanism, which is a geocentric orrery (celestial computer) dating from roughly 150–87 BC. Built using the xTool P2 CO2 laser cutter in just three days, the model comprises approximately 890 parts and demonstrates the planetary motions as seen from Earth, including epicyclic movement and lunar phases. A full step-by-step build video and STL files on Thingiverse are promised as follow-ups.
