## Overview

This short video explains the mechanical operation of a bicycle rear derailleur. Presenter Luke demonstrates how the parallelogram linkage moves the chain laterally across the sprocket cluster to change gears, and explains the role of limit screws in preventing the chain from overshooting. The key takeaway is that derailleur function is straightforward, relying on a simple parallelogram geometry combined with shaped sprocket teeth.
