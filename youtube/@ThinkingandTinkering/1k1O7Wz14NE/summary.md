## Overview
In this short Advent Science Magic video (Day 2 of a Christmas series), Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a "Magic Money Box" that makes coins appear to vanish. He quickly reveals the trick: a mirror angled at 45 degrees inside the box reflects the bottom, creating an optical illusion. He uses the demo as a jumping-off point to note that similar optical illusions are commonly used to fake "free energy" devices on YouTube.

## Key Topics
- Optical illusions and how the human eye/brain can be deceived
- The 45-degree mirror trick as a classic stage magic technique
- A gentle warning about free energy device hoaxes on YouTube that exploit the same principle

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Mirror (placed at 45 degrees inside a box)
- Coins/money (used as the vanishing objects)

## Techniques and Methods
- 45-degree mirror placement to reflect the box's interior base, making objects placed behind the mirror appear absent
- Close-up demonstration to show the illusion in action before revealing the mechanism
