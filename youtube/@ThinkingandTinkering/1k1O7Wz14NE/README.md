## Overview

In this short Advent Science Magic video (Day 2 of a Christmas series), Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates a "Magic Money Box" that makes coins appear to vanish. He quickly reveals the trick: a mirror angled at 45 degrees inside the box reflects the bottom, creating an optical illusion. He uses the demo as a jumping-off point to note that similar optical illusions are commonly used to fake "free energy" devices on YouTube.
