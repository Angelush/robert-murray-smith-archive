## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tests an Archimedes screw water turbine he built from builders' plastic in a small stream near his home, after previously being unable to test it due to drought conditions. The device produces 5.1 volts, which he considers a clear success. He highlights its potential as an ultra-cheap emergency power supply, noting it is a replication of a Japanese invention.
