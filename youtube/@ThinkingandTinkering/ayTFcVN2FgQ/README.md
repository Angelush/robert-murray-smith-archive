## Overview

Robert builds an improved water-powered turbine capable of generating approximately 3 watts of electricity — enough to charge a phone — by iterating on his earlier 1890s-inspired design (video 1464) based on viewer suggestions. The key improvement is directing the water jet at the edge of a flywheel fitted with timing-belt teeth, then enclosing it in a vented plywood box with an attached generator. The video emphasises accessibility: the device can be built from common, inexpensive materials by anyone.
