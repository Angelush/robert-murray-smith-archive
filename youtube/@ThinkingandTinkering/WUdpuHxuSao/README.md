## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to convert a commercially available Vevor tent stove into an L-type rocket stove with minimal materials and effort. He explains the history and operating principles of rocket stoves — specifically pyrolysis and staged combustion — and shows that the Vevor stove's geometry already behaves like one naturally. The conversion requires only two changes: reversing the grate and adding insulation.
