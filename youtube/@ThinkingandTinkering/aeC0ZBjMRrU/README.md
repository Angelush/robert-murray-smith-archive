This short video shows Robert's home-built SILAR (Successive Ionic Layer Adsorption and Reaction) machine running its first or early cycles. SILAR is a thin-film deposition technique that builds up conductive coatings by alternately dipping a substrate into ionic solutions. The video appears to be a brief working demonstration of the automated dipping apparatus.
