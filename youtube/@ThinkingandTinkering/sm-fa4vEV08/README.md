## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith tests the electric version of his paper bicycle — a bike frame built from rolled-up newspapers — by riding it around a car park. The test is a success: the paper frame holds up under his weight, the gear system works silently and effectively, and the 120W motor gets him moving. He considers the build a genuine win and is already planning a Mark 2 version with a 500W motor.
