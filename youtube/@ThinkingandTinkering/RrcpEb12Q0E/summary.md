## Overview
This short video features Luke (a lab collaborator) demonstrating a homemade "Miracle Cleaner" solution, originally formulated by Robert, on a heavily stained lab sink. Regular all-purpose cleaner fails to shift the stains, while the miracle cleaner removes them in just 3–4 minutes, leaving the sink visibly shining.

- Introduction to the "Miracle Cleaner" recipe developed by Robert
- Comparison test: commercial all-purpose cleaner vs. the miracle cleaner on a stained lab sink
- Practical demonstration of the cleaning process and results

## Techniques and Methods
- Spraying commercial cleaner and scrubbing with a sponge/cloth as a baseline comparison
- Applying the miracle cleaner solution directly to stained surface
- Mechanical scrubbing with a metal skewer to agitate and lift stains
- Wiping down with blue roll (blue paper towel) to reveal results
