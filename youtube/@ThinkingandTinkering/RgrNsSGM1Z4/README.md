## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explores the concept of wind belts and wind bars, tracing the evolution from fluttering ribbons to rigid oscillating bars, culminating in a DIY replication of the Zephyr Energy machine. He 3D prints a simplified version of the device, which uses transverse galloping and vortex shedding to generate oscillation, and demonstrates that even an unoptimized prototype produces measurable movement. The video serves as an open invitation for community experimentation using his shared STL files.
