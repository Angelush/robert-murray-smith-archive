## Overview

Robert revisits his SWAG (Solid-state Wideband... energy generation) project, demonstrating improved power output by switching to aluminium chloride as the electrolyte salt. He then explores separating the magnetically-aligned diamagnetic and magnetic carbon regions of SWAG ink to dramatically boost current output, extrapolating the results to suggest a potential of ~240 W/m² — exceeding commercial solar cells — if the technique is scaled up properly.
