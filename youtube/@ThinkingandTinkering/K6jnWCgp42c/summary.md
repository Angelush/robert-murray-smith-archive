## Overview
This short video demonstrates a simple trick for removing stripped screws without drilling them out. A presenter named Luke shares the technique using only an elastic band placed over the stripped screw head to restore grip for a drill bit. The method is presented as a reliable, low-effort solution that has worked for the presenter on multiple occasions.

## Key Topics
- Removing stripped screws without drilling
- Using household materials as a friction aid
- Demonstration on a purposely stripped Torx screw

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Elastic band (cut in half, used as a grip interface between drill bit and stripped screw head)

## Techniques and Methods
- Placing a cut elastic band over the stripped screw head to increase friction
- Applying firm downward pressure with a drill while engaging the bit
- Slowly reversing the drill once grip is felt to extract the screw
