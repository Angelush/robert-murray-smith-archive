## Overview

This video is part of Robert Murray-Smith's paper-based making competition, featuring a submission by a community member named Ron who built a Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) primarily from foam board and recycled materials. Robert opens with an apology for nearly missing Ron's entry due to email overload, then hands over to Ron who walks through the design, construction, and operation of his turbine. The main takeaway is a functional, low-cost VAWT prototype with an innovative asymmetric blade belly design intended to improve wind capture efficiency.
