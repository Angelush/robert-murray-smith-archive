## Overview

Robert demonstrates the assembly of a 3D-printed combined continuously variable transmission (CVT) and clutch designed to fit inside a bicycle wheel hub. By combining a variable hypocyclic mechanism, a reciprocating rack with clutch bearings, and a 6:1 planetary gear stage, he achieves a gear ratio range of 0:1 to 12:1, controlled by a single lever — which also acts as a clutch at the zero position.
