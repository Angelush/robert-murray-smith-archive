## Overview

Robert tests an aluminium brazing rod (Durafix EasyWeld) while fabricating a rotor for a Tesla turbine wind generator. He demonstrates the technique on sheet aluminium components, shares practical tips on surface preparation and heat application, and concludes that the product is genuinely useful and easier than TIG welding for non-critical aluminium fabrication work.
