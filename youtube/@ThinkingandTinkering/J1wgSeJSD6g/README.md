## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to convert a vintage gramophone clockwork mechanism (purchased for £20) into a working electrical generator. By mounting the mechanism to a board, attaching a disc with alternating-polarity magnets, and positioning a coil from a microwave oven transformer nearby, he successfully generates 9–12 volts — enough to light an LED. The video is framed as both a proof-of-concept and a template entry for a viewer competition.
