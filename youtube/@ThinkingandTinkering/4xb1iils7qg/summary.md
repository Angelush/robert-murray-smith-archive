## Overview
A short Lego-based science demonstration — likely a competition entry — showing how spinning a motor can generate electricity. The creator wired a Lego motor as a generator to power LED lights, but notes a practical drawback: the extra resistance from the generating wheel causes the car to drift to one side.

## Key Topics
- Using a Lego motor as an electrical generator
- Powering Lego LEDs from mechanically generated electricity
- Uneven wheel resistance causing directional drift in the car

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Lego motor (used as generator)
- Lego LED lights
- Lego car/wheels

## Techniques and Methods
- Spinning a motor to generate electricity (electromagnetic induction principle)
- Connecting generator output to LEDs to demonstrate power generation
