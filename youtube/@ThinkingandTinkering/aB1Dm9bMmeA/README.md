## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith and a colleague (Sam) discuss the development and near-market launch of "Ecofoam Supersoap," a hand soap originally formulated in their lab to remove graphite inks and paints from hands. The video showcases the product's creamy foam formula, improved squeeze-bottle packaging over a pump dispenser, and a concentrated powder format to reduce water weight in shipping. Sam is introduced as the team member taking the product to market.
