## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to upcycle a glass bottle into a vase or pen pot by cutting it using a thermal stress technique involving ink and a microwave. The cut edge is then smoothed with wet/dry sandpaper, and optionally coated with plaster dip for decoration and safety. The process requires minimal materials and yields a clean, usable result in a couple of attempts.
