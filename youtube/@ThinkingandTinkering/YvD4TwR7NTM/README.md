## Overview

In this fourth instalment of his "Exploding Water" series, Robert demonstrates two developments: a cylinder-and-plunger arrangement driven by his original spark-gap water explosion setup, and a significantly lower-energy version using photo flash capacitors. The episode concludes with a plan to combine both advances — a piston driven by the compact capacitor-based system — to be shown in a future update.
