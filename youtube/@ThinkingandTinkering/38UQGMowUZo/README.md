## Overview

Robert continues his exploration of EZ (Exclusion Zone) water cells by introducing zinc sulfide nanoparticles as a photoactive additive, building on a previous finding that methylene blue improved cell performance. He iterates through progressively better electrode designs — addressing galvanic battery concerns along the way — and demonstrates that illuminating the cell with a xenon lamp produces a stable, elevated voltage reading that decays when the light is removed, consistent with EZ layer formation and breakdown.
