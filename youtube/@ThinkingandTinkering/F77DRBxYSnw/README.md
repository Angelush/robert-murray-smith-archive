## Overview

This video explores how James Watt circumvented an existing patent on the crank mechanism to convert the reciprocating motion of a steam engine into rotary motion. Watt's solution was the Sun and Planet gear system, a form of epicyclic gearing, which Robert demonstrates using a 3D-printed model he designed in Tinkercad. The video connects this historical invention to modern applications of planetary gear systems.
