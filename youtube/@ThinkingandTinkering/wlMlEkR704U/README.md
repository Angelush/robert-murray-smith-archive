## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to build a simple, low-cost wind generator by salvaging a DC fan motor from a Nissan Micra (or any car) and extending its blades with sections cut from standard plastic drainage pipe. The build requires minimal tools and materials, and in testing the generator produced up to 20 volts. Robert considers it one of the cheapest and simplest functional wind generators possible to build in about half a day.
