## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith unboxes and reviews the Mingda Magician Pro 2, a budget-level consumer 3D printer with a large 400×400×400mm build volume. He walks through assembly, the auto-leveling system, the menu interface, and key hardware features, concluding that despite its relatively slow print speed, the printer is a strong contender in its price range due to its quietness, ease of assembly, and clean design.
