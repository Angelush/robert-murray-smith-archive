## Overview

This short update video demonstrates the Ugrinsky turbine design being tested with water, following a community comment from Stephen Evans revealing that the Ugrinsky device was originally designed as a hydroelectric turbine before being adapted for wind. Robert sets up a simple cradle and tests the turbine with both a bucket dump and a water spray, confirming it spins effectively under both conditions. The video raises new questions about deploying the design in river currents and ocean waves.
