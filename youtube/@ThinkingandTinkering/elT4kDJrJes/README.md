## Overview

Robert introduces a 2022 University of Minnesota research paper describing a device called the "catalytic condenser" — a capacitor-based thin-film stack that modifies the surface electron distribution of cheap metals like aluminium and copper, making them behave catalytically like precious metals such as rhodium and palladium. He argues this breakthrough could dramatically reduce the cost of key industrial processes and unlock a viable hydrogen economy, green fuels, bioplastics, and more.
