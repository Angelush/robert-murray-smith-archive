## Overview

Robert demonstrates a clever, low-cost method for fabricating a large gear/cog by repurposing a bicycle pulley rim (from his earlier power transmission video #1135) as the structural base. He fills the rim with expanding foam, lets it cure, trims and sands it flat, then glues a cut-to-size timing belt around the outer edge to create the gear teeth. The result is a true, square, functional gear that leverages the rim's built-in bearings.
