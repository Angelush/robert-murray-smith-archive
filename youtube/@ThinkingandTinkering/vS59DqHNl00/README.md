## Overview

This video demonstrates how to hand-wind toroidal inductors (toroids) with copper wire onto iron powder core rings. The presenter, Luke, shares a practical method for calculating the required wire length and preventing the wire from crimping or breaking during the tedious winding process. The toroids being wound (225 + 25 turns) are destined for a future mag amp build.
