## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates that cheap, readily available CDs can be used as reflective boosters to increase solar cell output, as an accessible alternative to the LCD film and aluminium foil method shown in a previous video. By arranging CDs around a solar cell, he measures a power increase from 0.76A to approximately 0.80A. He encourages viewers to experiment with improved pyramid/reflector designs and share their results.
