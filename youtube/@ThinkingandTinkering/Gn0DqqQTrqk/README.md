## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith demonstrates how to make traditional rivets from metal bar stock and use them to join two flat pieces of metal. The video was prompted by a collaborator ("Luke") who needed an attractive fastening method with no protrusion on either side. Robert covers both cold and hot riveting techniques, explaining when each is appropriate.
