## Overview

Luke (presenting on Robert Murray-Smith's channel) demonstrates printing a replacement tripod quick-release shoe on a resin 3D printer, sourced from Thingiverse, to solve the problem of two cameras sharing a single shoe. The print cost only 31 cents and took 30 minutes, saving approximately £19 compared to buying an official replacement part.
