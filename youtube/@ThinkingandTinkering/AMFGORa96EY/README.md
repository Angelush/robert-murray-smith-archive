## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith disassembles a donated NEC plasma TV, walking through the internal components layer by layer. He explains the basic operating principle of plasma displays and focuses on identifying and recovering reusable components — particularly power electronics, aluminium sheet, speakers, and inductors — for future projects.
