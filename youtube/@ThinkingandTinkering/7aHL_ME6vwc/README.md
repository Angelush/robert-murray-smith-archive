## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith walks through a complete step-by-step build of a replica Antikythera mechanism, constructed using a laser cutter and 3D printer rather than traditional brass and hand tools. The build is split into four parts covering the gear base unit, the inner planets (Sun, Mercury, Venus), the outer planets (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn), and the drive mechanism with decorative elements. Files are available on Thingiverse, and the project took approximately three days total — two days of 3D printing and one day of laser cutting.
