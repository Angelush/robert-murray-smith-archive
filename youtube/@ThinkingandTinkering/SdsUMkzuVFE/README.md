## Overview

Robert demonstrates how he is redesigning the original Darwin wind turbine — previously built from wood with flat vents that rattled loudly — into a quieter, flapless 3D-printed plastic version. The updated design was inspired by a paper model created by his collaborator Max Kennedy, which showed that a chimney-pot-style enclosure can direct airflow upward through a central vent without disturbing the rear airflow. Robert walks through the entire design process in Tinkercad and prints test blades to verify the concept works.
