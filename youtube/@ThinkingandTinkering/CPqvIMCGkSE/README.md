## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith presents the Dynapod Mark III, the third iteration of his pedal-powered machine design, incorporating two key improvements requested by viewers: a flywheel and a chain drive system. The Mark III is built primarily from salvaged exercise bike and bicycle parts, welded together to create a robust, gear-changeable human-powered mechanical output device. It represents a significant upgrade over previous versions in terms of power transmission quality and mechanical robustness.
