## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith examines the Hoberman Sphere (expanding ball toy), explaining the deceptively simple "Hoberman mechanism" — a pair of bent rods with a central pivot — that underpins it. He then walks through designing and 3D printing the interlocking linkage parts in Tinkercad, from raw bar primitives to snap-fit connectors. His central thesis is that true genius lies in the elegant application of simplicity to produce unexpected complexity.
