## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith defends triboelectric nanogenerators (TENGs) against criticism that they are useless, demonstrating how a South Korean research team combined wind-driven TENGs with embedded electrodes in solar panel glass to create a self-cleaning, waterless, mechanical-free dust removal system. The video argues that cross-disciplinary thinking — applying ideas from one field to solve problems in another — is a hallmark of genuine innovation.
