## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith provides a progress update on the FWG supercapacitor/battery project, explaining that the two-person team (Robert and Steve) is working on tight budgets and has settled on a carbon paper electrode approach compatible with existing paper-manufacturing infrastructure. Independent verification is planned with the UK's National Physics Laboratory at a cost of ~£10–12k, expected to begin in November 2019 with results by December–January.
