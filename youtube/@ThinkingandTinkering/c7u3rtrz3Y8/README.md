## Overview

This video explores prime numbers and their curious properties, focusing on the claim that every natural number greater than one is either prime or expressible as a product of primes (the Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic). The presenter walks through a proof by mathematical induction to demonstrate this holds for all natural numbers, not just specific examples. The video was likely made as a companion piece to a Robert Murray-Smith video on Pythagoras, with Robert planning a follow-up on inductive reasoning.
