## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the origins of the Rodin coil, rooted in Marco Rodin's "Vortex Based Mathematics" using base-9 number patterns mapped onto a toroid. He takes a pragmatic, skeptical view of the mystical claims around the coil while demonstrating how to wind a flat 12-point star version as a potentially easier alternative to the Fowler Harbur coil. A quick test with rotating magnets confirms it generates a small voltage, though nothing remarkable.
