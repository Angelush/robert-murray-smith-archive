## Overview
This short video demonstrates how to roll aluminium sheet into a curved shape without specialist tooling, using a fire extinguisher as an improvised roller. Robert shows the technique referenced in his main channel video 1702, emphasising edge pressure and patience as the keys to achieving a clean curve.

## Key Topics
- Improvised metal rolling using everyday workshop objects
- Technique for curving aluminium sheet without a dedicated sheet-metal roller
- Common mistake of applying pressure to the centre rather than the edge

## Materials and Chemicals Mentioned
- Aluminium sheet (flat stock being formed into a curve)

## Techniques and Methods
- Place aluminium sheet under a cylindrical fire extinguisher
- Press body weight against the sheet and roll it along the extinguisher
- Work consistently from the edge of the sheet, not the centre
- Apply steady pressure while rolling to progressively form the curve
