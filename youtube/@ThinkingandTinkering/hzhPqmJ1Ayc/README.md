## Overview

In this workshop tour, Steve (a collaborator in Robert Murray-Smith's lab) walks through the tools and techniques they use to overcome fabrication challenges on a limited budget. The central message is that expensive powered machinery can often be replaced or supplemented with affordable hand tools and custom-made solutions. The video concludes with a glimpse of a custom-built hopper for producing graphene filament for 3D printing — an example of making what you can't buy off the shelf.
