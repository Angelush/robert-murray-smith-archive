## Overview

Robert assembles and tests a 3D-printed Darwin wind turbine, replicating and building on work by community member Max Kennedy. He confirms the turbine captures and channels airflow, then demonstrates it generating enough electricity to light an LED — crucially, the turbine works in all three orientations (upright, sideways, and upside down). This orientation-independence sparks a key new idea: placing a lightweight collection structure at height and piping the air down to a ground-level generator.
