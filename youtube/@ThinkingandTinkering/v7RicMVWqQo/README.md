## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith explains the legal landscape surrounding home heating devices in the UK, focusing on the Ecodesign 2022 regulations and building regulations and how they affect DIY heater projects. He clarifies the key distinctions — between manufacturing vs. making, and installed vs. portable — that determine whether a homemade heater is legal to use. His main takeaway is that a portable, self-made heater burning compliant fuel is entirely lawful, while permanently installed or non-compliant devices are not.
