## Overview

Robert Murray-Smith disassembles a salvaged SMART Board — a touch-sensitive interactive whiteboard that was mass-deployed in UK schools during a government technology spending programme — to reveal the valuable materials hidden inside. He argues these discarded boards are a treasure trove of useful components for DIY electronics and construction projects, particularly for battery-making and structural applications.
