## Overview

Robert adapts his rocket stove (previously used in multiple incarnations) to burn waste oil, building on lessons from video 1685 where he made a simpler oil burner from concrete blocks. The key innovation is a mineral wool basket made from stainless steel expanded metal, placed inside the rocket stove's firebox and fed by a simple drip-feed system. The result is a ferociously hot, remarkably simple burner, though Robert notes improvements are needed — a longer chimney and more air holes — to reduce smoke and improve combustion.
