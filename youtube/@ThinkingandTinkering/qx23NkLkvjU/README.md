## Overview

Robert demonstrates how to build an improved version of the "Bright Betty" emergency oil lamp using a tin can and stainless steel mesh, framing it as a practical solution for potential power outages in 2023. The design is essentially a homemade Argand lamp — a centuries-old concept — constructed from cheap, accessible materials. He shows that with lamp oil (or kerosene as a smokier substitute) the result is a surprisingly powerful emergency light.
