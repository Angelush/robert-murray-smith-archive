## Overview

This short video demonstrates a simple but essential technique for joining carbon fiber to copper wire, enabling the construction of nano carbon heating elements. Robert shows how to mechanically bind the two materials using fine braided wire, then solder and tape the connection. The takeaway is that this reliable electrical joint makes it practical to use carbon fiber as a heating element in wearable or pad-style heaters.
