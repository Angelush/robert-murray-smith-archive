## Overview

A short Advent calendar science demonstration (Day 17) in which Robert adds food colouring to milk and then touches a soap-coated cotton swab to the surface, causing the colours to swirl dramatically. The experiment illustrates how soap disrupts the delicate balance between water, fat, proteins, and mineral salts in milk.
